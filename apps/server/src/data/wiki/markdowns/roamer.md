Category definitions
- Setter Roamers: Roamers who have a great combination of AOE and Crowd Control to initiate set ups for the team. Tanks are part of this category.
- Initiator Roamers: Roamers who professionally engage to start up team fights by targeting a single or double enemy; however they lack a combination of strong crowd control and AOE. Fighters and some tanks are part of this category.
- Utility/Healing Roamers: Roamers who use their abilities to protect their allies from debuffs. Supports are part of this category.
- Damage Roamers: Roamers who specialize in high damage to distract the enemy and target their backlines but are squishy and require a frontline jungler or EXP laner to protect them. Assassins are part of this category.
