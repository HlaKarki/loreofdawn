# Support

Image: File:Support_Icon.png (caption: 65px)

*This article is about the role of the hero. For the specialty of the hero, see [Hero specialties § Support](Hero specialties#Support).*

"The Support role is to aid and help protect your allies in any situation."
- Your role is is to shield or heal them, to tanking some damage or sacrificing yourself to keep others alive.
- You are often giving up the glorious kills in the game to support others, but in the end its usually the support to secure the true MVP award.
- Support heroes usually lane with Marksman-type Heroes in top lane or at mid lane in order to be able to travel quickly where they are needed. See [Heroes](Heroes).

Support is a type of role that provides some kind of benefit to their team.
- These benefits provide sustainability through healing or giving shields or deal some form of CC like stunning enemies.
- They often accompany carry heroes to help them level up faster.
- They are recommended to participate in team fights to help teammates in accomplishing objectives and not to solo a lane.

## Support Types

Supports are classified into three types: pure, primary, and secondary.

### Pure

Because they have no other role, these heroes are full supports.

Heroes:

- Estes
