# Gatotkaca

# Profile

- Chinese name: 金迦 (jīnjiā)
- Alias: Mighty Legend
- Born: Arcadhem Universe
- Origin: Nebula Chronorift
- Species: Human
- Gender: Male
- Title: Knight of Pringgadani
- Affiliation: Asuras (hostile)
- Weapons: Antakusuma armor; Braiamusti-Braiadenta gauntlets
- Abilities (not exhaustive): Electrokinesis; Flight

He is based on the character with the same name in Garudayana comics by Is Yuniarto, making him the first and currently the only hero in MLBB that is based on an intellectual property.

# Bio

Gatotkaca the "Mighty Legend" was the son of Bima and Arimbi from the Arcapada universe. Since his childhood, he's been a champion of Gods and fought against the wicked enemies of his homeland.

Gatotkaca once lost in battle and was beaten near death, but he rose again as a mighty warrior of the Pandava clan, and obtained supernatural power after training within Candradimuka, the cauldron of heaven. With his Antakusuma armor and Braiamusti-Braiadenta gauntlets, he can fly across the skies like lightning and hunt down his sworn enemies, the wrathful Asuras.

Gatotkaca, the Knight of Pringgadani, is a symbol of bravery and devotion. Based on Garudayana comic series created by Is Yuniarto and inspired from Indonesian traditional shadow puppets.

# Abilities

## Steel Bones

Gatotkaca gains 1 Physical Defense equal to 2% of his lost HP (up to 100). For every 300 damage he takes (calculated before damage reduction), he gains 5 Rage (up to 100). Upon reaching over 25 Rage, Gatotkaca's next Basic Attack becomes enhanced, consuming all Rage to deal extra Magic Damage and recover. The extra damage scales with his Rage, level, and Magic Power. The enhanced basic attack causes a short dash to the target.

- Enhanced basic attack damage: damage based on level, total physical attack, total magic power, and current Rage (enhanced basic attack formula based on level and stats).
- Healing effect: healing based on Rage and the rounding rules of Rage.
- Enhanced Basic Attack Range: 2.5

Notes:
- For each 1 lost HP, Gatotkaca gains 0.02 extra Physical Defense, up to a maximum of 100 Physical Defense if he has at least 5000 lost HP.
- Rage begins to deplete after approximately 3 seconds of Gatotkaca leaving combat, with 10 Rage depleted every 0.5 seconds.
- Rage is used in multiples of 5 and will be rounded to the nearest multiple of 5 for damage calculation. Example: having 38 Rage will output damage as if having 40 Rage, while having 7 Rage will output damage as if having 5 Rage.
- The heal is static and follows the rounding rule of Rage before it is calculated.
- Rage generation can be inconsistent, resulting in the same Rage but varied enhanced basic attack damage dealt.

## Blast Iron Fist

Gatotkaca slams the ground, creating a shattered zone in the target direction while dealing magic damage to enemies within. Enemies in the shattered zone will take sustained damage per second and be slowed by 30%.

- Duration: approx. 3.5 seconds
- Spell Vamp Ratio: 50%
- Base Damage: 200 / 220 / 240 / 260 / 280 / 300
- Sustained Damage: 100 / 110 / 120 / 130 / 140 / 150

Notes:
- Sustained damage is dealt every 0.5 seconds and can hit up to 7 times.
- Cooldown values by level: 8.0, 7.6, 7.2, 6.8, 6.4, 6.0 seconds.

## Unbreakable

Gatotkaca begins channeling, then sprints in the target direction with a battle cry, forcing enemies on the path to attack him and dealing magic damage for 1.5 seconds. The sprint distance scales with the channel time. Canceling the skill reduces 50% of the remaining cooldown.

- Taunt effect
- Magic Damage Skill
- Max HP scaling
- Spell Vamp Ratio: 50%
- Base Damage: 200 / 220 / 240 / 260 / 280 / 300

Quote: "Impregnable!"

Notes:
- Gatotkaca can fully charge the skill for approximately 1 and 1/6 seconds, and can overcharge for approximately another 3.75 seconds.
- Cooldown values by level: 12.0, 11.6, 11.2, 10.8, 10.4, 10.0 seconds.

## Avatar of the Guardian

Gatotkaca jumps to the target location, dealing magic damage to nearby enemies and knocking them airborne for 1 second. Enemies near the center of the area will be knocked airborne for a longer duration, while enemies on the fringes will be pulled to the center. The camera will move with the skill indicator but will not grant extra sight.

- Spell Vamp Ratio: 50%
- Base Damage: 500 / 750 / 1000

Quote: "A leap holds the world in awe."

Notes:
- This skill has control immunity and his descending animation makes Gatotkaca untargetable.
- Cooldown values by level: 54.0, 50.0, 46.0 seconds.
