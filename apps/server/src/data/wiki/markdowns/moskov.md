# Story

> "A warrior of the desert that embraced the darkness to seek his revenge."

Once a valiant and fearless desert warrior, Moskov met his end on the battlefield against his rival Khaleed. But in death, his soul encountered a great vortex of darkness buried beneath the sand by the desert tyrant Khufra. For centuries, the Vortex incited discord among the desert tribes and harvested the dark energy borne from their bloodshed. Though the Vortex's insidious influence trapped the desert in a cycle of strife and anguish, the Vortex itself sought to be freed. Tempted with the promise of revenge, Moskov extracted the core of the vortex, the Spear of Quiescence, and was reborn in the power of the darkness. Now he roams the sands, seeking revenge upon Khufra in the name of the desert.

# Abilities

## Spear of Quiescence

Moskov's Basic Attacks can penetrate the target and deal damage based on level and stats to enemies behind them (inherits Attack Effects). Each Basic Attack hit reduces the cooldowns of Skill 1 and Skill 2 by 0.5 seconds.

- Attack Effects: 100%
- Physical Attack Bonus (by level): 68% / 71% / 74% / 77% / 80% / 83% / 86% / 89% / 92% / 95% / 98% / 101% / 104% / 107% / 110%

Note: Damage values scale with the ability level and Moskov's attack stats.

## Abyss Walker

Moskov teleports to the target location, increasing his Attack Speed to 1.15–1.5 times for 3 seconds. Meanwhile, his Basic Attack deals 10% more damage to enemies behind the primary target.

- Attack Speed Bonus (by level): 1.15 / 1.22 / 1.29 / 1.36 / 1.43 / 1.5
- Duration: 3 seconds
- Mana Cost (by level): 45 / 50 / 55 / 60 / 65 / 70
- Cooldown (by level): 10.0 / 9.6 / 9.2 / 8.8 / 8.4 / 8.0

Effect types: Blink, Attack Speed buff.

Note: Attack Speed boost scales with the skill's level.

## Spear of Misery

Moskov launches a powerful strike at the target enemy hero or Creep, dealing physical damage, knocking them back, and revealing their position for 5 seconds. If the target collides with an enemy hero when knocked back, they'll both take damage and be stunned for 1.5 seconds. If the enemy is knocked into a wall, they will also be stunned for the same duration.

- Cast Range: 4.5
- Base Damage (by level): 300 / 330 / 360 / 390 / 420 / 450
- Extra Damage (by level): 200 / 215 / 230 / 245 / 260 / 275
- Physical Attack scaling: Base component scales with approximately 60% total Physical Attack; extra component scales with approximately 30% Physical Attack
- Stun Duration on collision or wall: 1.5 seconds
- Reveal Duration: 5 seconds
- Mana Cost (by level): 80 / 90 / 100 / 110 / 120 / 130
- Cooldown (by level): 12.0 / 11.5 / 11.0 / 10.5 / 10.0 / 9.5

Effect types: Displacement, Stun, Physical Damage skill.

Note: Damage values scale with level and Moskov's Physical Attack.

## Spear of Destruction

After a short delay, Moskov throws the spear in the target direction, dealing physical damage to enemies in its path and to the first enemy hero hit. Upon hitting an enemy hero, the spear pierces them dealing piercing damage to enemies behind them. The target and enemies hit by the piercing damage are slowed by 50% for 1 second (the slow duration scales with the spear's travel time, up to 3 seconds). When the Spear of Destruction hits the first enemy hero, it creates a shadow behind them and refreshes the cooldown of Abyss Walker. Moskov casts Abyss Walker from the shadow's location (shares he same cooldown as the normal skill).

- Base Damage (by level): 340 / 420 / 500
- Extra Physical Attack scaling: 100 (scaling value)
- Slow: 50% (duration scales with travel time, up to 3 seconds)
- Mana Cost (by level): 130 / 150 / 170
- Cooldown (by level): 55.0 / 50.0 / 45.0

Effect types: Physical Damage Skill, Physical Attack, Slow, Teleport (second-phase Abyss Walker).

Notes:
- Abyss Walker of this skill shares the same properties as the normal one.
- This means the attack speed boost is based on Skill 1's level. Therefore it is possible to use the 2nd phase of Ultimate skill without learning Skill 1.
- An overlay of camera is shown tracking the spear and its shadow.
