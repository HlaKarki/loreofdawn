# Bio

Moskov was once the prince of the Wildsand Clan and a fearless desert warrior. He fell on the battlefield in a feud with Khaleed, but in death his soul encountered a buried vortex of darkness that fed on war and anguish. Seduced by the promise of revenge, he took the core of the vortex, the Spear of Quiescence, and was reborn as a corrupted being that now wields darkness.

• Notable features: scars that exude dark power; a body bearing his people's legacy; wielder of the Spear of Quiescence.

• Origin: Emerald Road, Agelta Drylands.

# Story

> "A warrior of the desert that embraced the darkness to seek his revenge."

Once a valiant and fearless desert warrior, Moskov met his end on the battlefield against his rival Khaleed.

But in death, his soul encountered a great vortex of darkness buried beneath the sand by the desert tyrant Khufra. For centuries, the Vortex incited discord among the desert tribes and harvested the dark energy borne from their bloodshed. Though the Vortex's insidious influence trapped the desert in a cycle of strife and anguish, the Vortex itself sought to be freed.

Tempted with the promise of revenge, Moskov extracted the core of the vortex, the Spear of Quiescence, and was reborn in the power of the darkness. Now he roams the sands, seeking revenge upon Khufra in the name of the desert.

# Abilities

## Spear of Quiescence

Moskov's Basic Attacks can penetrate the target and deal scaling piercing damage to enemies behind them (inherits Attack Effects). Each Basic Attack hit reduces the cooldowns of Skill 1 (Abyss Walker) and Skill 2 (Spear of Misery) by 0.5 seconds.

- Attack Effects: 100%
- Physical Attack Bonus: 68% / 71% / 74% / 77% / 80% / 83% / 86% / 89% / 92% / 95% / 98% / 101% / 104% / 107% / 110%

Note: Piercing damage is scaling damage based on Moskov's physical attack and skill level.

## Abyss Walker

Moskov teleports to the target location, increasing his Attack Speed for 3 seconds and making his Basic Attacks deal more damage to enemies behind the primary target.

- Cooldown: 10.0 / 9.6 / 9.2 / 8.8 / 8.4 / 8.0 seconds
- Mana Cost: 45 / 50 / 55 / 60 / 65 / 70
- Attack Speed Bonus: 1.15 / 1.22 / 1.29 / 1.36 / 1.43 / 1.5 (multiplicative to base attack speed)
- Effect duration: 3 seconds

This skill is a blink (teleport) that grants a temporary attack speed buff and increases damage to enemies behind the primary target while active.

## Spear of Misery

Moskov launches a powerful strike at the target enemy hero or Creep, dealing physical damage, knocking them back, and revealing their position for 5 seconds. If the target collides with an enemy hero when knocked back, both will take additional physical damage and be stunned for 1.5 seconds. If the enemy is knocked into a wall, they will also be stunned for 1.5 seconds.

- Cast Range: 4.5
- Cooldown: 12.0 / 11.5 / 11.0 / 10.5 / 10.0 / 9.5 seconds
- Mana Cost: 80 / 90 / 100 / 110 / 120 / 130
- Base Damage: 300 / 330 / 360 / 390 / 420 / 450 (physical)
- Extra Damage: 200 / 215 / 230 / 245 / 260 / 275 (physical)
- Stun Duration on collision or wall: 1.5 seconds

This is a displacement skill that deals physical damage and provides crowd control through knockback and stun.

## Spear of Destruction

After a short delay, Moskov throws the spear in the target direction, dealing physical damage to enemies in its path and to the first enemy hero hit. Upon hitting an enemy hero, the spear pierces them dealing additional damage to enemies behind them. The target and enemies hit by the piercing damage are slowed by 50% for up to 3 seconds (slow duration scales with the spear's travel time, up to 3 seconds). When the spear hits the first enemy hero, it creates a shadow behind them and refreshes the cooldown of the second phase: Moskov can cast the spear again from the shadow's location. The second phase shares the same cooldown as the normal skill.

- Base Damage: 340 / 420 / 500 (physical)
- Extra Physical Attack scaling: 100 (additional physical attack scaling)
- Slow: 50% (duration scales with travel time, up to 3 seconds)
- Cooldown: 55.0 / 50.0 / 45.0 seconds
- Mana Cost: 130 / 150 / 170

Notes:

- Abyss Walker properties of Skill 1 apply to this ultimate's second phase when applicable. This means the attack speed boost is based on Skill 1's level, and it is possible to use the second phase without learning Skill 1 if the ultimate's mechanics allow.

- An overlay camera effect is shown tracking the spear and its shadow during the skill.

# Trivia

- Moskov's name is derived from the Moskva River in Russia, from which its capital, Moscow, is also derived.
- Also his name is of Russian descent.
- Moskov throws his spear using his left hand, making him the only left-handed, single-wielding marksman.
- Moskov's skin Javelin Champion has the number on his shirt "1718" which relates to the lore Laboratory 1718.
- Moskov's Infernal Wyrmlord skin is the first ever skin to receive "Stunning Special Effects".
