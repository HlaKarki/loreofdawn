# Gold Laner

A Gold Laner is a hero who goes on Gold Lane to take advantage of the 20% gold bonus from minion waves for the first 5 minutes of a game.

## Categories

Gold Laners are "unofficially" divided into 3 different categories:

### Late Game Gold
- Heroes who are disadvantaged and weak at the start but become very strong carries in the late game.

### Burst Gold
- Heroes who deal a large amount of damage in the early game; they either fall off in effectiveness in the late game or keep their strength.

### Magic Gold
- Heroes who deal exclusively magic damage early and into the late game, having greater advantages against their opponents. Mages and magic-damage specialty marksmen are part of this category.

## Rotation Guide

Recommended items
- Magic Blade
- Rose Gold Meteor

As you are usually the most squishy and lack damage output, the gold laner should stay in lane and try to hoard as much gold as possible:
- Secure last hits on minions with abilities.
- Kill the gold crab if possible.
- Peel the turret's golden shield if possible.
- Slay nearby small jungle creeps if no minions are nearby.
- Avoid dying at all costs. If there is no enemy sight on the map, try to hide in the bush between the first and second turret, as it will thwart most ganking attempts.
- Consider [items/strategies] for better survival. 
- If the enemy team ganks you with 3–4 members, they will likely dive the turret; give up the minion wave in that case.
- It is not recommended to purchase a high-tier boot early; a basic boot is usually sufficient. Some marksmen work fine with full attack items.

### Before 2 minutes
- Your wave clear speed is slow, so focus on minions first.
- Keep in mind that around 1:15–1:30 the enemy jungler finishes initial clears; avoid disclosing your location.

### 2–5 minutes
- Clearing minions in gold lane is still a priority. You can rotate to mid to clear minions when both teams contest the Turtle, then quickly return to gold lane.
- You can help your team if there is a fight near the Turtle pit, then quickly return to lane. Choose a route that goes through the fewest bushes.

### 5 minutes onward
Rotate to other lanes to find minion waves to farm, or clear small jungle creep camps.
