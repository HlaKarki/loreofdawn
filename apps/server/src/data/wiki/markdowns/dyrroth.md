# Bio

Twelve years ago, a prince was born in the Moniyan Empire. He was a symbol of salvation and justice for his people. It was believed that he would lead the Moniyan Empire in unifying the Land of Dawn. The Empire Nobles gave everything to the prince.

On the day of the prince's first birthday, he was found dead in his baby bed blanketed in blood. The room was covered with claw marks and the guards in charge of protecting the prince were also found dead. Sorrow engulfed the whole Moniyan Empire. The hope of the Empire had fallen and, to this day, there has never been such an inspiring prince. Some people believed the prince was killed by the Demons, while others still hoped the Prince was alive. The Empire Nobles appointed the Imperial Knights and the Monastery of Light to find out the truth. At the same time, the Son of Darkness was "born" too.

After several years, something strange appeared in the Southern Hills of the Moniyan Empire. In the deepest part of the Abyss, Thamuz, the Lord Lava, watched a special battle. In this glorious battle, the monsters fought with each other until the strongest emerged. He not only possessed the best Evil Gene but also proved to be the strongest fighter in the Demons.

Alice embraced the beloved fighter and recalled the "Royal Plan" that she had carried out. He was a masterpiece for her. He mastered Alice's trick, deception, and military strategy. He also learned combat from Thamuz. Now, the Son of Darkness is a capable assistant for the Abyss. He will represent the Lord of the Abyss, fighting against the Moniyan Empire, implementing the "Dawn Plan", and destroying the "Shining Spark" of the Empire.

# Abilities

## Wrath of the Abyss

# Wrath of the Abyss

When Dyrroth's HP reaches 50%, he will enhance Burst Strike and Spectre Step. After every 2 basic attacks, Dyrroth will release Circle Strike, dealing scaling damage to enemies in the circle and regenerating (50 plus scaling based on level and stats) HP. Circle Strike does not have ATK Effect. Each time he hits an enemy hero, the cooldowns of Burst Strike and Spectre Step will be decreased by 1 second.

- Circle Strike can critically strike and benefits from Physical Lifesteal.
- It counts as a skill and can benefit from skill effects.
- Circle Strike HP regeneration is halved against minions.
- Circle Strike deals the same damage as a regular basic attack against turrets.

## Burst Strike

# Burst Strike

Dyrroth releases a burst strike in a designated direction. Each burst deals scaling damage to enemies and slows them by 25% for 1.5 seconds. The damage decays against the same target and decreases to only scaling damage on minions.

Abyss Enhanced: Burst Strike has a longer range, deals 140% of the original damage, and its slow effect is doubled.

- Base Damage: 200 / 250 / 300 / 350 / 400 / 450
- Decay Ratio: (100%, 30%, 30%)
- Decay Ratio (Abyss Enhanced): (140%, 42%, 42%, 42%, 42%)
- Cooldown: 5.0 seconds
- Cost: 50 Rage

Notes:

- Dyrroth regens 10 HP for each enemy hit by this skill.

## Spectre Step

# Spectre Step

Dyrroth dashes in the designated direction. He will stop when he hits a non-minion target, dealing scaling damage to enemies. When he uses this skill again, he will lock onto a non-minion target and release a Fatal Strike, dealing a total of scaling damage and reducing the target's Physical Defense by 40% for 4 seconds.

Abyss Enhanced: Fatal Strike will deal increased damage, slow the target by an extra 90% for 1 second and reduce the target's Physical Defense by 60% for 4 seconds.

- Base Damage (phase 1): 230 / 255 / 280 / 305 / 330 / 355
- Added Damage (phase 2): 345 / 390 / 435 / 480 / 525 / 570
- Additional small hit values: 115 / 130 / 145 / 160 / 175 / 190 (plus physical attack scaling)
- Cooldown (level 1): 8.0 seconds
- Cost: 50 Rage

Notes:

- Bumping into an enemy hero with phase 1 of this skill applies a mini knockback effect.
- Phase 2 of this skill deals damage three times, each dealing scaling damage.
- The skill goes into cooldown on the first cast.
- Fatal Strike cannot target minions and cannot target the summoned Lord.

## Abysm Strike

# Abysm Strike

After a short delay, Dyrroth launches a destructive strike in the target direction (cannot be interrupted), dealing scaling damage to enemies along the way and slowing them by 55% for 0.8 seconds. The strike also deals additional damage based on enemies' lost HP (20%).

- Base Damage: 650 / 950 / 1250
- Cooldown (level 1): 36.0 seconds

Notes:

- Deals up to 1500 / 2000 / 2500 to non-hero units.

## Dyrroth BUFF

If Silvanna is in the enemy's team, increases Dyrroth's attack speed by 1%.
