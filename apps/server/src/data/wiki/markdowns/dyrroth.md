# Bio

Twelve years ago, a prince was born in the Moniyan Empire. He was a symbol of salvation and justice for his people. It was believed that he would lead the Moniyan Empire in unifying the Land of Dawn. The Empire Nobles gave everything to the prince.

On the day of prince's first birthday, he was found dead in his baby bed blanketed in blood. The room was covered with claw marks and the guards in charge of protecting the prince were also found dead. Sorrow engulfed the whole Moniyan Empire. The hope of the Empire had fallen and, to this day, there has never been such an inspiring prince. Some people believed the prince was killed by the Demons, while others still hoped the Prince was alive. The Empire Nobles appointed the Imperial Knights and the Monastery of Light to find out the truth. At the same time, the Son of Darkness was born too…

After several years, something strange appeared in the Southern Hills of the Moniyan Empire. In the deepest part of the Abyss, Thamuz, the Lord Lava, watched a special battle. In this glorious battle, the monsters fought with each other until the strongest emerged. He not only possessed the best Evil Gene but also proved to be the strongest fighter in the Demons.

Alice embraced the beloved fighter and recalled the "Royal Plan" that she had carried out. He was a masterpiece for her. He mastered Alice's trick, deception, and military strategy. He also learned combat from Thamuz. Now, the Son of Darkness is a capable assistant for the Abyss. He will represent the Lord of the Abyss, fighting against the Moniyan Empire, implementing the "Dawn Plan", and destroying the "Shining Spark" of the Empire.

# Abilities

## Wrath of the Abyss

When Dyrroth's HP reaches 50%, he will enhance Burst Strike and Spectre Step.

After each two basic attacks, Dyrroth will release Circle Strike, dealing damage based on level and stats to enemies in the circle and regenerating 50 plus additional HP based on his stats. Circle Strike does not have Attack Effect. Each time he hits an enemy hero with Circle Strike, the cooldown of Burst Strike and Spectre Step is decreased by 1 second.

- Physical Skill: Circle Strike counts as a physical skill.
- Regen Effect: Circle Strike regenerates HP (halved against minions).
- CD: Circle Strike reduces cooldowns of Burst Strike and Spectre Step when it hits enemy heroes.

Notes:

- Circle Strike can critically strike and benefits from Physical Lifesteal.
- It counts as a skill and can benefit from skill effects.
- Circle Strike HP regeneration is halved against minions.
- Circle Strike deals the same damage as a regular basic attack against turrets.

## Burst Strike

Dyrroth releases a burst strike in a designated direction. Each burst deals damage based on level and stats to enemies and slows them by 25% for 1.5 seconds. The damage decays against the same target and is reduced against minions. Abyss Enhanced: Burst Strike has a longer range, deals increased damage, and its slow effect is doubled.

- Base Damage: 200 / 250 / 300 / 350 / 400 / 450
- Decay Ratio: initial hit 100%, subsequent hits 30%, 30% on repeated hits
- Abyss Enhanced damage scaling is increased compared to the original

Notes:

- Costs 50 mana.
- Dyrroth regenerates 10 HP for each enemy hit by this skill.

## Spectre Step

Dyrroth dashes in the designated direction. He will stop when he hits a non-minion target, dealing damage based on level and stats to enemies. When he uses this skill again, he will lock onto a non-minion target and release a Fatal Strike, dealing added damage based on level and stats and reducing the target's Physical Defense by 40% for 4 seconds.

Abyss Enhanced: Fatal Strike will deal increased damage, slow the target by an extra 90% for 1 second, and reduce the target's Physical Defense by 60% for 4 seconds.

- Base Damage (phase 1): 230 / 255 / 280 / 305 / 330 / 355
- Added Damage (phase 2): 345 / 390 / 435 / 480 / 525 / 570
- Cooldown: 8.0 / 7.2 / 6.4 / 5.6 / 4.8 / 4.0 seconds

Notes:

- Costs 50 mana.
- Bumping into an enemy hero with phase 1 of this skill applies a mini knockback effect.
- Phase 2 of this skill deals damage three times, each instance dealing damage based on level and stats.
- The skill goes into cooldown on the first cast.
- Fatal Strike cannot target minions or the summoned Lord.

## Abysm Strike

After a short delay, Dyrroth launches a destructive strike in the target direction (cannot be interrupted), dealing damage based on level and stats to enemies along the way and slowing them by 55% for 0.8 seconds. The skill also deals additional damage based on enemies' lost HP (scaling value).

- Base Damage: 650 / 950 / 1250
- Cooldown: 36.0 / 32.0 / 28.0 seconds

Notes:

- Deals up to 1500 / 2000 / 2500 to non-hero units.

## Dyrroth BUFF (Shared Siblings Buff)

If Silvanna is in the enemy's team, increases Dyrroth's attack speed by 1%.
