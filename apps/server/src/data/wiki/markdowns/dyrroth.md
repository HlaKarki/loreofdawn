# Lore

Twelve years ago, a prince was born in the Moniyan Empire. (Moniyan Empire) He was a symbol of salvation and justice for his people. It was believed that he would lead the Moniyan Empire in unifying the Land of Dawn. The Empire Nobles gave everything to the prince.

On the day of prince's first birthday, he was found dead in his baby bed blanketed in blood. The room was covered with claw marks and the guards in charge of protecting the prince were also found dead. Sorrow engulfed the whole Moniyan Empire. The hope of the Empire had fallen and, to this day, there has never been such an inspiring prince. Some people believed the prince was killed by the Demons, while others still hoped the Prince was alive. The Empire Nobles appointed the Imperial Knights and the Monastery of Light to find out the truth. At the same time, the Son of Darkness was "born" too…

After several years, something strange appeared in the Southern Hills of the Moniyan Empire. In the deepest part of the Abyss, Thamuz, the Lord Lava, watched a special battle. (Abyss, Thamuz) In this glorious battle, the monsters fought with each other until the strongest emerged. He not only possessed the best Evil Gene but also proved to be the strongest fighter in the Demons.

Alice embraced the beloved fighter and recalled the "Royal Plan" that she had carried out. (Alice) He was a masterpiece for her. He mastered Alice's trick, deception, and military strategy. He also learned combat from Thamuz. Now, the Son of Darkness is a capable assistant for the Abyss. He will represent the Lord of the Abyss, fighting against the Moniyan Empire, implementing the "Dawn Plan", and destroying the "Shining Spark" of the Empire.

## Skill 1 — Burst Strike

- Scaling:
  - Base Damage: 200–450 (extra-pa 60, pd scaling)
  - Abyss Enhanced: 140% of the original damage
- Effects: AoE, slows
  - Slow: 25% for 1.5s (doubled under Abyss Enhanced)
- Cooldown: 5.0s
- Description: Dyrroth releases a burst strike in a designated direction. Each burst deals damage to enemies and slows them by 25% for 1.5s. The damage decays against the same target and decreases on minions. Abyss Enhanced: longer range, increased damage, and doubled slow effect.
- Decay ratios: {100%, 30%, 30%}; Abyss Enhanced decay ratio: {140%, 42%, 42%, 42%, 42%}
- Notes:
  - Costs 50 Rage.
  - Dyrroth regens 10 Rage for each enemy hit of this skill.

## Skill 2 — Spectre Step

- Scaling:
  - Phase 1 Base Damage: 230–355 (extra-pa 60, pd)
  - Phase 2 Added Damage: 345–570 (extra-pa 120, pd)
  - Alternate scaling: 115 / 130 / 145 / 160 / 175 / 190 (+40 pd)
- Effects: Blink, Damage; Fatal Strike on second cast reduces Physical Defense
- Cooldown: 8.0 / 7.2 / 6.4 / 5.6 / 4.8 / 4.0
- Description: Dyrroth dashes in the designated direction. He stops when he hits a [non-minion] target, dealing damage. Using the skill again locks onto a [non-minion] target and releases a Fatal Strike, dealing added damage and reducing the target's Physical Defense by 40% for 4s. Abyss Enhanced: Fatal Strike deals more damage, slows the target by an extra 90% for 1s and reduces Physical Defense by 60% for 4s.
- Notes:

  - Bumping into an enemy hero with phase 1 applies a mini knockback effect.
  - Phase 2 deals damage 3 times.
  - The skill goes into cooldown on the first cast.
  - Fatal Strike cannot target minions and cannot target the summoned Lord.

  - Deals up to 1500 / 2000 / 2500 to non-hero units.
