# Bio

South of the Moniyan Empire lies a bottomless abyss, engraved into this barren landscape as if an ancient demon used the sharpest of blades to carve a disfiguring scar into the Land of Dawn. People call this the Shadow Abyss.

Sealed away at the bottom of this legendary abyss lies the most terrible demon in the Land of Dawn - the Abyss Dominator. But as the seal slowly fades, the will of the abyss gradually grows stronger.

The demons at the bottom of the abyss tirelessly devise plots to strengthen the power of darkness, persuading misguided believers to provide them with sacrifices for their evil cause. They use these sacrifices to feed the Abyss Devil, a monster formed of the purest dark energy that waits in the darkness to devour its prey.

Selena, a young Dark Elf, was sacrificed to the Shadow Abyss by fanatics in exchange for dark energy, and as Elves are the Abyss Devil's favorite sacrifices, Selena was offered up immediately by the demons. The Abyss Devil maniacally drilled into Selena, severe pain and desperation spreading through every corner of the young Elf girl's body. Just as she was about to be consumed by dark magic, a miracle occurred - a warm blue light enveloped the girl, and the dark abyssal magic and this mystical power began waging a savage battle inside her fragile, young body.

Selena awoke to find herself lying in the depth of the abyss, her skin now a mysterious blue tone and her lavender eyes filled with a scarlet glow, with the Abyss Devil lying quietly at her side.

The Demons of the Abyss were busy wondering what had just happened. Little did they know, the will of the Abyss had arrived.

> "Leave this girls to me. She will become the strongest warrior of the Shadow Abyss!"

The demons obeyed the abyss' command and left her there. When Selena reappeared before the demonic inhabitants of the abyss, with the Abyss Devil at her side, she had completely forgotten her past.

She found that she could skillfully manipulate the abyss to release dark magic, a demon capable of tearing her enemies to shreds. The will of the Shadow Abyss appointed her as its voice, making it her duty to rule over the demonic denizens. Even the proud Queen of Blood Magic began to feel unnerved upon seeing this young Dark Elf Girl, not only because the abyss had chosen her, but also because Alice could sense the terrible power lying within this seemingly cute and mischievous youngster.

# Story

> "Dark Elf girl who fell into the Abyss"

The Dark Elf Selena had been dependent on her sister Karina since childhood. Selena maintained her innocent nature under the protection of her sister, until she discovered the dirty things her sister Karina had done for her. She thought that she had become a burden for her sister. In order to gain more power, she dedicated herself to the Abyss, but did not want to be completely controlled by the Abyss and ultimately became a horrifying demon.

# Abilities

## Symbiosis

Selena can switch freely between her two forms. Selena applies marks with her skills. The mark can stack up to 2 times. When a marked skill hits an enemy, it immediately adds an "Abyssal Mark". All damage will consume 1 stack of "Abyssal Mark" to deal extra magic damage.

- Base Damage: 240 / 255 / 270 / 285 / 300 / 315 / 330 / 345 / 360 / 375 / 390 / 405 / 420 / 435 / 450
- Damage type: Magic Skill

Abyssal Mark: a debuff applied by Selena's skills that can be consumed to deal additional scaling magic damage based on level and stats.

## Abyssal Trap

Variations: Zenith

Cooldown: 8.0 / 7.6 / 7.2 / 6.8 / 6.4 / 6.0 seconds
Mana Cost: 60 / 70 / 80 / 90 / 100 / 110

Selena summons an Abyssal Devil to lurk in a designated location. The Abyssal Devil will attach itself to any enemy that approaches the location and slow them by 70%. After 1 second, the Abyssal Devil deals damage to the enemy and other nearby enemies and slows them by 50% for 1 second. The Abyssal Devil lasts up to 60 seconds. Up to 3 Abyssal Devils can exist at the same time. Each extra Abyssal Devil increases the final damage by 50%.

- Base Damage: 350 / 390 / 430 / 470 / 510 / 550
- Skill Type: Magic Skill
- Movement Speed effect: slows enemy by listed amounts

Notes:
- The Abyssal Devils grant vision to the surrounding area.

## Abyssal Arrow / Garotte

Abyssal Arrow (Elven Form)

Cooldown: 12.0 seconds
Mana Cost: 70 / 80 / 90 / 100 / 110 / 120

Selena fires magic arrows in a designated direction, dealing damage to the first enemy hero hit and stunning it for 0.5 seconds to 3 seconds. Stun duration and damage increase with the distance that the arrows travel. The arrow absorbs power as it travels and will apply effects of Abyssal Traps to the enemy target upon hit. If an enemy is stunned for 1 second or longer, Selena's Movement Speed will be increased by 40% for 2 seconds.

- Base Damage: 250 / 280 / 310 / 340 / 370 / 400
- Base Max Damage: 500 / 560 / 620 / 680 / 740 / 800
- Effects: Stun, Movement Speed buff

Garotte (Abyssal Form)

Cooldown: 12.0 / 11.4 / 10.8 / 10.2 / 9.6 / 9.0 seconds
Mana Cost: 70 / 80 / 90 / 100 / 110 / 120

Selena charges in a designated direction, dealing damage to enemies along the way. If this damage triggers Abyssal Mark effect, its cooldown resets immediately.

- Base Damage: 150 / 190 / 230 / 270 / 310 / 350
- Skill Type: Magic Skill
- Blink ability: yes

Notes:
- Despite being a magic skill, Garotte does not trigger some magic-specific interactions but only triggers Abyssal Mark effects and related mechanics.

## Primal Darkness / Blessing of the Moon Goddess

Total Mana Cost: 25
Damage Type: Magic Damage

Primal Darkness (Abyssal Form)

Cooldown: 4.5 / 4.0 / 3.5 / 3.0 seconds

Selena enters Abyssal Form and increases her Movement Speed by 30% for 0.8 seconds. Immediately resets the cooldown of Abyssal Trap and Soul Eater. Selena gains new skills. Her basic attack will deal extra magic damage to a single target.

- Movement Speed Bonus: 30% / 35% / 40% / 45%
- Effects: Morph, Speed Up

Notes:
- Her basic attack's extra damage only applies to one target; multiple targets hit will receive her basic physical damage.

Blessing of the Moon Goddess (Elven Form)

Cooldown: 4.5 / 4.0 / 3.5 / 3.0 seconds

Selena enters Elven Form and increases her Movement Speed by 30% for 0.8 seconds. Immediately resets the cooldown of Abyssal Arrow and Garotte.

- Movement Speed Bonus: 30% / 35% / 40% / 45%
- Effects: Morph, Speed Up

# Trivia

- Except from her basic skin "Wasp Queen", all of Selena's skins were first released as time-limited.
- The name Selena comes from the Greek name "Selene" (Σελήνη) or Selina, which means "moon" or "goddess of the moon." In Greek mythology, Selene was the Titan goddess of the moon, often depicted riding a chariot across the night sky.
- Selena was one of the first heroes in MLBB to have two separate skill sets in one hero. Her switching mechanics make her both an Assassin and a Mage, depending on the form.
- Selena was supposed to be released on the date was released, but the developers decided to postpone her release to a month later because some of them saw her as over-powered and others saw her under-powered.
- Her Abyssal Arrow (2nd Skill in Elven Form) can stun enemies for up to 3 seconds, making it one of the longest stuns in the game!
- Selena's Abyssal skin "Lady Vengeance" pays a wicked tribute to Starcraft’s Kerrigan.
- Selena's Zenith skin "Curse of Cinder" refers to Ashes. In Selena's Zenith skin back story her stepsister's calls her little Ashes. The name 'Cinder' is also a shortened form of 'Cinderella,' from the classic fairy tale. Additionally, her outfit, theme, and skin story are inspired by it while Zenith skin is inspired by Snow White.
