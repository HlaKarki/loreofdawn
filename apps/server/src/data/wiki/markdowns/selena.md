# Selena

# Profile

- Alias: Abyssal Witch
- Born: Shadow Swamp, Azrya Woodlands
- Species: Dark Elf
- Gender: Female
- Weapon: Abyssal Devil
- Relationships: Karina (older sister); enemies: Aldous, Alice, Obsidia
- Japanese VA: Nokubi Naoko (野首 南帆子)
- Indonesian VA: Jane Leisilla Zahara

# Story

> "Dark Elf girl who fell into the Abyss"

The Dark Elf Selena had been dependent on her sister Karina since childhood. Selena maintained her innocent nature under the protection of her sister, until she discovered the dirty things her sister Karina had done for her. She thought that she had become a burden for her sister.

In order to gain more power, she dedicated herself to the Abyss, but did not want to be completely controlled by the Abyss and ultimately became a horrifying demon.

# Bio

South of the Moniyan Empire lies a bottomless abyss, engraved into this barren landscape as if an ancient demon used the sharpest of blades to carve a disfiguring scar into the Land of Dawn. People call this the Shadow Abyss.

Sealed away at the bottom of this legendary abyss, lies the most terrible demon in the Land of Dawn - the Abyss Dominator. But as the seal slowly fades, the will of the abyss gradually grows stronger.

The demons at the bottom of the abyss tirelessly devise plots to strengthen the power of darkness, persuading misguided believers to provide them with sacrifices for their evil cause. They use these sacrifices to feed the "Abyss Devil", a monster formed of the purest dark energy that waits in the darkness to devour its prey.

Selena, a young Dark Elf, was sacrificed to the Shadow Abyss by fanatics in exchange for dark energy, and as Elves are the Abyss Devil's favorite sacrifices, Selena was offered up immediately by the demons. The Abyss Devil maniacally drilled into Selena, severe pain and desperation spreading through every corner of the young Elf girl's body. Just as she was about to be consumed by dark magic, a miracle occurred - a warm blue light enveloped the girl, and the dark abyssal magic and this mystical power began waging a savage battle inside her fragile, young body.

Selena awoke find herself lying in the depth of the abyss, her skin now a mysterious blue tone and her lavender eyes filled with a scarlet glow, with the Abyss Devil lying quietly at her side.

The Demons of the Abyss were busy wondering what had just happened. Little did they know, the will of the Abyss had arrived. This girl is loved deeply, and this love has protected her from being consumed by darkness. The Abyss and the Moon Goddess - these two opposing forces have reached a balance within her body.

> "Leave this girls to me. She will become the strongest warrior of the Shadow Abyss!"

The demons obeyed the abyss' command and left her there. When Selena reappeared before the demonic inhabitants of the abyss, with the Abyss Devil at her side, she had completely forgotten her past.

She found that she could skillfully manipulate the abyss to release dark magic, a demon capable of tearing her enemies to shreds. The will of the Shadow Abyss appointed her as its voice, making it her duty to rule over the demonic denizens. Even the proud Queen of Blood Magic began to feel unnerved upon seeing this young Dark Elf Girl, not only because the abyss had chosen her, but also because Alice could sense the terrible power lying within this seemingly cute and mischievous youngster.

# Abilities

## Symbiosis (Elven Form / Abyssal Form)

- Magic Damage
- Symbiosis: Selena can switch freely between her two forms. Selena applies "Abyssal Mark" with her skills. The mark can stack up to 2 times. When Selena hits an enemy with certain effects, it immediately adds an "Abyssal Mark". All damage will consume 1 stack of "Abyssal Mark" to deal extra damage (scaling damage based on level and stats).

- Base Damage: 240 / 255 / 270 / 285 / 300 / 315 / 330 / 345 / 360 / 375 / 390 / 405 / 420 / 435 / 450 (damage based on level and stats)

- Mana: total MP 40 (scaling with level)

- Additional notes: Passive involves form-switching between Elven Form and Abyssal Form and interacting with Abyssal Traps/Abyssal Marks. Damage and exact scaling depend on ability level and Selena's magic power.

## Abyssal Trap

> "The iron will of the Abyss befalls."

- Skill Type: Summon / Slowed
- Cooldown (base): 8.0 seconds (scales down per level)
- Mana Cost (base): 60 (increases by level: 60 / 70 / 80 / 90 / 100 / 110)

- Description: Selena summons an Abyssal Devil to lurk in a designated location. The Abyssal Devil will attach itself to any enemy that approaches the location and slow them by 70%. After 1 second, the Abyssal Devil deals damage to the enemy and other nearby enemies and slows them by 50% for 1 second. The Abyssal Devil lasts up to 60 seconds. Up to 3 Abyssal Devils can exist at the same time. Each extra Abyssal Devil increases the final damage by 50%.

- Base Damage: 350 / 390 / 430 / 470 / 510 / 550 (actual damage scales with ability level and magic power)

- Notes:
- The Abyssal Devils grant vision to the surrounding area.
- Each additional Abyssal Devil increases final damage by 50%.

## Soul Eater

> "The iron will of the Abyss befalls."

- Skill Type: Burst / Shield
- Cooldown (base): 6.0 seconds (scales down per level)
- Mana Cost (base): 60 (increases by level: 60 / 65 / 70 / 75 / 80 / 85)

- Description: Selena strengthens her claws with the power of the Abyss, causing the next basic attack to deal extra damage, while granting a Shield that absorbs damage (shield amount scales with ability level and stats).

- Base Damage (extra): 200 / 230 / 260 / 290 / 320 / 350 (damage based on level and magic power)
- Basic Shield: 300 / 320 / 340 / 360 / 380 / 400 (shield value scales with ability level)

- Term: Magic Skill / Shield

- Notes: The skill grants a shield and enhances the next basic attack. Exact extra damage and shield absorption scale with Selena's level and magic power.

## Abyssal Arrow

- Skill Type: CC / Speed Up
- Cooldown: 12.0 seconds (base)
- Mana Cost (base): 70 (70 / 80 / 90 / 100 / 110 / 120)

- Description: Selena fires magic arrows in a designated direction, dealing damage to the first enemy hero hit and stunning it for 0.5 seconds to 3 seconds. Stun duration and damage increase with the distance that the arrows travel. The arrow absorbs power as it travels and hence will apply effects of "Abyssal Traps" to the enemy target upon hit. If an enemy is stunned for 1 second or longer, Selena's Movement Speed will be increased by 40% for 2 seconds.

- Base Damage: 250 / 280 / 310 / 340 / 370 / 400
- Max Damage (at max travel): 500 / 560 / 620 / 680 / 740 / 800 (damage scales with travel distance and magic power)

- Term: Stunned / Magic Skill / Movement Speed

- Notes: Stun duration scales with travel distance. Applies effects of Abyssal Traps on hit. Movement speed buff triggers if stun >= 1 second.

## Garotte

- Skill Type: Blink / BUFF
- Cooldown (base): 12.0 / 11.4 / 10.8 / 10.2 / 9.6 / 9.0 (resets if certain conditions met)
- Mana Cost (base): 70 (70 / 80 / 90 / 100 / 110 / 120)

- Description: Selena charges in a designated direction, dealing damage to enemies along the way. If this damage triggers an "Abyssal Mark" effect, its cooldown resets immediately.

- Base Damage: 150 / 190 / 230 / 270 / 310 / 350 (damage scales with level and magic power)

- Term: Magic Skill / Blink / CD

- Notes:
- Despite being a magic skill, Garotte does not trigger certain effects that only trigger on basic attacks; it only triggers Abyssal Mark interactions as specified.
- If Garotte's damage triggers Abyssal Mark, its cooldown resets immediately.

## Primal Darkness (Abyssal Form)

- Skill Type: Morph / Speed Up
- Cooldown (base): 4.5 / 4.0 / 3.5 / 3.0 (first value shown)
- Mana Cost: 25 (total MP scale)

- Description: Selena enters Abyssal Form and increases her Movement Speed by 30% for 0.8 seconds. Immediately resets the cooldown of Abyssal Trap and Abyssal Arrow. In Abyssal Form, Selena gains new skills. Her basic attack will deal extra magic damage to one target.

- Movement Speed Bonus: 30% / 35% / 40% / 45%

- Term: CD / Movement Speed / Magic Basic Attack

- Notes:
- Her basic attack's extra damage only applies to one target; multiple targets hit will receive her basic physical damage.
- Transforms Selena's skill set into Abyssal Form abilities.

## Blessing of the Moon Goddess (Elven Form)

- Skill Type: Morph / Speed Up
- Cooldown (base): 4.5 / 4.0 / 3.5 / 3.0 (first value shown)
- Mana Cost: 25 (total MP scale)

- Description: Selena enters Elven Form and increases her Movement Speed by 30%. Lasts 0.8 seconds. Immediately resets the cooldown of Soul Eater and Garotte.

- Movement Speed Bonus: 30% / 35% / 40% / 45%

# Trivia

- Except from her basic skin "Wasp Queen", all of Selena's skins were first released as time-limited.
- The name Selena comes from the Greek name "Selene" (Σελήνη) or Selina, which means "moon" or "goddess of the moon." In Greek mythology, Selene was the Titan goddess of the moon, often depicted riding a chariot across the night sky.
- Selena was one of the first heroes in MLBB to have two separate skill sets in one hero. Her switching mechanics make her both an Assassin and a Mage, depending on the form.
- Selena was supposed to be released on a certain date, but the developers decided to postpone her release to a month later because some of them saw her as over-powered and others saw her under-powered.
- Her Abyssal Arrow (2nd Skill in Elven Form) can stun enemies for up to 3 seconds, making it one of the longest stuns in the game!
- Selena's Abyssal skin "Lady Vengeance" pays a wicked tribute to Starcraft’s Kerrigan.
- Selena's Zenith skin "Curse of Cinder" refers to Ashes. In Selena's Zenith skin back story her stepsister calls her little Ashes. The name 'Cinder' is also a shortened form of 'Cinderella.' Additionally, her outfit, theme, and skin story are inspired by that tale while another hero's Zenith skin is inspired by Snow White.
