# Bio

The Celestial Palace, home of the gods, was once a bustling city patrolled by innumerable Nazar guardians. Any demon who dared approach the city would be overwhelmed by their unrivaled flying speed and bound by their frightening lightning whips which would strip their victims of all magical abilities. No demon could escape when pursued by these divine warriors, and thus perpetual peace was maintained in the Celestial Palace.

As their leader, Kaja was able to refine the power of lighting into pure energy to control as he so desired, inflicting catastrophic damage to all enemies who faced him, and was charged with directly protecting the ruler of the city. Countless eons ago, after the city's ruler created the Nazar guardians and departed, the city gradually fell into a state of ruin. When Uranus was activated, the ruler of the Celestial Palace handed down new orders to the Nazar—to drive the evil demons from the city and rebuild the Celestial Palace in anticipation of the master's return.

# Story

> "Leader of Nazar who guards the Celestial Palace"

Living in the Celestial Palace where the gods inhabit, he is the leader of the Nazar who is responsible for guarding the Celestial Palace. Being the incarnation of the Eagle Angel, he resists the Empire's invasion of the Celestial Palace holding a divine whip.

# Abilities

## Wrath Sanction

Kaja achieves Wrath Sanction every 6 seconds. His next will send a lightning to enemies, dealing scaling magic damage based on level and stats and paralyzing them. The lightning will hit enemies nearby as well (up to 3 times). Damage against minions and creeps increases by a percentage of the target's Max HP.

Paralyzing: Slows enemies by approx. 90% for approx. 0.4 second and reduces their damage by 8% (up to 4 stacks) for 4 seconds.

- Cooldown: 6.0 seconds
- Damage type: Magic (scaling damage)
- Additional effect: Lightning can hit up to 3 nearby enemies
- Damage Dealt Reduction to Enemies by passive rank: 8% / 16% / 24% / 32%

## Ring of Order

Kaja releases a Ringed Electric Blade that quickly expands and contracts, dealing magic damage to enemies hit on its way out and back and paralyzing them. Each time an enemy is hit by the Electric Blade, Kaja's energy is restored by 100 (reduced to 30% after multiple hits).

- Base Damage by skill level: 135 / 165 / 195 / 225 / 255 / 285
- Skill type: Magic Skill
- Effects: AoE, Slow, Paralysis
- Movement interaction: Restores energy on hit
- Mana cost (by level): 50 / 55 / 60 / 65 / 70 / 75
- Cooldown (by level): 6.5 / 6.3 / 6.1 / 4.9 / 4.7 / 4.5 seconds

## Lightning Bomb

Kaja dashes in the target direction while leaving 3 Lightning Bombs along the way. The Bombs will explode upon enemy contact, dealing magic damage based on level and stats and paralyzing them.

- Base Damage by skill level: 200 / 230 / 260 / 290 / 320 / 350
- Skill types: Magic Skill, Blink
- Effects: Blink, Damage, Paralysis
- Mana cost (by level): 65 / 70 / 75 / 80 / 85 / 90
- Cooldown (by level): 13.0 / 12.4 / 11.8 / 11.2 / 10.6 / 10.0 seconds

## Divine Judgement

Kaja deals magic damage to a designated enemy data, suppresses and paralyzes them for 1.5 seconds. During the suppression, Kaja can drag the enemy data along with him.

- Base Damage by skill rank: 300 / 450 / 600
- Effect: Suppress (cannot take action), Paralysis
- Mana cost: 100
- Cooldown: 60 seconds
- Notes: While the target is suppressed, Kaja may relocate and drag the suppressed data with him.
