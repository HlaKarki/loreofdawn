# Story

> "King of the Fire Demons."

A warrior who believed in the Abyss and was appointed as the leader of the Abyss Legion. Because the Fire Demons were affected by the Abyss for thousands of years and are considered the race closest to Abyss, Thamuz has always had a high sense of superiority and self-esteem.

## Lore

Deep within the mountains, darkness reigns over everything. The only light is the crimson lava that flows in the bottomless crevices. Since the beginning of the abyss's reign on the mainland, countless demons became loyal followers of the Abyss. Fervent in their beliefs, the demons chant the will of the Abyss in these deep caverns—

> "Only the strong deserve to rule!"

Guarding the Abyss for millenniums, a tribe of fire demons nearly became the rulers of the Abyss. Thamuz, the king of the fire demons, and his people made the Abyss into a formidable power, unabashed to use cruel methods in achieving his goals. Possessing ungodly natural strength, Thamuz was appointed chief of the Abyss, who led the Abyss to conquer all of the underworld.

To Thamuz, all of his opponents were worthless scum, unworthy of living in this world. In his eyes, only he was worthy to lead. Thamuz could not accept the Blood Demon, Alice, as her bloodline was not pure — thus, unworthy to command and enter war.

With the gates to the Abyss about to open, legions of demons eagerly await Thamuz's return. However, once the King of Fire Demons returns, he will burn away all the weak demons — only the strong deserve to be a part of the Abyssal Army. Thamuz has been waiting for the day he leads the Abyssal Army across the Land of Dawn to let it be known—Only the strong shall reign!

# Abilities

## Passive

- Name: Grand Lord Lava
- Triggers: Chance to conjure Lava Energy beneath a target (10%–60% chance depending on context). Basic Attacks that don't trigger Lava Energy increase Thamuz's Lava Rage.
- Effects:
  - Lava Energy erupts after a brief delay and deals true damage (skill damage type).
  - When without (Lava Rage?), Thamuz gains 25% Movement Speed.
  - After retrieving his Scythes, his next Basic Attack slows the target by 30% and is guaranteed to trigger Lava Energy; that enhanced Basic Attack allows a dash to the target (range 3.0).
- Notes:
  - Lava Energy deals skill damage.
  - Enhanced basic attack dashes to the target.
  - Enhanced attack range: 3.0.

## Skill 1 — Molten Scythes

- Type: AOE / Slow
- Cooldown: 3.0 s
- Description: Thamuz tosses his scythes in the target direction. After travelling a distance or hitting the first enemy hero, they deal physical damage, then slow nearby enemies by 30% and deal sustained damage every 0.5 s. After about 5 seconds or after a set distance, the Scythes return to Thamuz and drag enemies in their path. Thamuz can approach the Scythes or cast the skill again to retrieve them.
- Scaling:
  - Base Damage: 120 / 150 / 180 / 210 / 240 / 270
  - Sustained Damage: 45 / 52 / 59 / 66 / 73 / 80
- Notes:
  - Cooldown starts after retrieving the Scythes.
  - Scythes do not stop upon hitting minions.
  - Cannot cast 2nd phase for ~0.2 s after casting.
  - Retrieving by tapping again is not considered a skill cast.
  - Can be used while under CC, including Suppression.

## Skill 2 — Chasm Trample

- Type: Mobility / Buff
- Cooldown: 8.0 / 7.6 / 7.2 / 6.8 / 6.4 / 6.0 s
- Description: Thamuz jumps to a designated area, dealing physical damage and slowing enemies by 25% for 2 s upon landing. If the scythes are still travelling, they return and the skill's cooldown is instantly reset.
- Scaling:
  - Base Damage: 230 / 255 / 280 / 305 / 330 / 355

## Ultimate — Cauterant Inferno

- Type: AOE / Buff / Regen
- Cooldown: 38.0 / 34.0 / 30.0 s
- Description: Thamuz spouts the lava stored in his body, dealing physical damage to nearby enemies and creating a cauterant area lasting 9 seconds. The area deals sustained damage every 0.5 s. Thamuz gains extra Max HP, heals himself by the same amount, and increases Attack Speed by 60%–100%. He also recovers a percentage of Max HP each time he deals damage with Basic Attacks or his skills.
- Scaling:
  - Base Damage: 300 / 390 / 480
  - Sustained Damage: 30 / 45 / 60
  - HP Regen per hit: 1.5% / 2% / 2.5% Max HP
  - Attack Speed: 60% / 80% / 100%
  - Extra Max HP: 30% / 40% / 50%
- Notes:
  - Attack speed increase multiplies total attack speed.
  - Healing from dealing damage with skills and basic attacks does not include sustained damage components.

# Trivia

## Lore (additional notes)
- In the Determination event, Thamuz claims himself as the Lord of Darkness and wants Lunox to join him.
- Thamuz appears in Helcurt's lore; when Helcurt defended the Lord of the Abyss (Obsidia) against corrupted demons, the opposing forces were led by Lord Lava himself.
- In Dyrroth's trailer, Thamuz praises Dyrroth, seeing a part of himself in the demon prince.
- Dyrroth mentions Thamuz in a voiceline, mocking that his enemy should've been taught by Thamuz instead.
- Thamuz is half-heartedly mentioned in two of Helcurt's voicelines, describing him as:
  > "a walking puddle of lava with a pair of dull blades"
  Their relationship is likened to that of a father and a family pet.
- Thamuz shows genuine empathy toward his kind, depicted as a strict yet well-meaning ruler who prioritizes his tribe's survival.

## Belerick Sacrificed Himself! Who's Responsible?
- During the Abyss's invasion of the Moonlit Forest (in "Belerick Sacrificed Himself! Who's Responsible?"), Thamuz indirectly killed Belerick; Belerick sacrificed himself defending the Tree of Life.
- When Belerick was revived, his kit was revamped to better counter continuously damaging opponents like Thamuz.

## Demons Approaching
- In the official comic "Demons Approaching" (which debuted Brody, Benedetta, and Phoveus), Thamuz reveals possession of one of the "Nine Demon Lords of the Abyss," an orange crystal called 'Gamunti'. Canon status is unclear after Phoveus's lore revamp.

## The Abyss: Sovereign's Will / Post-Revamp
- In the cinematic Sovereign's Will, Thamuz interrupts Alice and Dyrroth harvesting Obsidia's blood, arguing her corruption persists and lamenting Obsidia's past crimes against his people.
- Thamuz later encounters a corrupted Lava Demon and is forced to kill it, fueling his rage.
- In Obsidia's concept video, Lava Demons may not naturally have a head as Thamuz does, suggesting he may have carved one to assert dominance.

## Miscellaneous
- Thamuz's original hero showcase video on the official YouTube channel had an abrupt change in thumbnail design.
- Thamuz had four different walking animations for his scytheless form during development.
- In the advanced server prior to release, he ran on all fours without scythes; on release he walked on hind legs with arms outstretched.
- A soft rework (Patch 1.6.84, May 24, 2022) changed his posture to be straighter and to jog; that change was later rolled back.
- On his revamp (September 17, 2025), he received a new scytheless animation similar to the removed one.
- Thamuz was the first hero introduced from the Abyss faction, predating heroes like Alice, Dyrroth, and Selena.
- His passive Grand Lord Lava was among the first abilities to consistently deal true damage based on enemies' HP.
- Some players compare his look to Balrog from Lord of the Rings and Ifrit from Final Fantasy.
- His weapons, Molten Scythes, return to him on their own (boomerang-like).
- When Thamuz moves, a lava trail forms behind him; his footsteps leave elemental effects.
- After revamp, Thamuz has different glow effects on his model during his ultimate, depending on his skin.
