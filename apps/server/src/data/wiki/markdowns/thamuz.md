Story
> "King of the Fire Demons."

A warrior who believed in the Abyss and was appointed as the leader of the Abyss Legion. Because the Fire Demons were affected by the Abyss for thousands of years and are considered the race closest to the Abyss, Thamuz has always had a high sense of superiority and self-esteem.

Lore

Deep within the mountains, darkness reigns over everything. The only light is the crimson lava that flows in the bottomless crevices. Since the beginning of the abyss's reign on the mainland, countless demons became loyal followers of the Abyss. Fervent in their beliefs, the demons chant the will of the Abyss in these deep caverns—Only the strong deserve to rule!

Guarding the Abyss for millenniums, a tribe of fire demons nearly became the rulers of the Abyss. Thamuz, the king of the fire demons, and his people made the Abyss into a formidable power, unabashed to use cruel methods in achieving his goals. Possessing ungodly natural strength, Thamuz was appointed chief of the Abyss, who led the Abyss to conquer all of the underworld.

To Thamuz, all of his opponents were worthless scum, unworthy of living in this world. In his eyes, only he was worthy to lead. Thamuz could not accept the Blood Demon, Alice, as her bloodline was not pure — thus unworthy to command and enter war.

With the gates to the Abyss about to open, legions of demons eagerly await Thamuz's return. However, once the King of Fire Demons returns, he will burn away all the weak demons — Only the strong deserve to be a part of the Abyssal Army. Thamuz has been waiting for the day he leads the Abyssal Army across the Land of Dawn to let it be known—Only the strong shall reign!

Abilities

Passive — Grand Lord Lava
- Triggers: 10%–60% chance (scales) to conjure Lava Energy beneath a target that erupts after a brief delay, dealing true damage. Basic Attacks that don't trigger Lava Energy increase Thamuz's Lava Rage.
- When without (lava energy?), Thamuz gains 25% Movement Speed. After retrieving his scythes, his next Basic Attack slows the target by 30% and is guaranteed to trigger Lava Energy.
- Notes:
  - Lava Energy deals skill damage (true-damage component).
  - The enhanced basic attack allows Thamuz to dash to the target's location (range ~3.0).
  - The enhanced basic attack slows the target and guarantees Lava Energy.

Skill 1 — Molten Scythes
- Type/effects: AOE, Slow
- Cooldown: 3.0s
- Description: Thamuz tosses his scythes in a target direction. After traveling a certain distance or hitting the first enemy hero, they deal physical damage. The scythes slow nearby enemies by 30% and deal sustained physical damage every 0.5s. After leaving Thamuz for ~5s or traveling a set distance, the scythes return and drag enemies in their path back to him. Thamuz can approach the scythes or press the skill again to retrieve them.
- Level scaling:
  - Base Damage: 120 / 150 / 180 / 210 / 240 / 270  
  - Sustained Damage (per tick): 45 / 52 / 59 / 66 / 73 / 80
- Notes:
  - Cooldown starts after retrieving the scythes.
  - Scythes do not stop upon hitting minions (patch change).
  - Cannot cast the second phase for ~0.2s after casting.
  - Retrieving by tapping again is not considered a skill cast.
  - Can be used under crowd control effects, including Suppression.

Skill 2 — Chasm Trample
- Type/effects: Mobility, Buff (resets scythe behavior)
- Cooldown: 8.0 / 7.6 / 7.2 / 6.8 / 6.4 / 6.0s (scales)
- Description: Thamuz leaps to a designated area, dealing physical damage and slowing enemies by 25% for 2s on landing. If the scythes are still traveling, they return to Thamuz and this skill's cooldown is instantly reset.
- Level scaling:
  - Base Damage: 230 / 255 / 280 / 305 / 330 / 355

Ultimate — Cauterant Inferno
- Type/effects: AOE, Buff (self-sustain and attack-speed)
- Cooldown: 38.0 / 34.0 / 30.0s
- Description: Thamuz spouts the lava stored in his body, dealing physical damage to nearby enemies and creating a cauterant inferno around him that lasts 9s. The inferno deals sustained damage every 0.5s. Meanwhile, Thamuz gains increased Max HP (percentage), heals himself by the same amount, and gains large Attack Speed bonus. He also recovers a percentage of Max HP each time he deals damage with Basic Attacks or his skills.
- Level scaling:
  - Base Damage: 300 / 390 / 480  
  - Sustained Damage (per tick): 30 / 45 / 60  
  - HP Regen on hit: 1.5% / 2% / 2.5% Max HP per hit  
  - Attack Speed: 60% / 80% / 100%  
  - Max HP bonus: 30% / 40% / 50%
- Notes:
  - Attack speed increase multiplies to total attack speed.
  - Healing from dealing damage with skills and basics does not include sustained damage ticks.

Trivia
- In the Determination event, Thamuz claims himself as the Lord of Darkness and wants Lunox to join him.
- Thamuz appears in Helcurt's lore; when Helcurt defended the Lord of the Abyss (Obsidia), opposing forces were led by Thamuz.
- In Dyrroth's trailer, Thamuz praises Dyrroth, seeing a part of himself in the demon prince. Dyrroth also mentions Thamuz in voicelines, mocking how enemies should've been taught by Thamuz.
- Helcurt's voicelines mock Thamuz as a "walking puddle of lava with a pair of dull blades," and their relationship is likened to a father and a family pet.
- Thamuz shows genuine empathy toward his kind: a strict but well-meaning ruler who prioritizes his tribe's survival.
- In the comic "Demons Approaching," Thamuz possesses one of the "Nine Demon Lords of the Abyss," an orange crystal called 'Gamunti' (canon status may vary with revamped lore).
- In the cinematic "Sovereign's Will," Thamuz interrupts Alice and Dyrroth from harvesting Obsidia's blood and later is forced to kill a corrupted Lava Demon, fueling his rage.
- Obsidia's concept suggests Lava Demons may not naturally grow heads as Thamuz has; he may have carved his to assert dominance.

Belerick Sacrificed Himself! Who's Responsible?
- During the Abyss invasion of the Moonlit Forest, Thamuz indirectly caused Belerick's sacrifice defending the Tree of Life. When Belerick was revived, his kit was revamped to better counter continuously damaging opponents such as Thamuz.

Miscellaneous
- Thamuz's original hero showcase video had an abrupt thumbnail change.
- He had several different walking animations for his scytheless form during development and testing; on release he walked on hind legs, later adjusted to a straighter jog.
- On a soft rework (Patch 1.6.84, May 24, 2022) posture/animation changed; later some changes were rolled back. (A revamp on September 17, 2025 added a new scytheless animation.)
- Thamuz was the first hero introduced from the Abyss faction, predating Alice, Dyrroth, and Selena in lore.
- His passive (Grand Lord Lava) was one of the earliest abilities to consistently deal true damage based on enemy HP.
- Some players compare his look to Balrog (Lord of the Rings) or Ifrit (Final Fantasy).
- His Molten Scythes return to him automatically, similar to boomerangs.
- When Thamuz moves, a lava trail forms behind him; his footsteps leave elemental effects.
- After his revamp, different skins grant different glow effects during his ultimate.
