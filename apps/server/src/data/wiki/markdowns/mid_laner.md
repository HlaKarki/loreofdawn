Meta Mid laners
It is recommended that you practice each Mid lane hero at least once, so that you know enemy skill types, cooldowns, and learn matchups and recommended skill builds for different heroes.

A Mid laner will determine the pick off and ganking routes (because mid laners usually rotate with the roamer).

These heroes are currently meta and will dominate (most of the time as shown in tournaments) if you mastered them (In no particular order):

Categories
Mid Laners are "unofficially" divided into different categories.

Utility Mid Laners
- Utility Mid Laners - are Mid Laners who have strong crowd control and utility to provide the team either having low or high damage.
- Official
- Unofficial

Hybrid Mid Laners
- Hybrid Mid Laners - are Mid Laners who can deal continuous damage at the same time they have strong crowd control and utility giving a great advantage to the team.
- Official
- Unofficial

Damage Mid Laners
- Damage Mid Laners - are Mid Laners who deal continuous or high amount of damage but have weaker crowd control and utility to provide their team.
- Official
- Unofficial

High Dive Mid Laners
- High Dive Mid Laners - are Mid Laners who can survive dangerous situations at a greater advantage through their high mobility skills. Melee Mage Hybrids, Magic Damage Speciality Melee & Mages with High Mobility are part of this category.
- Official
- Unofficial
