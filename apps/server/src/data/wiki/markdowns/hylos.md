# Bio

Amidst the misty mountains within the northern reaches of the Land of Dawn, resides the ancient and mysterious race of Centaurs, half-man, half-horse creatures. The Centaurs guard a fountain that rests among the highest mountain peak. This fountain is said to give the gift of Immortality and Infinite wisdom. Dark forces have relentlessly tried to gain the power found within the waters of this fountain, but only to be time and time again be thwarted by the centaurs.

However, as time went on, the centaurs became tired and attrition soon took hold. It was not until Hylos had arrived, where he threw his mastery of nature's wrath, he was able to crush and punish all the invaders of the fountain. He had become a legendary figure within the annals of the Land of Dawn.

# Story

> "Centaur guarding the Misty Mountains"

He lives in the Misty Mountains and belongs to the Centaur race. He has the ability to convert natural magic into life force, and at the same time he can continuously punish any enemy who invades the Misty Mountains by consuming his own life force.

# Abilities

## Thickened Blood

- Hylos gains 1.5 extra Max HP for every 1 extra Max Mana he possesses.  
- When out of Mana, Hylos can use his HP for skill casts.  
- Scales with: Max HP and Max Mana.

## Law and Order

- Cooldown: 12.0 / 11.2 / 10.4 / 9.6 / 8.8 / 8.0 seconds.  
- Mana Cost: 80 / 90 / 100 / 110 / 120 / 130.  
- Description: Hylos gains 50% Movement Speed and causes his next Basic Attack within 3 seconds to deal magic damage to the target, knock back and stun them for 1 second. This damage counts as Skill Damage. The extra Movement Speed rapidly decays over the duration and ends after the next Basic Attack.  
- Base Damage: 300 / 350 / 400 / 450 / 500 / 550.  
- Notes: The enhanced basic attack range is increased to 2.5 (1.25 times increase).

## Ring of Punishment

- Formula for total skill damage: Total skill DMG = ([70 + (30 × skill lv.) + 20% total magic power] + [5% + (3% × skill lv.)]) × number of stacks.  
- Cooldown: 1.0 second.  
- Mana Cost while active: 30 / 40 / 60 / 90 / 120 / 150 per second (consumes mana per second while active).  
- Description: Hylos releases the Ring of Punishment, dealing magic damage to nearby enemies per second while slowing them by 4%–6% and reducing their Attack Speed by 7.5%–15% for 2.5 seconds (up to 8 stacks). Each stack also increases the enemy's damage taken from the Ring of Punishment by 5%–20%. The Ring of Punishment consumes mana per second when it's active. Hylos can cancel the Ring of Punishment.  
- Magic Damage: 100 / 140 / 180 / 220 / 260 / 300.  
- Movement Speed Reduction per tick: 4% / 4% / 5% / 5% / 6% / 6%.  
- Damage Increase (enemy takes more damage from the ring): 5% / 8% / 11% / 14% / 17% / 20%.  
- Attack Speed Reduction examples: values scale up to large reductions at higher stacks.  
- Properties: Spell Vamp Ratio: 50%.  
- Notes: The skill lasts until Hylos runs out of Mana.

## Glorious Pathway

- Cooldown: 40.0 / 36.0 / 32.0 seconds.  
- Mana Cost: 150 / 300 / 450.  
- Description: Hylos creates a huge pathway in the target direction, reducing enemies' Movement by 75% for 1 second. The pathway lasts 6 seconds. When on the pathway, Hylos gains Slow Immunity and recovers 3% of his Max HP per second. Allied heroes moving along the pathway gain 60% extra Movement Speed.  
- Stats:  
  - HP Regen: 3% / 4% / 5%.  
  - Movement Speed Bonus for allies: 60% / 65% / 70%.  
  - Slowing Effect values listed in sources: 25% / 45% / 45%.

# Trivia

- - He is a centaur, which is based on the creature of Greek mythology.
- - He is inspired by Malachi of Moonton's Magic Rush.
- - Hylos is the only hero who can use HP to consume a skill.
- - Alongside other heroes, Hylos is one of the only heroes who can reduce his own HP through a skill.
- - Belerick used to consume a portion of his HP, but this was removed when he got revamped for the second time.
- - Hylos' skill is the first among the game's toggle skills.
- - Related heroes and references: Minotaur, Masha, X.Borg.  
- Notable skills referenced: Nature's Strike, Ring of Punishment.
