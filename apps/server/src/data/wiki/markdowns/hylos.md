> "Centaur guarding the Misty Mountains"

Story
- He lives in the Misty Mountains and belongs to the Centaur race.
- He has the ability to convert natural magic into life force, and at the same time he can continuously punish any enemy who invades the Misty Mountains by consuming his own life force.

Lore
- Amidst the misty mountains within the northern reaches of the Land of Dawn, resides the ancient and mysterious race of Centaurs, half-man, half-horse creatures.
- The Centaurs guard a fountain that rests among the highest mountain peak.
- This fountain is said to give the gift of Immortality and Infinite wisdom.
- Dark forces have relentlessly tried to gain the power found within the waters of this fountain, but only to be time and time again thwarted by the centaurs.
- However, as time went on, the centaurs became tired and attrition soon took hold.
- When Hylos arrived, he used his mastery of nature's wrath to crush and punish all the invaders of the fountain, becoming a legendary figure within the annals of the Land of Dawn.

Trivia / Notes
- He is a centaur, based on the creature from Greek mythology.  
- He is inspired by Malachi from Moonton's Magic Rush.  
- Hylos is the only hero who can use HP to cast skills.  
- Alongside a few others, Hylos is among the heroes who can reduce their own HP through a skill.  
- Belerick previously used to consume a portion of his HP for abilities, but this was removed after a later revamp.  
- Hylos' Ring of Punishment is among the earliest examples of a 'toggle' skill in the game.
