# Bio

Amidst the misty mountains within the northern reaches of the Land of Dawn, resides the ancient and mysterious race of Centaurs, half-man, half-horse creatures. The Centaurs guard a fountain that rests among the highest mountain peak. This fountain is said to give the gift of Immortality and Infinite wisdom. Dark forces have relentlessly tried to gain the power found within the waters of this fountain, but only to be time and time again be thwarted by the centaurs.

However, as time went on, the centaurs became tired and attrition soon took hold. It was not until Hylos had arrived, where he threw his mastery of nature's wrath, he was able to crush and punish all the invaders of the fountain. He had become a legendary figure within the annals of the Land of Dawn.

# Story

> "Centaur guarding the Misty Mountains"

He lives in the Misty Mountains and belongs to the Centaur race. He has the ability to convert natural magic into life force, and at the same time he can continuously punish any enemy who invades the Misty Mountains by consuming his own life force.

# Abilities

## Thickened Blood

Hylos gains 1.5 Max HP for every 1 extra Max Mana he possesses. When out of Mana, Hylos can use his HP for skill casts.

- Affected stats: Max HP, Max Mana
- Notes: When Mana is depleted, skills consume HP instead.

## Law and Order

Cooldown: 12.0 / 11.2 / 10.4 / 9.6 / 8.8 / 8.0 seconds

Mana cost: 80 / 90 / 100 / 110 / 120 / 130

Hylos gains 50% Movement Speed and causes his next Basic Attack within 3 seconds to deal base damage to the target, knock back and stun them for 1 second. This damage counts as Skill Damage. The extra Movement Speed rapidly decays over the duration and ends after the next Basic Attack.

- Base Damage: 300 / 350 / 400 / 450 / 500 / 550
- Effects: Movement Speed gain, enhanced Basic Attack that knocks back and stuns (1 second)
- Notes: The enhanced basic attack range is increased to 2.5 (1.25 times increase).

## Ring of Punishment

Cooldown: 1.0 second

Mana cost: 30 / 40 / 60 / 90 / 120 / 150 (consumed per second while active)

Hylos releases an AoE ring that deals magic damage to nearby enemies per second while slowing them and reducing their Attack Speed. The ring can stack on enemies (up to 8 stacks). Each stack also increases the enemy's damage taken from the Ring of Punishment. Hylos can cancel the Ring of Punishment manually. The skill lasts until Hylos runs out of Mana.

- Magic Damage (per tick): 100 / 140 / 180 / 220 / 260 / 300
- Movement Speed Reduction: 4% / 4% / 5% / 5% / 6% / 6%
- Attack Speed Reduction: 7.5% / 15% / 22.5% / 30% / 37.5% / 45%
- Damage Increase from stacks: 5% / 8% / 11% / 14% / 17% / 20%
- Properties: Spell Vamp Ratio: 50%

Total skill damage formula (per number of stacks): ((70 + (30 × skill level) + 20% total magic power) + (5% + (3% × skill level))) × number of stacks.

- Notes: The Ring of Punishment consumes Mana per second when active. The skill lasts until Hylos runs out of Mana or cancels it.

## Glorious Pathway

Cooldown: 40.0 / 36.0 / 32.0 seconds

Mana cost: 150 / 300 / 450

Hylos creates a huge pathway in the target direction. The pathway lasts 6 seconds.

- Slowing effect on enemies: reduces enemies' Movement by 75% for 1 second (listed values vary by level)
- While on the pathway, Hylos gains Slow Immunity and recovers a percentage of his Max HP per second.
- Allied heroes moving along the pathway gain extra Movement Speed.

Stats by level:

- HP Regen: 3% / 4% / 5% (recovered per second while on the pathway)
- Movement Speed Bonus for allies: 60% / 65% / 70%
- Slowing Effect values: 25% / 45% / 45%

Terms: Slow Immunity, Max HP, Movement Speed.

# Trivia

- He is a centaur, which is based on the creature of Greek mythology.
- He is inspired by Malachi of Moonton's Magic Rush.
- Hylos is the only hero who can use HP to consume a skill.
- Alongside other heroes, Hylos is one of the few who can reduce his own HP through a skill.
- Belerick used to consume a portion of his HP, but this was removed when he got revamped for the second time.
- Hylos' skill is the first among the game's toggle skills.
