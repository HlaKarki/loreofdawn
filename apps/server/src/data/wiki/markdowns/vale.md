# Story

> "Heir to Wind Fort, wind mage"

A magician of Wind Fort and the only genius in the entire family who can change the form of wind magic at will. Once a close friend of Valir of Fire Throat. After the changes in the Land of Fire and Valir left his hometown, Vale has always adhered to the agreement he made with him, waiting for his return.

# Lore

> Valir, my beloved brother.  
> You lost everything the day we made the pact.  
> I'll see to it that the Fire Kingdom restores everything to the way it once was.  
> Even if you still wish to avenge your cruel father, I do not blame you as long as you can cast away the storm within your heart.  
> Nothing is as important as your happiness, not even my life.

Vale fell into deep contemplation as he wrote his letter to Valir. Those first few lines brought Vale back to the days of his youth, when he and Valir were the closest of friends.

As Vale fell further into nostalgia, he remembered the day they met at a royal party. Ever since then, they both felt a strong affinity towards each other's magic. For a long time, Vale and Valir were inseparable. Together they left many memories in the Valley of the Winds, the Fire Kingdom and many other exotic lands. When unrest flooded the Fire Kingdom, Valir quickly made his leave. Only Vale knows exactly how much Valir sacrificed that day.

As time went on, the day of the pact was coming soon. Vale's magical abilities to control winds had grown more sophisticated over the years. In fact, he was the only member of his family who could change the winds with magic. Yet, Vale had never shown his magic to anyone before. Vale waited and waited for the day of the pact to come. On that day, he would put on a spectacular display of wind magic for Valir. Vale believes that Valir will see his magic, remember the precious times they had together, and return how things were in their youth.

# Abilities

- Windtalk (stack)  
  - Effect: Buff — Speed Up  
  - Description: Vale gains 1 stack each time he gets a kill or an assist. Each stack increases Movement Speed by 8. Can stack up to 10 times.  
  - Extra Movement Speed per stack: 8 / 16 / 24 / 32 / 40 / 48 / 56 / 64 / 72 / 80

## Skill 1

- Wind Blade  
  - Effect: AOE  
  - Cooldown: 6.0 / 5.6 / 5.2 / 4.8 / 4.4 / 4.0 s  
  - Mana Cost: 60 / 65 / 70 / 75 / 80 / 85  
  - Spell Vamp: 50%  
  - Description: Vale unleashes two wind blades toward the left and right of a target area.  
  - Base Damage: 250 / 290 / 330 / 370 / 410 / 450 (Magic Damage Skill)

## Skill 2

- Windblow  
  - Effect: CC, AOE  
  - Cooldown: 10.0 s  
  - Mana Cost: 90 / 95 / 100 / 105 / 110 / 115  
  - Description: Vale sends a whirlwind in the target direction, dealing damage to enemies hit and knocking them airborne for 1 second (each enemy can only be knocked airborne once). When the whirlwind reaches its destination, it remains for 1.6 seconds while continuously dealing sustained damage to enemies in range.  
  - Base Damage: 300 / 320 / 340 / 360 / 380 / 400  
  - Sustained Damage (total while active): 200 / 240 / 280 / 320 / 360 / 400  
  - Notes:
    - The sustained damage is applied per hit and can occur up to 4 times.  
    - The sustained damage does not trigger damage-on-hit effects.  
    - During the cast delay, you can use Flicker to reposition the source of the whirlwind.  
  - Terms: Airborne, Movement Speed, Magic Damage Skill

## Ultimate

- Windstorm  
  - Effect: CC, AOE (Pull)  
  - Cooldown: 40.0 / 36.0 / 32.0 s  
  - Mana Cost: 150 / 200 / 250  
  - Description: Vale summons a windstorm at the target location, dealing sustained damage to enemies in the area while slowing them by 20% and continuously pulling them into its center. After 1.6 seconds the windstorm explodes, dealing base damage; remaining whirlwinds are also pulled into the center.  
  - Sustained Damage per tick: 150 / 225 / 300 (can hit up to 6 times)  
  - Base Damage (explosion): 1000 / 1200 / 1400 (Magic Damage Skill)  
  - Notes:
    - The sustained damage is applied per hit and can deal up to 6 times.
