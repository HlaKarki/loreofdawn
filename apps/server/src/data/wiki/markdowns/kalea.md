# Kalea

# Profile

She is the face of MLBB Season 36: Breaking Waves.

- Alias: Surging Wave
- Origin: Solari Isle, Vonetis Sea
- Gender: Female
- Height: Shorter than 160cm
- Relationships: Great Serpent (companions)
- Japanese Voice Actor: Miho Ichidera (一寺美穂)

# Story

> "Descendent of the Great Serpent that swims gracefully through the Vonetis Sea with her serpent tail."

The descendant of the legendary Great Serpent, Kalea once had trouble accepting her human-like body. But after discovering who she was, she learned to fight with her fists and powerful serpent tail while healing allies with the power of the Great Serpent surging inside her. Always full of energy, she is the proud protector of the Vonetis Sea.

However, when faced with gratitude from others, she tends to put on a facade of arrogance to hide her shyness–after all, she has to live up to her reputation as the descendant of the Great Serpent!

# Abilities

## Surge of Life

After casting a skill, Kalea creates a Water Zone on the ground. When Kalea casts a skill within this zone, she will absorb it to enhance her next three Basic Attacks with different effects:

- First Basic Attack: Kalea dashes to the target, dealing scaling damage based on level and her stats and slightly knockbacks the target. The first enhanced basic attack increases her attack range to approximately 3.2 units and the indicator is visible to enemies.
- Second Basic Attack: Kalea unleashes a flurry of attacks, dealing scaling damage based on level and her stats and recovering healing based on level and her magic power for herself and the nearby lowest HP ally. This attack cannot be manually cancelled and takes approximately 1.2 seconds to execute. It launches 4 projectiles that lock onto a nearby ally to heal them; the heal target is determined at the time of the attack and does not change mid-animation.
- Third Basic Attack: Kalea uses her tail to strike the area around her target, dealing scaling area damage based on level and her stats and creating a new Water Zone.

Enhanced Basic Attacks are not affected by Attack Speed.

Notes:
- The enhanced basic attacks last as a 12-second buff, which persists as long as there is unused enhanced attack(s).
- Each next enhanced attack can be manually halted except for the second enhanced attack which auto-executes.
- If the enemy dies or becomes untargetable during an enhanced attack, the animation persists; the third enhanced basic attack will be reserved.
- The nearby ally healed must be within approximately 7.5 units.
- Each Water Zone lasts 6 seconds, will combine with another Water Zone if Kalea is within the old one, and is created centered on Kalea. It is impossible to have two Water Zones from Kalea exist simultaneously.
- The Water Zone gives vision within approximately 9 units. Enemies entering the range are sighted if they are not concealed. When a Water Zone is created in a bush while enemies are inside it, the entire bush remains visible to Kalea even after she leaves.
- Kalea does not benefit from enemy Water Zones nor combine with them.

## Wavebreaker

Kalea slams her fist into the ground, creating a wave that deals scaling AOE damage to nearby enemies and slows them by 40% for 1.5 seconds.

Base Damage by level: 180 / 200 / 220 / 240 / 260 / 280

Notes:
- The wave has a radius of approximately 3 units, which is slightly shorter than her first enhanced basic attack range.

## Tidal Strike

Kalea charges forward and body slams the first enemy hero she encounters, knocking them airborne for 0.6 seconds while dealing scaling damage based on level and her stats. If she collides with an obstacle or touches the edge of a Water Zone during her charge, Kalea will leap into the air. Cast the skill again to slam down at a target location, dealing scaling damage based on level and her stats and knocking enemies airborne for 0.6 seconds.

Cooldown by level: 13.0 / 12.6 / 12.2 / 11.8 / 11.4 / 11.0

Base Damage values by hit (by level): 150 / 175 / 200 / 225 / 250 / 275 (initial charge) and 150 / 175 / 200 / 225 / 250 / 275 (slam)

Notes:
- Fully affects concealed and invisible enemies.
- After jumping to a wall or obstacle, Kalea has approximately 2 seconds to select the landing area until she descends.
- If Kalea is affected by crowd control while perched on a wall, she will be unperched and the wall stance ends. If she jumps on a temporary wall and the wall disappears while midair, she is not unperched.
- The cooldown begins as soon as the ability is cast.
- The landing animation can be interrupted or repositioned by certain enemy crowd controls.
- The airborne effect on landing only occurs if Kalea successfully lands.
- This ability triggers effects that respond to dashes twice due to two movement phases.

## Tsunami Slam

Kalea grabs an enemy hero with her tail and slams them down in the joystick direction, dealing scaling damage to the target and nearby enemies.

Cooldown by level: 55.0 / 50.0 / 45.0

Base Damage by level: 300 / 400 / 500

Notes:
- Kalea is CC Immune for the entire duration while performing this ultimate.
- Maximum grab distance is approximately 7.5 units.
- The ability has two phases: Kalea first grabs an enemy, performing a stun, and then continuously applies airborne effect while making the target move along with her. Upon landing, a Water Zone is created at the landing spot and Kalea loses CC Immunity. She then dashes toward the joystick direction a short distance.
- Because of the two movement phases, this ability triggers movement-related effects twice.
- The landing area is visible to Kalea only.
- Kalea cannot use other abilities, battle spells, or equipment actives during the ultimate's duration.
- Both Kalea and the target can be displaced if the landing area is over an obstacle.
- This skill is disabled in certain map-specific zones despite not having the Mobility tag.

# Trivia

- The name Kalea is of Hawaiian origin, meaning "brightness" or "clear." It is often associated with Hawaiian culture and spirituality rather than a specific religion. In Hawaii, names often carry deep meanings and connections to nature, family, and heritage. But her design and inspiration are taken from Southeast Asian cultures.
- Her design features an aquamarine color scheme, reflecting the vibrant hues of coastal waters and emphasizing her role as the protector of the Vonetis Sea.
- Her stance represents S which symbolizes the great serpent.
- She loves coconuts and it's her favorite.
- She and are the only heroes currently with a Support/Fighter role.
