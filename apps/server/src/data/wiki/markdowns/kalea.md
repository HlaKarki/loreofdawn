# Profile

She is the face of MLBB Season 36: Breaking Waves.

- Name: Kalea
- Alias: Surging Wave
- Origin: Solari Isle, Vonetis Sea
- Gender: Female
- Height: Shorter than 160cm
- Relationships: Great Serpent (companions)
- Japanese VA: Miho Ichidera (一寺美穂)

Kalea is the descendant of the legendary Great Serpent and protector of the Vonetis Sea.

# Story

> "Descendent of the Great Serpent that swims gracefully through the Vonetis Sea with her serpent tail."

The descendant of the legendary Great Serpent, Kalea once had trouble accepting her human-like body. But after discovering who she was, she learned to fight with her fists and powerful serpent tail while healing allies with the power of the Great Serpent surging inside her. Always full of energy, she is the proud protector of the Vonetis Sea. However, when faced with gratitude from others, she tends to put on a facade of arrogance to hide her shyness–after all, she has to live up to her reputation as the descendant of the Great Serpent!

Out in the Vonetis Sea, an island called Solari Isle was facing the storm of the century. As the waves rose higher and higher, muffled thunder rumbled through the gray clouds.

The villagers on the island were in a panic–the typhoon had come without warning and one fishing boat had not yet returned from the sea. They prayed to an ancient idol, hoping that the girl with the blood of the Great Serpent would hear them and bring their kin back safely.

That girl's name was Kalea.

The children, hugging their toys made in the image of the Great Serpent, began to sing a song.

> "The wind howls; the waves roar. O Great Serpent, mighty as a mountain, hold back the raging tides..."

Their singing gradually faded in the increasingly fierce storm. Suddenly, a serpent tail swept through the waves and headed towards the depths.

Legend has it that a thousand years ago, a gigantic serpent emerged from the ocean to protect this island. It swallowed the typhoons and held off waves as high as the mountains with its body until it had exhausted all of its power. Before the Great Serpent perished, it left behind an egg. A thousand years later, a girl miraculously hatched from the egg. She had a human body, a gift from the land, and a sea serpent's tail, a blessing from the ocean. The goddess Kadita named her Kalea and took her to the sea where she secretly watched her grow up.

> "Oh Mister Sea Turtle, why do you swim so slowly?"

Kalea joked, as she wrapped her tail around the sea turtle and flung it across the sea with surprising strength. After seeing the splash way in the distance, she nodded with satisfaction.

> "You're welcome!"

> "Silly sharks, why are you so weak?"

Kalea spun her tail at great speed, stirring up a strong undercurrent. The sharks were tossed up and down, but Kalea effortlessly swam off.

> "Oh, it must be because your tails are so tiny!"

Kalea had awakened the power of the Great Serpent at an early age, but she was never able to control it. Her power would surge outwards without warning, turning the seas upside down, much to the dismay of the creatures living there. Even Kalea began to worry. Every time she tried to channel the full power of the Great Serpent, it would disperse out of her control.

Kalea firmly believed that it was her weak, human-like body that was holding her back:

> "Look at those feeble humans. They're slower than the turtles, and even scared of the sharks!"

If only she didn't have these bothersome arms and legs, she thought, then she could swim even faster! If she could finally take the form of the Great Serpent, no monster in the sea or on land would be a match for her! Oh, how she longed for that day. Kadita, who had been secretly watching over her, appeared and explained that she could not just shed her skin like a serpent. Unwilling to accept Kadita's words, Kalea challenged all manner of powerful creatures, hoping to prove herself worthy of the Great Serpent's power.

One day, while wandering the beaches of Solari Isle, Kalea was attacked by a swarm of corrupted elementals. Hearing the commotion, the villagers, led by Badang, came to her rescue. Badang erected giant walls of sand with his fists to split the enemy, while the villagers threw spears and fishing nets to subdue them. Ultimately, their combined efforts were successful in defeating the rogue elementals.

> "These humans... maybe they're not so weak after all..."

Kalea began to secretly study how Badang fought with his fists as she quietly learned to peel bananas with her fingers. As the descendant of the Great Serpent, she was always reluctant to lower herself to learn from mortals, yet she couldn't help but be curious about their peculiar lives. Even though she tried to keep her distance from the villagers, she found her power was inexplicably connected to them. Whenever her power surged outward, it would always seek out those in need and heal them.

> "I'm just here to test my strength against Badang!"

Kalea would explain as she appeared more and more often around Solari Isle. Badang would always smile helplessly and act defeated. He saw from their sparring that the girl's heart was no longer set on just proving her strength.

Knocking down a whole coconut tree with one punch, freeing a stranded fishing boat with her tail–such acts were not just for her pride. When the villagers gathered to thank her, Kalea would always put her hands on her hips and proudly say something like,

> "I wasn't trying to help you, I was just bored and exercising my tail!"

But all the while, the villagers could see her face blushing from the attention.

No matter how much she tried, Kalea was no closer to becoming the Great Serpent. Her body barely grew at all, but despite that, the villagers began turning to her for help, just as they had once turned to her ancestor in times of need; times like now.

A tail sliced through the waves at extreme speed. On the surface, the howling wind caused the waves to surge to towering heights. A lone fishing boat, caught in the storm, was lifted high into the air by the waves before being slammed down with great force.

With a splash, Kalea leaped to action. She grabbed the side of the boat and gently deposited the fallen fishermen back on board with her tail.

> "Humans really are fragile! But you're pretty brave to be out here, even when you don't have a tail!"

Kalea proclaimed while standing proudly and tapping the deck with her tail. The Great Serpent's power surged from her body and flowed into the fishermen, causing them to cough up the seawater they swallowed. Miraculously, they regained their strength as if nothing had happened.

> "I'm sorry the boat can't be saved, but you two will get to witness the power of the Great Serpent!"

Without waiting for a reply, Kalea grabbed the hands of the fishermen and dove off the boat as she zoomed towards the shore. With her tail, she bounced over the waves, riding the wind and breaking through the surf. Kalea saw her reflection flickering in the parting waters; her tailing, swaying with purpose, and her hands, holding tightly onto the fishermen. That was who she was. She was no longer fixated on becoming the Great Serpent. As for the power that she once sought to contain, she finally realized she had already found its purpose.

> "I guess this body isn't so bad after all!"

With the turbulent waves behind her, the shore came into sight. A child hugging a toy of the Great Serpent excitedly pointed towards the sea and shouted:

> "Look! It's Kalea!"

With her serpent tail, this proud descendant of the Great Serpent swims gracefully through the Vonetis Sea, aspiring to one day carry the full power of her heritage.

# Abilities

## Surge of Life

After casting a skill, Kalea creates a Water Zone on the ground. When Kalea casts a skill within this zone, she will absorb it to enhance her next three Basic Attacks with different effects:

- First Basic Attack: Kalea dashes to the target, dealing scaling damage based on level and Kalea's stats. The first enhanced basic attack increases her attack range to 3.2 and slightly knocks back the target. This attack is a dash.
- Second Basic Attack: Kalea unleashes a flurry of attacks, dealing scaling damage and recovering scaling HP for herself and the nearby lowest HP ally.
- Third Basic Attack: Kalea uses her tail to strike the area around her target, dealing scaling damage and creating a new Water Zone.

Enhanced Basic Attacks are not affected by Attack Speed.

Notes:

- The enhanced basic attacks last as a 12-second buff, which persists as long as there is unused attack(s).
- Each next attack can be manually halted.
- The second enhanced basic attack cannot be manually cancelled, and takes approximately 1.2 seconds to execute.
- The second attack launches 4 projectiles that lock onto a nearby ally to heal them, and does not change midway if the ally has more health than another nearby ally.
- If the enemy dies or becomes untargetable, the heal from the second attack will not occur but the animation persists; the third enhanced basic attack will be reserved.
- The nearby ally must be at least within approximately 7.5 units.
- The first and third basic attacks are dashes, hence this ability triggers movement-related effects twice.

Water Zone properties:

- Each Water Zone lasts 6 seconds, will combine with another if Kalea is within the old zone and is created centered at Kalea.
- It is impossible to have two Water Zones exist at the same time.
- Kalea does not benefit from enemy Water Zones in any way nor combine with them.
- The Water Zone gives vision within 9 units. Enemies entering the range are sighted if they are not concealed.
- When a Water Zone is created in a bush when enemies are inside it, the entire bush is still visible to Kalea even after she leaves.

## Wavebreaker

Kalea slams her fist into the ground, creating a wave that deals scaling damage to nearby enemies and slows them by 40% for 1.5 seconds.

Level scaling (base damage by level): 180 / 200 / 220 / 240 / 260 / 280

Notes:

- The wave has a radius of 3, which is slightly shorter than her first enhanced basic attack.

## Tidal Strike

Kalea charges forward and body slams the first enemy data she encounters, knocking them airborne for 0.6s while dealing scaling damage. If she collides with an obstacle or touches the edge of a Water Zone during her charge, Kalea will leap into the air. Cast the skill again to slam down at a target location, dealing scaling damage and knocking enemies airborne for 0.6s.

Cooldown by level: 13.0 / 12.6 / 12.2 / 11.8 / 11.4 / 11.0

Base Damage (two-stage): 150 / 175 / 200 / 225 / 250 / 275 (first stage) and 150 / 175 / 200 / 225 / 250 / 275 (second stage)

Notes:

- Fully affects concealed and invisible enemies.
- After jumping to a wall or obstacle, she has approximately 2 seconds to select the landing area until she descends.
- If Kalea is affected by crowd control effects while perched, she is unperched from the wall and the remaining effect is wasted.
- The cooldown begins as soon as the ability is cast.
- The landing animation can be blocked or repositioned by certain enemy abilities.
- The airborne upon landing is only performed if Kalea successfully lands.
- This ability triggers movement-related effects twice.

## Tsunami Slam

Kalea grabs an enemy data with her tail and slams them down in the joystick's direction, dealing scaling damage to the target and nearby enemies.

Cooldown by level: 55.0 / 50.0 / 45.0

Base Damage by level: 300 / 400 / 500

Notes:

- Kalea is CC Immune for the entire duration of the ability.
- Max distance of grab is approximately 7.5 units.
- The ability has two phases:
  - Kalea first grabs an enemy, performing a stun.
  - Kalea continuously applies an airborne effect and makes the target enemy move along with her. After this, she creates a Water Zone on the landing spot and loses CC Immunity. She then dashes towards the joystick direction a distance of approximately 1.5 units.
- This ability triggers movement-related effects twice.
- The landing area is visible to Kalea only.
- Kalea cannot use any other abilities, battle spells, or equipment for the duration.
- Both Kalea and the target will be displaced if the landing area is over an obstacle.

# Trivia

- The name Kalea is of Hawaiian origin, meaning "brightness" or "clear." It is often associated with Hawaiian culture and spirituality rather than a specific religion. In Hawaii, names often carry deep meanings and connections to nature, family, and heritage. But her design and inspiration are taken from Southeast Asian cultures.
- Her design features an aquamarine color scheme, reflecting the vibrant hues of coastal waters and emphasizing her role as the protector of the Vonestis Sea.
- Her stance represents S which symbolizes the great serpent.
- She loves coconuts and it's her favorite.
- She and are the only heroes currently with a Support/Fighter role.
