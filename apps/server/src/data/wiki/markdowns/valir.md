# Story

> "Heir of Fire Throat, fire mage."

In his hometown of Agelta, he showed an amazing talent for arcane energy manipulation when he first arrived at the Magic Academy, so he was valued by his mentor Gord. But Gord got very angry when he discovered that he had incorporated the spells of his hometown into the arcane, thinking that he had defiled the arcane. After a battle between the master and the disciple, Valir left the Magic Academy.

# Bio

In the southwest of the Land of Dawn lies an active volcano that brought deadly peril to all who dared approach.

> "Inferno of Fire"

It was also known as the place of trial for Mages eager to master flame spells. After leaving the magic academy, Valir dwelled himself there for five years while carrying a ten-year promise with Vale. He hoped that day would come more quickly, but at the same time, he also hoped that day would never come. One day, Valir cast his arcane flame magic towards the volcano but the result was not to his expectation.

In the western desert, there was a man who was looking for Valir. The betrayal of years was something he could never forgive. With great anger and hate, and to maintain the reputation of the magic academy, he decided to track down the traitor. The moment he felt an unusual arcane power releasing from the volcano, he smiled.

The long-term inhabitance in this "Inferno of Fire" brought changes to Valir's body. His skin was burned by the flames of the volcano, but in order to become the Son of Flames, he had to combine the power of his arcane flame magic with the volcano's inferno fire. His body and his will must withstand the trials of these scalding lava. Suddenly, a strong wind blew, bringing a familiar sense to Valir. Vale had come! They had never met each other since childhood.

This encounter led to the first fight between them, between fire and wind. The Windtalker and the Son of Flames cast their all magic to attack each other. No words were exchanged, but they shared a common thought - they didn't want to kill each other. However, their fight generated great power, which was aggravated the volcano. If the volcano burst, they would surely die in an inferno.

> "Five years left - can't you wait Vale!?"

Before Vale said anything, Valir heard another familiar voice, it was Gord. Gord's presence interrupted this fight between these brothers. He wanted to abolish the arcane magic of Valir, because in his mind, his pure arcane wisdom should never be tarnished. However, the arcane spell he cast to Valir was stopped by Vale. Vale turned to sympathy upon looking at Valir's tortured body. He didn't want anyone to hurt his brother. However, Valir didn't need such sympathy.

One of them was his master who wanted to destroy his apprentice for his apparent betrayal. Another one of them had been his closest brother. However, he was also the son of the one who killed Valir's father. The sacred flame would never be quenched. Valir successfully combined the power of his arcane flame magic with the inferno fire and burst out a powerful energy. With this power, the lava became unstable. Meanwhile, Valir gained a mighty body while his eyes became scarlet flames. At that time, Vale could understand his father's worries. This kind of power astonished Gord, and as his teacher, he would rather kill his student than to let him be possessed by this crazy power.

Valir's flame, Vale's wind, and Gord's arcane power aggravated the volcano, which led to a great collapse. After that, Vale and Gord tried to find Valir, but they couldn't find him. Vale knew that in his heart Valir was still alive, and that their ten-year promise was not yet broken. The five-years made Valir formidable, what was to happen after another five years? Vale knew the next time they encountered each other, it would no longer be as friends and brothers. Rather, it would be about the greatest fight for their lives.

# Abilities

## Ashing

- Valir's skills apply a stack of Ablaze to enemies hit, dealing them Magic Damage equal to 0.6% of their Max HP per second for 4s.
- At 3 stacks, the fire detonates, dealing Magic Damage euqal to 6% of the target's Max HP to the enemy and stunning them for 1s (the same target can only be stunned by Valir's skills once every 7s.)
- Terms: Stun, Max HP

## Burst Fireball

- Mana Cost: 50 / 58 / 66 / 74 / 82 / 90
- Base Damage: 235 / 270 / 305 / 340 / 375 / 410
- Explosion Damage: 118 / 135 / 153 / 170 / 188 / 205
- Description: Valir launches a fireball that explodes upon hitting the first target, dealing damage (scaling damage based on level and stats) and slowing them by 30% for 1s. The lingering flame on the ground will explode after 1s, dealing explosion damage (scaling damage based on level and stats). Valir recharges 1 fireball every 9s and can hold up to 2 fireball charges. Hitting an enemy hero or Legend Creep with this skill will immediately restore a fireball charge, while hitting a Minion will restore 50% of the recharge time.
- Terms: Energy Charging Time, Magic Skill, Movement Speed

## Searing Torrent

- Mana Cost: 80 / 86 / 92 / 98 / 104 / 110
- Base Damage: 200 / 220 / 240 / 260 / 280 / 300
- Firewall Damage (per second): 80 / 88 / 96 / 104 / 112 / 120
- Spell Vamp Ratio: 50%
- Description: Valir unleashes a torrent of flames in the target direction, dealing damage to enemies hit and knocking them to the end of its path. Vengeance Flame: The torrent forms a fire wall at the end of its path that lasts 4s and deals firewall damage to nearby enemies per second. Enemies hit by the torrent or the firewall are slowed by 25% for 1s.
- Terms: Knocked Back, Magic Skill, Movement Speed

## Vengeance Flame

- Mana Cost: 100 / 125 / 150
- Cooldown: 36.0 / 33.0 / 30.0
- Damage Increase: 30% / 35% / 40%
- Description: Valir removes all debuffs on himself and conjures 4 Vengeance Flames that last 9s. Each of his subsequent skills consumes 1 Vengeance Flame to deal 30% extra base damage. The effects of both Burst Fireball and Searing Torrent can be enhanced. Meanwhile, Valir gains 40% extra Movement Speed that decays over 3s.
- Terms: Remove CC, BUFF
