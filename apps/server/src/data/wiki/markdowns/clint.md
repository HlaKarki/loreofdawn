Story

> "A sharpshooter full of righteousness and justice."

Clint is a sheriff in a small western town. He ordered the execution of Kane's nephew Blandy. The residents were afraid of Kane so they had handed Blandy over to a gang and expelled Clint. But Blandy still didn't let the town go. Clint came out to guard the town in its time of crisis. After the matter was over, he went to Los Pecados to completely eradicate Kane's evil forces.

Lore

On the desolate frontier of Moniyan (Moniyan Empire), the border with Agelta (Agelta Drylands), there was an ordinary town. The residents here lived a peaceful and stable life away from the hustle and bustle of human society. They worked during the day, and at night they went to the tavern in the town for a few drinks to relax.

Such an orderly and stable life was all due to Clint, the Sheriff of the town. Clint was a famous fast gunner, with unmatched marksmanship, and was known for his fastest speed of drawing gun. The dutiful Clint always took maintaining the security of the town as his duty. He loved his job and duty, and believed in the fairness and justice of the law. He hated evil as his enemy, and had been protecting the town from robbers and bandits for many years. Therefore, he was loved by the residents of the town.

On an ordinary night, Clint went on his daily patrol with his gun as usual. In the middle of his patrol, he suddenly heard gunshots and shouts from the tavern. Clint knew instinctively that a group of uninvited guests had broken into the peaceful town.

Clint immediately rushed to the tavern, where a group of weirdly dressed drunkards were smashing wildly. Many residents were beaten to death by them, while the owner of the tavern was shot and killed by the gangster leader. In the face of Clint’s warning, the gangsters were not frightened, but blatantly declared that the town would soon be taken over by them, and advised Clint not to meddle.

What the gangsters did angered Clint, so he drew out his gun and fought fiercely with them. Finally, except for a minion who escaped by chance, the rest of the gangsters were either killed by Clint’s gun or lost their combat effectiveness and were captured without resistance.

At noon the next day, Clint took the captured villains to the center of the town, preparing to give the criminals a just punishment under the witness of all the residents. The angry people also demanded severe punishment of these outsiders. But just then, the villain leader revealed his identity: he was Blandy, the nephew of Waldo Kane, the most powerful boss in Los Pecados. His men had fled back to Los Pecados the night before to deliver a message that if anything happened to Blandy, his uncle would raze the small town and everyone would be buried with him.

Blandy had barely finished speaking when the angry crowd suddenly quieted. They knew the City of Sin and Waldo Kane, and they knew their ways of doing things. Soon, the residents wavered. They advocated releasing Blandy and hoped that Blandy would “forget” his unpleasant experience in the town.

In the eyes of Clint, who hated evil most, this was a compromise with criminals and a trampling on justice. Regardless of the residents’ opposition, he threw Blandy into prison and announced that he would hang him three days later to avenge the tavern owner who had been shot.

After no more than three days, a large number of gangsters came to the outskirts of the town. They brought lots of weapons, demanding that the residents immediately hand over Blandy, and offer a large ransom to apologize.

The safety of the town was greatly threatened by those criminals. Clint came out boldly and called on everyone to take up arms to fight back the gangs and defend their homeland. But what Clint didn’t expect was that the residents and colleagues he had tried to protect did not intend to stand and die with him. Instead, they blamed Clint for his stubbornness and inflexibility, which they said brought disaster to the town. After a hasty vote, the residents relieved Clint of his post as Sheriff and drove him out of town.

The residents then released Blandy and paid a large ransom. But even so, the ferocious Blandy still chose treachery, leading the gangs to occupy the town and preparing to burn it.

By this time, Clint had carried his bags and walked far out of town. However, when the fire in the small town soared into the sky, Clint could not ignore it. Even if those people he had protected betrayed him, the law of justice in his mind could not be erased; he could not stand by and watch the gangsters slaughter the town. Even if he was alone, Clint would fight crime to the end.

When the gangsters were destroying the town, Clint appeared as if a soldier had come from heaven. His invincible marksmanship and amazing speed of drawing gun became the criminals' worst nightmare that night. After a fierce battle, Clint was scarred, and the gangsters suffered heavy casualties. Blandy, wounded by Clint, was protected by the gangsters to escape.

The surviving residents realized their mistakes and begged Clint for forgiveness, hoping that he would stay and protect the town. But Clint only smiled and didn’t answer. That night, Clint rode his old donkey, left the town quietly, and set foot on the road to the City of Sin. He knew that if he didn’t go to find Kane, Kane would come to find him.

Trivia
- Clint was the very first hero to be reworked. His rework was intended to nerf him so he would be "a marksman who's good at poking and consuming enemies from distance, rather than a hero who charges up and deals crowd damage like an assassin" (see Patch Notes 1.1.50).
- Many of his old skills were reused or repurposed for other heroes.
- Clint has the most redesigns out of all heroes in the game and currently has 6 redesigns.
- Clint's name is presumably a reference to Clint Eastwood (see wikipedia: Clint Eastwood).
- Despite having a double-barreled shotgun by design, his weapon behaves as a rifle that deals in a straight line instead of a typical shotgun spread.
- Before revamp, one of his skills could blind enemies (reducing hit rate); after the revamp that mechanic was removed.
- His ultimate is one of the few skills in the game that can stack uses.
- Clint's Valentine skin references the American hard rock band Guns N' Roses.
- Clint is one of the heroes who doesn't have a basic skin.
- He's the first hero to have two skins titled "Rock and Roll" and "Operator CL".
