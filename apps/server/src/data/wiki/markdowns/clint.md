# Abilities

## Double Shot

After each skill cast, Clint's next Basic Attack within 4 seconds penetrates a line of enemies, dealing damage based on level and stats. This damage can trigger attack effects, and the part affected by Physical Attack can critically strike.

- Enhanced Basic Attack Range: 7.
- Single-target attack effects such as 's Unique Passive - Judgement or Marskman Talent Weakness Finder prioritizes the targeted enemy of this enhanced basic attack.
- The enhanced basic attack can stack multiple 's Unique Passive - Thunderbolt stacks within a single attack.

## Quick Draw

Cooldown: 8.0 / 7.4 / 6.8 / 6.2 / 5.6 / 5.0 seconds. Mana cost: 50.

Clint fires 5 bullets in quick succession in the target direction, each dealing damage based on level and stats to the enemy hit. The bullets will spread evenly across enemies in a fan-shaped area. Enemies hit by multiple bullets will take reduced damage on subsequent bullets (30%–50% for the second bullet depending on level).

- Base Damage by Level: 250 / 280 / 310 / 340 / 370 / 400.
- Damage of the Second Bullet Hit on the Same Target: 30% / 34% / 38% / 42% / 46% / 50%.
- Decay Ratio per Bullet Received (example values): level 1: 100% / 30% / 5% / 5% / 5%; level 6: 100% / 50% / 5% / 5% / 5%.

Notes:
- This skill has reduced foreswing time by 50% and reduced backswing time by 20% (Patch Notes 1.9.32).
- The 3rd to 5th bullets deal 5% of the initial damage.
- This skill's attacks prioritize enemy heroes the most.

## Trapping Recoil

Cooldown: 10.0 / 9.6 / 9.2 / 8.8 / 8.4 / 8.0 seconds. Mana cost: 65 / 75 / 85 / 95 / 105 / 115.

Clint shoots a trap net in the target direction, slightly jumping back while dealing damage based on level and stats to the first enemy hit and immobilizing them for 1.2 seconds. Successfully hitting an enemy reduces the cooldown of this skill by 40%.

- Skill Range (Patch Notes 1.9.32): 3.8.
- Dash Distance (Patch Notes 1.9.32): 3.
- Base Damage by Level: 140 / 160 / 180 / 200 / 220 / 240.

Terms: Blink; Immobilize; Physical Damage Skill.

Notes:
- The dashing speed is increased by 20% (Patch Notes 1.9.32).

## Grenade Bombardment

Cooldown: 3.0 seconds. Grenade recharge time: 12.0 / 11.0 / 10.0 seconds. Mana cost: 50 / 55 / 60.

Clint launches a grenade in the target direction that explodes on the first enemy hit, dealing damage based on level and stats and slowing them by 25% for 1.2 seconds. Clint gains a grenade recharge every 12–10 seconds (up to 5).

- Skill Range (Patch Notes 1.9.32): 7.
- Base Damage by Level: 280 / 340 / 400.

Terms: Physical Damage Skill; Movement Speed; Skill Charging Time.

Notes:
- This skill has reduced foreswing time and backswing time by 33% (Patch Notes 1.9.32).
