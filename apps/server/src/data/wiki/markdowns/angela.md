# Story

> "Artificial human made in Laboratory 1718"

An android created by the founder of Laboratory 1718, Dr. Baker (Laboratory 1718; Dr. Baker). It was the first life form created without using human body or soul. In order to gain the knowledge of her, other scientists in the lab went after Dr. Baker. Before being taken away, Dr. Baker stuffed her into a flight capsule to escape.

# Lore

Dr. Baker was one of the co-founders of Laboratory 1718, an institution created with the aim of bringing peace to the world. But as his colleagues — the other scientists — fell deeper into depraved human weaponization experiments, Dr. Baker felt he had no choice but to leave and pursue a new project.

Renouncing the questionable ethics of human experimentation, he endeavored to create a new kind of lifeform, solely from a mechanical skeleton, puppet strings, and synthetic skin. Finally, a new generation of artificial life was born.

Dr. Baker firmly believed that love and hope were the greatest inventions of mankind. He programmed the world's most moving stories into the heart of his new creation, transforming her into an android-based robot meant for helping others.

One bright morning, Peace Android Prototype 1 awakened from her bed, and Dr. Baker bestowed upon this adorable mechanical angel the name Angela. Angela followed Dr. Baker's tutelage, studying hard to understand the world, and hoping to meet the inventor's high expectations.

However, their peaceful life together was soon cut short. When the mad scientists of Lab 1718 heard rumors that Dr. Baker had created a living soul outside a human body — which didn't meet their aims of creating flesh weapons — they couldn't bear to believe it. Convinced Baker had betrayed them and kept this knowledge to himself before fleeing, they ordered Dr. Baker's past creations (Alpha and Beta) to hunt down Dr. Baker and his creation.

As their pursuers were about to close in, Dr. Baker placed Angela into a flight capsule, instructing her to seek out his good friend Dr. Rooney (Dr. Rooney).

## Artificial Soul — The Creation of Angela

Dr. Baker was one of the founders of Lab 1718, an institution created with the aim of bringing peace to the world. As his co-founders sank deeper into human weaponization experiments, Dr. Baker left to pursue a new project.

Renouncing human experimentation, he created a new lifeform from a mechanical skeleton, puppet strings, and synthetic skin. He believed love and hope were mankind’s greatest inventions and programmed moving stories into his creation. One morning, Peace Android Prototype 1 awakened and was named Angela.

Angela studied under Dr. Baker, but their life was cut short when Lab 1718’s scientists learned of the living soul created outside a human body. They ordered Dr. Baker's past creations, Alpha and Beta, to hunt Baker and Angela. As pursuers closed in, Dr. Baker placed Angela in a flight capsule to seek out Dr. Rooney. The flight capsule was struck by a terrible thunderstorm and landed in a remote corner of the Land of Dawn. Emerging from the wreckage, Angela began her journey to find Dr. Rooney and save her father.

## Ultimate — Heartguard

- Name: Heartguard  
- Type: Attach / Shield  
- Mana cost: 100 / 150 / 200  
- Cooldown: 70.0s  
- Description: Angela channels for 2 seconds, applying a shield to a target allied hero that lasts 6 seconds and then attaches to that allied hero for 12 seconds regardless of distance. While attached she can cast skills at zero Mana cost. Angela can cast the skill again to cancel channeling or detach by blinking to a designated location away from the allied hero. The attaching state ends early if the allied hero is killed. Angela is untargetable during the attaching state and cannot use equipment or battle spells. The effect persists if the target ally becomes untargetable. Channeling can be interrupted by crowd control or displacement. Cooldown starts after casting.
- Cast range: infinite  
- Base Shield: 1000 / 1300 / 1600  
- Shield Bonus: 200% / 250% / 300%  
- Notes:
  - During attachment Angela can cast the ultimate again to detach and blink away.
  - The attaching state is disabled in certain domains and interactions with other heroes' abilities can prevent or force detachment (specific interactions noted in original data).
- Shield scaling (listed): 1000–2000 base, 200–300% bonus (per level)

# Trivia

- She is the first hero who can be attached to a hero by using her Ultimate; the second is (unspecified).
- She carries a distinct doll for each skin that resembles her appearance on the hero selection screen. During development she used to toss the puppet as her basic attack, but the animation was changed to heart-shaped projectiles. The doll is visible in-game only when she uses her ultimate to possess an ally; the doll appears above the allied hero's model.
- Because some users found her former eyes unsettling, Angela's splash art was modified.
- Her Starlight skin was renamed from China Doll to Shanghai Maiden (Starlight Member).
