## Lore

The City of the Dragon is located to the northeast of Cadia Riverlands, connecting the Frozen Sea and the Sea of Hope. (The City of the Dragon; Cadia Riverlands; Frozen Sea; Sea of Hope)  
It is the only permitted passage for all ships sailing from Northern Vale to Cadia Riverlands and further south. (Northern Vale) The geological advantage allowed this city to develop foreign trade business, making it a prosperous harbor and a booming sea-port city.

But this bursting city never had one day of peace since its establishment. Pirates from the Frozen Sea and the Sea of Hope repeatedly invaded, raiding the harbor and plundering merchant ships. After years of failed resistance, the people built a mighty navy, the Paenlong Fleet, to fight back and protect their homeland and trading routes.

The general of this naval fleet was the legendary hero Yi Sun-shin. Born and raised in The City of the Dragon, Yi Sun-shin witnessed countless tragedies—ships raided and fishermen murdered by pirates—and resolved to become an admiral to protect his homeland. He trained in sailing, archery, and fighting, even venturing into the Dragon's Altar to learn from the elders.

At 18 he joined the naval fleet as a sailor. Over the next decade he fought in numerous battles between the Frozen Sea and the Sea of Hope, showing wisdom and leadership that eventually made him Captain of the entire Paenlong Fleet. Under his command the fleet repeatedly defeated the pirates, driving many back and restoring peace to the city.

As Yi Sun-shin's assaults continued, pirates grew desperate and resorted to trickery. Disguised buccaneers infiltrated the city and spread rumors claiming Yi Sun-shin sought to rule the city and the Sea of Hope. The city rulers believed these rumors, dismissed Yi Sun-shin, and made him a commoner.

The pirates then spread word that they were assembling in the Frozen Sea. Despite Yi Sun-shin's warnings, the ruler sent most of the Paenlong Fleet to that supposed location, leaving only a few ships to defend the harbor. The pirates had in fact set a trap in the northern Frozen Sea and ambushed the fleet. Without Yi Sun-shin to lead them, the navy suffered catastrophic losses; many veterans drowned and most battleships sank. The coalition of pirates then sailed into The City of the Dragon, threatening destruction and causing panic among civilians—some even suggested surrendering property to the pirates to save lives.

In this desperate moment Yi Sun-shin stood up again. He gave a motivational speech to restore hope and morale, mobilized civilians, armed them, and formed a new fleet of fishing boats and a few battleships. When the pirate coalition reached the city, Yi Sun-shin led this new fleet to confront them. Despite being outnumbered and knowing he might sacrifice his life, he charged ahead and inspired his men. Miraculously, the outnumbered defenders won: a coalition of more than 150 pirate battleships was defeated by just twelve vessels and some fishing boats.

The pirates were devastated and unable to threaten The City of the Dragon for decades. Yi Sun-shin rebuilt the Paenlong Fleet and kept watch over the seas.

## Trivia
- Yi Sun-shin (이순신) was a Korean Naval Commander in real life, considered one of the greatest naval commanders and a historical hero, compared with Horatio Nelson. In the game his name is spelled "Yi Sun-shin", but the historically correct Romanization is Yi Sun-sin (이순신). (See: Yi_Sun-sin on Wikipedia; Horatio Nelson on Wikipedia)
- He is the first hero to be based on a historical figure, along with [names omitted in source].
- His armor and weapons are inspired by traditional Joseon-era Korean warriors. (Joseon)
- Currently, Yi Sun-shin is the only hero in the game who can change both the appearance and function of the allied base, and who has skins that change the allied base accordingly.
- The Turtle Ship (Geobukseon, 거북선) was an iron-plated warship used historically by Admiral Yi Sun-sin and plays a key role in his MLBB design. (Turtle ship)
- Yi Sun-shin is the first and, so far, the only Assassin/Marksman hero in the game; other heroes occupy different combined roles. (Assassin; Marksman)
