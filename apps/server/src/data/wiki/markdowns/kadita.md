# Kadita

# Profile

Kadita, Queen of the Southern Seas

- Alias: Ocean Goddess
- Origin: Blue Flame, Vonetis Islands
- Species: Mermaid Goddess (currently); Human (formerly)
- Gender: Female
- Weapons: Sea trident; "Seven legendary spears"
- Abilities: Ocean control; Summons tsunami
- Voice actor (English): Alicia Gerrard

# Bio

The Queen of the Southern Seas, Kadita, was once the princess of a small island nation. Admired by everyone, Kadita was considered by all to be genuine, intelligent, and kind-hearted. However, fate oftentimes finds its way to hit people with strife. Kadita was no different. As the heir apparent to the king, a member within the royal family grew jealous of Kadita and conspired to ruin her — An unknown mage wielding the power of black magic viciously attacked the princess. Kadita came under a powerful spell that robbed her of all her beauty, giving her a strange skin disease in the process.

The people of the kingdom tried everything they could think of to help their beloved princess, but no one could break the spell. Overtime, people's love for Kadita faded, causing her to lose power and confidence. Out of tremendous despair, Kadita threw herself into the stormy Southern Seas. However, Kadita was rescued by the ocean demons. Using the trident of the seas, the ocean demons were able to break Kadita's powerful spell, but due to her skin disease her skin was never able to return to its original complexion. On that day, Kadita become the legendary Queen of the Southern Seas.

Assuming all new powers, Kadita became adept at controlling the ocean waters and the weather. Sailors from all corners of the world came to fear the Southern Seas as they were bound to suffer the wrath of Kadita — No one dared to conquer the Southern Seas any longer. Survivors recant their experiences facing Kadita. Some said she had the appearance of a strange mermaid, while others said she was more beautiful than the princess they knew before. As stories of Kadita began to spread, legends say that she possessed seven legendary spears, each with its own unique abilities, used to punish any invaders who intended to violate the Southern Seas.

# Story

> "Beautiful and powerful mermaid queen"

A princess of a certain island country in the Vonetis Islands. She was victimized by political enemies and lost her beauty and power. When she threw herself into the sea in despair, an Elf in the sea rescued her. After that, she regained her beauty and turned into the form of a mermaid. With the help of the Elves, she became the Queen of the Southern Seas who all sailors fear.

# Abilities

## Thalassophobia

Blessing of the Ocean

Kadita gains the Blessing of the Ocean every 30 seconds. The Blessing is triggered when she takes damage from enemy heroes and lasts 4 seconds, allowing her to recover 65% of the HP lost during the duration afterward.

## Ocean Ode

Description

Kadita summons an ocean wave to carry herself in the target direction and back, dealing damage based on level and magic power to enemies hit and slowing them by 30%. While inside the wave, she gains Control Immunity and 50% Damage Reduction. This skill can pass through obstacles and terrain. If Kadita exits the wave, she does not interrupt the wave's movement.

Notes

- This skill can be cast again to exit the wave early.
- If Kadita is suppressed or uses certain crowd controls while inside the wave, the remaining wave movement persists but she cannot stop at will.
- Formerly named "Ocean Oddity."

Level scaling

- Base Damage: 250 / 290 / 330 / 370 / 410 / 450
- Mana Cost per level: 80 / 90 / 100 / 110 / 120 / 130
- Cooldown per level: 9.0 / 8.5 / 8.0 / 7.5 / 7.0 / 6.5

## Breath of the Ocean

Description

After a short delay, Kadita summons an ocean wave to erupt at the target location, dealing damage based on level and magic power to enemies hit and knocking them airborne for 1.5 seconds. Kadita can cast this skill during Ocean Ode to summon an ocean wave beneath her with a shorter eruption delay.

Level scaling

- Base Damage: 350 / 380 / 410 / 440 / 470 / 550
- Mana Cost per level: 90 / 100 / 110 / 120 / 130 / 140

## Rough Waves

Description

Kadita dives into the ground and sends ocean waves in all directions, each dealing damage based on level and magic power to enemies hit and slowing them by 30%. Enemies hit by multiple waves take increased damage. After a short period, the waves return to Kadita, each dealing return damage based on level and magic power to enemies hit.

While underground, Kadita becomes untargetable and gains 60% Movement Speed.

Level scaling

- Outgoing Base Damage: 270 / 300 / 330
- Return Damage: 180 / 200 / 220
- Mana Cost per level: 200 / 240 / 280
- Cooldown per level: 40.0 / 36.0 / 32.0

Notes

- When Kadita successfully dives underground, this skill removes all existing debuffs, crowd controls, and damage over time effects on Kadita.
- She can freely use equipment and battle spells while underground. Using certain actions will make her unable to move.

# Trivia

- Kadita is inspired by Indonesian folklore, particularly the legend of Nyi Roro Kidul, the Queen of the Southern Sea. In the myth, Nyi Roro Kidul is said to rule the Indian Ocean and has the power to control the waters and command sea creatures.
- Kadita is one of the few heroes in *Mobile Legends* whose powers are purely water-based, and this is reflected both in her abilities and appearance.
- Kadita’s headgear and accessories have sea goddess-inspired designs, resembling coral crowns and sea creature motifs, highlighting her divine and otherworldly status. She has sea shell patterns on her attire, connecting her more to the ocean’s beauty and mystery.
- In Patch 1.4.94, Kadita underwent role adjustments, transitioning from Mage to Mage/Assassin.
- Her Passive "Thalassophobia" is based on a real phobia meaning intense fear of deep bodies of water.
