# Story

> "The Madien of the Glacier who grants sternal remebrane in the legends of the North."

Aurora, the Maiden of the Glacier, commanding the unwavering faith of innumerable mortals in the North, bestows eternity upon the world. In her youth, she endured the torment of her innate prowess for Frost Magic until the disciples of the Light brought her to Queen's Peak, whereupon she received an infusion of light which granted her immortality. Tearfully, her mother bade her farewell as she succumbed to the cold embrace of blizzard. Aurora slumbered for a thousand years atop the peak. Upon her awakening, the truth dawned on her: genuine eternity lay in fleeting emotions, such as her mother's final comforting smile. Thus, Aurora resolved to bestow this eternity upon the world.

It was at that moment that a legend was born in the North. The legend spoke of people close to death who would climb Queen's Peak, seeking the frigid embrace of the goddess there. If granted, all of the one's memories, longings, and emotions would be left to the goddess, who would cherish them until the end of time. And thus, they would live on the eternity.

# Abilities

## Pride of Ice

Upon taking fatal damage, Aurora freezes herself for 1.5 seconds, becoming invincible during this time and gradually recovering 30% Max HP. This effect has a 150 seconds cooldown. Aurora's freeze effects can also affect Turrets.

- Terms: Freeze; Death Immune

Notes:

- When being untargetable, fully benefits from allied abilities like Luo Yi's or Floryn's.
- The availability indicator is visible to everyone.
- This effect triggers before being counted as a death.

## Hailstone Blast

Aurora summons an icy meteorite to strike at the target location, dealing magic damage and slowing targets hit by 40% for 1 second. Afterward, 5 hailstones fall, each dealing scaling magic damage.

- Skill effects: AOE, Slow

- Level scaling:
  - Base Damage: 350 / 390 / 430 / 470 / 510 / 550
  - Combo Damage (per hailstone): 40 / 44 / 48 / 52 / 56 / 60

- Mana cost per level: 55 / 65 / 75 / 85 / 95 / 105
- Cooldown per level: 6.0 / 5.6 / 5.2 / 4.8 / 4.4 / 4.0

Damage type: Magic Damage. Movement Speed reduction: 40% for 1 second.

## Frosty Breeze

Aurora blows Frosty Breeze in a fan-shaped area in the target direction, dealing magic damage to enemies hit, freezing them for 1 second, and creating a frozen area at the far end of the attack that deals additional sustained magic damage to enemies in the area.

- Skill effects: AOE, CC (Freeze)

- Level scaling:
  - Base Damage: 225 / 255 / 285 / 315 / 345 / 375
  - Sustained Damage (frozen area): 225 / 255 / 285 / 315 / 345 / 375

- Mana cost per level: 110 / 110 / 120 / 130 / 140 / 150
- Cooldown: 13.0 seconds

Notes:

- There is a travelling animation delay, which means the freezing effect does not take effect instantly.
- Enemies and turrets in a half cone 2-6 unit away are frozen. Frozen enemies are damaged once more.

Damage type: Magic Damage. Freeze duration: 1 second (base).

## Frigid Glacier

Aurora creates a frost path in the target direction, dealing magic damage to enemies in the path and reducing their Movement Speed by 80% for 1.2 seconds. The frost path gradually becomes glaciers that spread until reaching their maximum size and shattering, dealing area magic damage to all enemies in the area and freezing targets for 1 second. Every 100 Magic Power that Aurora has will increase the freeze duration by 0.2 seconds.

- Skill effects: CC, AoE

- Level scaling:
  - Base Damage: 100 / 150 / 200
  - AOE Damage (shatter): 600 / 900 / 1200

- Mana cost per level: 150 / 175 / 200
- Cooldown per level: 50.0 / 45.0 / 40.0

Notes:

- The area of effect is 7.5 x 7 units.
- Damage type: Magic Damage. Base freeze duration: 1 second; increased by 0.2 seconds per 100 Magic Power.

# Trivia

- The Aurora Borealis, also known as the Northern Lights, is naturally associated with the northern regions of the world, particularly in places like Scandinavia, Canada, and Alaska. This geographic connection reinforces the idea that Aurora’s abilities and powers are tied to the north and to ice and cold, much like the stunning light displays that are characteristic of the Northern Hemisphere.
- Aurora might be based on the titular character of the story The Snow Queen by Hans Christian Andersen.
- The design where her clothing that exposes her right hip has been retained on her official portrait, while her official art and 3D models have covered it in 2018 to comply with app stores' guidelines.
- Although revamped, her old model can be still seen in the in-game emote "Back Off".
- She is the only hero that can freeze turrets.
- Aurora's Passive shares a similar function with another hero's passive in the sense that both heroes have a mechanic that allows them to heal themselves at low HP.
- Aurora's revamped design strongly resembles Elsa from Frozen, capturing the same regal, ice-wielding aesthetic and control over cold elements. Her palace is located at the top of the mountain (Queens Peak) similar to Elsa who also built her palace at the top of the mountain.
- The name **Aurora** is derived from the Latin word "aurora," meaning "dawn" or "sunrise." In mythology, Aurora is the Roman goddess of the dawn, symbolizing the arrival of light and a new day. This connection aligns well with Aurora's role as a powerful mage associated with ice and cold, bringing light and clarity through her abilities, much like the dawn brings light to the world.
- Aurora is a hero deeply tied to ice and cold elements, and the Aurora Borealis, with its mesmerizing ice-like colors (especially greens, purples, and blues), serves as a perfect visual metaphor for her control over frost. Just as the Aurora Borealis lights up the northern skies with its icy glow.
