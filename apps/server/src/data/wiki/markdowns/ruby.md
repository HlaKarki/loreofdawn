# Story

> "A girl who grew up with Roger's help"

The village where Ruby lived was attacked by werewolves. At the time of crisis, Roger appeared to help them repel the werewolves. Roger taught Ruby how to fight and gave her a sickle so that she could protect herself and the village.

# Lore

It could have been just another peaceful night in a remote village, near the outskirts of the Dark Forest, but they were interrupted by the vicious howling of the wolves from afar. In a small cabin, a little girl was kneeling beside her bed, praying. Next to her stood a disproportionately giant scythe. Indeed, she was praying for the return of the legendary Hunter — Roger (Roger). Only he could save the village from being vandalized by the wolves.

Years earlier, Roger had saved her family from a pack of wild wolves they had encountered when wandering on the Land of Dawn (Land of Dawn). His bravery took a huge toll on the wolves. Under request from the village elders, Roger stayed to train the villagers. One day he left the village without notice, and no trace was found.

What happened in fairy tales can seldom be the same in reality. On a silent, moonless night, Pale Tusk, the new Wolf King, caught the village off guard and sneaked in with his kind. In the end, Ruby's grandfather and grandmother both died protecting Ruby, who escaped by hiding beneath their corpses.

From the moment she witnessed her grandparents' death, her character changed completely. Outraged, she picked up the scythe and, to everyone's shock, rushed into the pack of wolves and slaughtered almost all of them. Under the seemingly weak appearance lay an unsettling, irritable soul. Pale Tusk was the only wolf to survive. Staring at the skyline, Ruby was determined to end everyone responsible for those deaths.

Pale Tusk had never been defeated before, but this time the battle was severe. During the fight, Ruby hesitated to strike down the wolf; Pale Tusk seized the chance and clawed her in the left eye, knocking her down. Surprisingly, Pale Tusk did not finish her but fled. Ruby later regained consciousness and discovered she had lost one eye. Now, whenever she hears wolves howl, her remaining eye is engulfed with rage and pain.

# Trivia
- The name Ruby is derived from the ruby gemstone (red), symbolizing passion, strength, and vitality — matching her red-themed design, scythe weapon, and blood-related lifesteal abilities.
- Her appearance might be inspired by Ruby Rose (RWBY) and the fairy-tale character Little Red Riding Hood.
- Her story and title "Little Red Hood" reference Little Red Riding Hood. In some game files she is named X, which translates to "Little Red Riding Hood" in Chinese.
- Her original lore was retconned during a "family friendly update" that removed darker elements (for example, an older story where Ruby ripped a wolf's heart and drank its blood).
- Old full artwork shows her eyepatch on the left eye, but the in-game model places it on the right.
- Her eyes are described as blue, but the model has green eyes.
- Ruby has one of the highest lifesteal rates in the game; her passive (Let's Dance!) enables massive spell vamp from items and skills — with the right build she can heal to full HP in just a few attacks.
- She is also inspired by "Little Red" from Magic Rush (a reference to Little Red Riding Hood).
