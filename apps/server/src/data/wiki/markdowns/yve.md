## Lore

Aurora was the first person to discover Yve. (Aurora)  
This was the first time such a mysterious, cold, and emotionless creature had appeared in the Land of Dawn. (Land of Dawn)

Massive energy fluctuations came from the time rift.  
Aurora thought that Zhask had returned and that she would finally have the chance to avenge her beloved. (Zhask)  
But as it turned out, she saw someone who seemed to be even more powerful.

Yve came down from the sky above the Frozen Ocean. (Frozen Ocean)  
She considered everything and everyone beneath her notice and was only interested in finding Zhask.  
Her instincts told her that her old nemesis had once appeared here.

Yve simply glanced at Aurora with indifference, then turned and left.  
Just as Aurora was hesitating about whether to chase down this mysterious visitor, Yve released an explosion of tremendous energy, closing the time rift temporarily.  
She then turned and left.

This time rift torn open by Yve has existed for thousands of years since the Ancient Ones triggered the great explosion using the Twilight Orb. (Twilight Orb)  
Nobody can get near it, and nobody knows where it leads.  
Only Aurora watches it closely at all times, waiting to take revenge on her nemesis Zhask for her lover Tobias.

Aurora clearly understands what sort of powerful energy it would take to close the time rift: For this reason, she has had a premonition: Perhaps both Zhask and Tobias are coming back.

Yve comes from Mithlora, a mysterious region of the galaxy.  
Her people are few in number but possess immense strength.  
However, they also have an arch-enemy - the Swarm.  
As some of the most proficient hunters in the universe who are born with great power, Yve's race must find and deal with the Swarm wherever they appear.  
Their endless multiplying means destruction and destruction means imbalance.

Zhask and Yve, just like their races, are natural enemies.  
With great force comes great responsibilities.  
As the most powerful dominator amongst the thousands of Swarms, Zhask wishes to lead his Swarm to invade and multiply ceaselessly, and thereby spread it across the galaxy.  
And only by killing Zhask can Yve prove her strength and gain the acknowledgment of her people.

They have been destined enemies since time immemorial.

From as far back as she can remember, Yve has known her mission.  
She is confident in her strength, which she has relied on to eliminate many threats to the plane balance.  
But her frustrations began when she encountered Zhask.  
Even with her great power and having fought Zhask on multiple occasions in different parts of the universe, and even though she was so close to success, Yve still hasn't managed to eliminate Zhask's swarm.

The story that follows is that of a drawn-out chase.  
The devious Zhask skips from one planet to another.  
The creatures on these planets can offer no resistance against Zhask, so the rapidly multiplying Swarm destroys their homes one after another.  
And each time Yve makes it to these devastated planets, her innate sense of responsibility as a Mithloran and her feelings of self-doubt will arouse turmoil in her heart.

Chasing after Zhask has become Yve's obsession.  
She tracked down Zhask to the Land of Dawn. (Land of Dawn)  
She expected to have been too late, and that it too would be nothing but ruins.  
But she was surprised to find that the heroes of the Land of Dawn are strong and courageous, who vow to protect their homelands with their lives.  
This made it difficult for Zhask to achieve his plans and bought her time.

The hunters of Mithlora won't allow every destroyer of harmony in the universe to run rampant.  
Yve will eventually fight against Zhask in a duel here.  
She knows that she and her destined nemesis will fight their final battle here.

# Trivia

- Early concepts of Yve’s design showed her as a celestial mage with star-based powers, but her initial designs were more grounded and simpler, focusing more on magical robes and basic energy effects. However, as her design was refined, she evolved into a more grandiose, space-themed character, with her final design incorporating celestial and cosmic motifs that fit her role as a **Protector of the Stars**.
- **Yve** is certainly one of the most powerful beings in lore, particularly in the context of the **Cosmic World**. As the **Protector of the Stars**, she has immense control over **cosmic energy** and the **manipulation of space**, which positions her as a force to be reckoned with.
- One of Yve's quotes, "great power comes with great responsibility" is a variation of a quote popularized in the Spider-Man comics created by Stan Lee. (great power comes with great responsibility) (Spider-Man)
- Yve's ultimate creates an optical illusion where if you compare her ultimate together, you will notice that one is bigger and other is smaller even though they are just the same size in comparison.
