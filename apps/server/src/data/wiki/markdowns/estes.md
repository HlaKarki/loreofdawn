> "Elf King who rules the Moon Elves"

When the Moon Elves were once again in imminent danger because of the awakening of the Abyss, he woke up early on a night of lightning and thunder.

# Lore

At the habitat of the moon elves, somewhere west of the Moonlake, lies the legendary Emerald Woodland, which was the birthplace of the first generation of elves. Every elven king was born in the Tree of Life at the center of the Emerald Woodland.

The elven king of the current generation is the unique Estes, who was badly hurt during the Second Plane War and had to go into deep sleep in the Emerald Woodland. Moon elves lost the guidance from their king ever since that day.

When the flames of wars between humans and orcs burned on this peaceful woodland, the young Moonlight Archer Miya stood up and gathered the rest of the elves to protect their homeland.

However, the dominator of the dark Abyss thirsted for more believers and the dark force grew stronger in secret. Seduced by the Blood Queen Alice, some young elves fell. The race of moon elves was once again at stake, which outraged the great elven king!

During a night of great thunder and lightning, Estes woke up. He's strict but benevolent, empowered by the sacred moonlight to protect his people and support his allies. With the Code of Moonlight in his hands, he swore to repel every enemy who dares to violate his homeland.

## Skill 1 — Moonlight Immersion

Scales and costs (various entries from templates)
- Mana cost: 110 / 130 / 150 / 170 / 190 / 210
- Cooldown: 11.0 / 10.2 / 9.4 / 8.6 / 7.8 / 7.0
- Enhanced cooldown (when enhanced): see level scaling (e.g., 8.80 / 8.20 / ...)
- Additional HP-based values present in templates (examples): 250–325, 325–400, 500–700, 1170–1470, etc.

Description
- Estes restores HP to a target allied hero immediately and links himself with them for 3 seconds, slowly restoring HP to the target over time. Linking with an allied hero increases Estes' Hybrid Defense by 20–45 and Movement Speed by 15%, and increases the charging speed of Scripture of the Moon Elf. Being too far from the target breaks the link.

Properties / Healing amounts (summarized)
- Basic Healing: 250 / 265 / 280 / 295 / 310 / 325
- Sustained Healing: 325 / 340 / 365 / 370 / 385 / 400
- Basic Healing (Enhanced): 500 / 550 / 600 / 650 / 700
- Sustained Healing (Enhanced): 1170 / 1230 / 1290 / 1350 / 1410 / 1470
- Defense increase when linked: 20 / 25 / 30 / 35 / 40 / 45

Notes
- Default sustained healing restores multiple times (up to 5).
- Casting the enhanced version replaces active Moonlight Immersion.
- An ally already linked by Blessing of Moon Goddess benefits only from basic healing (sustained healing does not stack).
- Enhanced Moonlight Immersion, when activated by Blessing of Moon Goddess, has reduced cooldown and no mana cost, and restores sustained healing more times (up to 15).
- The link is visible to enemies (may reveal allies in bushes).
- Special interactions apply when casting enhanced versions late or when healing Estes himself.

## Ultimate — Blessing of Moon Goddess

Associated skills:
- Enhances Moonlight Immersion (Skill 1) for nearby allied heroes while the buff lasts.

Scales / costs
- Cooldown: 55.0 / 50.0 / 45.0
- Mana cost: 150 / 250 / 350
- HP Regen (continuous on Estes): 1230 / 1350 / 1470 (per template entries)

Description
- Estes casts an enhanced blessing on nearby allied heroes. For the next 8 seconds, Moonlight Immersion is enhanced and Estes continuously recovers HP.
