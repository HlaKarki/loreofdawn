# Story

> "Elf King who rules the Moon Elves"

The Elf King who ruled the Moon Elves fell into a deep sleep after contracting a serious injury of the Second Plane War. When the Moon Elves were once again in imminent danger because of the awakening of the Abyss, he woke up early on a night of lightning and thunder.

# Bio

At the habitat of the moon elves, somewhere west of the Moonlake, lies the legendary Emerald Woodland, which was the birthplace of the first generation of elves. Every elven king was born in the Tree of Life at the center of the Emerald Woodland.

The elven king of the current generation is the unique Estes, who was badly hurt during the second plain war and had to go into deep sleep in the Emerald Woodland. Moon elves lost the guidance from their king ever since that day. When the flames of wars between humans and orcs burned on this peaceful woodland, the young Moonlight Archer, Miya, stood up and gathered the rest of the elves to protect their homeland.

However, the dominator of dark Abyss is thirsty for more believers and the dark force has been growing stronger in secret. Seduced by the Blood Queen, Alice, some young elves have fallen. The race of moon elves was once again at stake which outraged the great elven king! During a night of great thunder and lightning, Estes woke up. He's strict but benevolent, and empowered with the strength by the sacred moonlight to protect his people and to support his allies. With the Code of Moonlight in his hands, he swore to repel every enemy who dares to violate his homeland.

# Abilities

## Scripture of the Moon Elf

Scripture of Moon Elf slowly charges Estes with energy. When the energy reaches 100 stacks, Estes' next Basic Attack will become enhanced, dealing to the target and to nearby enemies, and slowing them by 60% for 1.5 seconds.

- The default recharge speed for Scripture of the Moon Elf is 6.6667 stacks/second; stacks can be 100 in 15 seconds.
- The follow-up damage deals skill damage.
- The icon for Scripture of the Moon Elf is a book (or code), which was the precursor of Estes' current scroll (or scripture).

## Moonlight Immersion

Estes restores for the target hero immediately and links himself with them for 3 seconds, slowly restoring to the target. Linking with an allied hero will also increase Estes' Hybrid Defense by 20–45 and Movement Speed by 15%, as well as the energy charging speed of the Scripture of the Moon Elf. Being too far from the target will break the link. (Estes restores for the target hero immediately, and links himself with them for 3 seconds, slowly restoring to the target for 15 times...)

Properties:

- Basic Healing: 250 / 265 / 280 / 295 / 310 / 325
- Sustained Healing: 325 / 340 / 365 / 370 / 385 / 400
- Basic Healing (Enhanced): 500 / 550 / 600 / 650 / 700
- Sustained Healing (Enhanced): 1170 / 1230 / 1290 / 1350 / 1410 / 1470

- Both Defense Increase: 20 / 25 / 30 / 35 / 40 / 45

Notes:

- Default skill effect:

  - Sustained healing from Moonlight Immersion restores each, up to 5 times.
  - Casting cancels the active Moonlight Immersion and replaced by Enhanced Moonlight Immersion over time.
  - An ally already linked by Blessing of Moon Goddess will still benefit from the heal of Moonlight Immersion but only from Basic Healing, as Sustained Healing cannot be stacked.

- Enhanced skill effect:

  - Upon activation of Blessing of Moon Goddess, enhanced Moonlight Immersion's cooldown will be reduced to 20%, and does not cost mana.
  - Sustained healing from enhanced Moonlight Immersion restores each, up to 15 times.
  - An ally already linked by Blessing of Moon Goddess will still benefit from the heal of enhanced Moonlight Immersion but only from Basic Healing, as Sustained Healing cannot be stacked.
  - If Estes casts the enhanced Moonlight Immersion lately to an ally, it will end prematurely as soon as the 8-second duration ends.

  - This won't work by healing Estes himself, hence he will benefit from the heal even after the duration of Blessing of Moon Goddess ends. The default and enhanced Moonlight Immersion's Sustained Healing can be simultaneously stacked this way, if Estes has enough cooldown reduction to cast the default one.

- The link is visible to enemy, and it might prompt enemy that the ally is in the bush.

Estes casts an enhanced on all nearby allied heroes. Within the next 8 seconds, "Moonlight Immersion" will be enhanced and Estes will continuously recover.
