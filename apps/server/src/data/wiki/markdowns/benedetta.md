Story

Vengeance on the Monsters

> "The bio of Benedetta, the 100th hero who is known as "Shadow Ranger" is unveiled for the first time. Under the Rantha Mountains, her ""Alecto"" symbolizes both honor and vengeance."  
> — Benedetta bio publicity blurb

After the Abyss returned, the land became plagued and the entire southern part of the Land of Dawn turned into a barren place. Though the high Rantha Mountains block the corruption of the Abyss, Mother Nature in the southern realm continued to wither away. This once prosperous land is now nothing but a silent place filled with death, and people call it the “Despair Place”.

The only passage that connects the Moniyan Empire is broken, separating the trapped refugees and the exiled wanderers from the rest of the world. They now live in fear and despair, haunted by monsters, yet still struggling to survive.

For these people, the name of Benedetta brings hope and a sense of security. This courageous, persevering female ranger leads a team and fights in the land south to the Rantha Mountains, protecting the scattered settlements set up by the last survivors in the Despair Place.

This is the fourth year since Benedetta and her team started their campaign in the Rantha region. Whenever she draws “Alecto”, the rampaging monsters scatter in fear of this long sharpened sword infused with unstoppable power. People call her the Ranger Leader who walks in the shadow, Protector of the Despair Place. She has earned everyone’s admiration and respect in this desolate wasteland.

However, the distant Moniyan Empire leaves a deep scar in Benedetta’s heart, and the wound may never heal. Alucard isn’t the only person whose life was changed by the war years ago; Benedetta is also one of the victims. But Benedetta’s family is unlike that of Alucard. Her father wasn't an officer, he was just an ordinary soldier.

Unfortunately, the second division was convicted of “rebellion” after they were defeated in battle, and so no honor or glory was given to the soldiers who gave their lives for the country. As an officer's child, Alucard was sent to the Monastery of Light and grew up with the blessing of Light. Benedetta, who didn’t receive such privilege as the child of a low-rank soldier, had to struggle to make a living without her parents by her side.

Ever since she was little, Benedetta wished to become a Warrior of Light, to take back the glory that rightfully belonged to her father, and to avenge her father's death by killing those monsters. To this end she trained constantly, treated herself as a warrior, and inherited the long sword left by her father, preparing for battles to come.

However, the passionate Benedetta was rejected when she signed up for the Empire Army trial because those who ran the Knight's Order ordered that no descendants of a “rebel” could ever join the Empire Army, so they would never seek the truth like Alucard did.

Disappointed and enraged by the empire, Benedetta took up her long sword and left the Moniyan Empire, heading for the Barren Lands to annihilate the monsters and take vengeance herself.

Even though she was mentally prepared, Benedetta was shocked by the corruption and desolation the first time she saw the Barren Lands. What surprised her more was that people lived near the foothills of the Rantha Mountains in such a harsh environment. Life there was hard, but those people were optimistic and hard-working; they improved the soil, raised the next generation, and defeated the monsters.

At first Benedetta was not interested in these people. She aimlessly wandered the Barren Lands and gave her long sword a new name, Alecto. She unleashed her anger on the monsters and slaughtered them all. But over time she realized the land was vast and the power of the Abyss and its monsters far more formidable than she had expected. She could not save the Despair Place alone.

One day Benedetta ran across a ranger squad in the middle of a fight against monsters. The odds were against them, but they fought bravely and drove back the attackers. During the campaign, Benedetta and the rangers established friendship and trust through their joint effort. These people demonstrated fortitude and a strong will to survive, which touched Benedetta’s soul and made her believe that it was not weapons that would triumph over the Abyss but the everlasting hope in their hearts.

Benedetta chose to stay and built a ranger squad whose members shared the same vision but had different skills. They helped defend the homeland. One by one the monsters across the Rantha Mountains’ southern foothills were defeated by Benedetta and her comrades. Benedetta would always leave a mark on the battlefield after she struck down enemies, reminding them she had not forgotten. More settlements sprang up and human civilization could develop thanks to their protection.

They’ve been through hell and made sacrifices, but nothing could shake their will. Under Benedetta’s command they believed that so long as hope existed, flowers would one day bloom in the Barren Lands.

Meanwhile, Benedetta had another ambition: to eliminate the monsters and recover her father’s glory, proving to the foolish rulers of the Moniyan Empire that honor is hard-earned, not given. This is not just a battle between her and the Abyss; it is also a war between her and the Empire.

Abilities

Passive
- Names/keywords: Swordout state; Sword Intent; Swordout Slash
- Other tags: Elapse Daytime; Thunder Belt; Blade of the Heptaseas; Flicker; Lethal Ignition; King's Calling
- Passive description (Elapsed Daytime / basic attack hold mechanic):
  - When holding down the Basic Attack button, Benedetta enters Swordout state and charges Sword Intent. If released after Sword Intent is fully charged, Benedetta performs Swordout Slash in the facing direction and dashes forward, dealing damage to enemies on the path (counts as Skill Damage; reduced vs Minions/Creeps). Benedetta gains Sword Intent by dealing damage with Basic Attacks and skills.
  - Key notes:
    - Dash distance ~3.8 units. Damage is treated as basic attack damage (triggers attack effects) but cannot critically strike.
    - Holding and canceling Swordout State counts as a skill cast.
    - Sword Intent gains ~66.667 points per second, full at 1.5s (100). Overcharge possible for 3s. Taking damage/using skills grants additional intent; intent decays over time if not replenished. Death resets Sword Intent.
    - While charging, Benedetta can move, and the charging sound/animation is visible/audible to all (current amount visible only to Benedetta).

Skill 1 — Phantom Slash
- Type/effects: AOE, Mobility
- Damage scaling:
  - Shadow Damage: 200 / 240 / 280 / 320 / 360 / 400
  - Slash Damage: 75 / 100 / 125 / 150 / 175 / 200
- Cooldown: 10.0 → 6.5 (levels)
- Mana cost: 55 → 85 (levels)
- Description: Benedetta withdraws swiftly and leaves a shadow in front. After a short delay, the shadow slashes in a fan, dealing damage and slowing enemies by 60% for 0.5s. Benedetta dashes forward to slash, dealing damage; if the target is hit by the shadow, Benedetta's slash deals increased damage.
- Notes:
  - Benedetta dashes backward ~1.9 units then forward ~3.8 units (triggers passive twice).
  - The forward slash can be interrupted by CC or obstacles; obstruction of the backward dash affects the forward distance.

Skill 2 — An Eye for An Eye
- Type/effects: Mobility, Buff (Control Immunity & defense)
- Base damage: 300 / 330 / 360 / 390 / 420 / 450
- Cooldown: 15.0 → 12.0 (levels)
- Mana cost: 100 → 125 (levels)
- Description: Benedetta raises her weapon to defend, gaining Control Immunity and blocking damage for 0.8s. Afterward she stabs forward dealing damage. If she fends off any damage while defending, she gains full Sword Intent; if she fends off control effects, the stab stuns targets for 1.5s.
- Notes:
  - Stun applies to all targets hit in the area. Some control-like effects (slow/root/disarm/pulls) do not trigger the stun. This ability does not remove ongoing control effects or evade suppression.
  - Sword Intent is only filled if damage taken while defending is > 0.
  - Voice line may sometimes play incorrectly when defend fails.

Ultimate — Alecto: Final Blow
- Type/effects: Burst, Mobility; Benedetta is untargetable during dash
- Base damage: 1560 / 1820 / 2080 (+ extra physical scaling)
- Cooldown: 45 / 40 / 35
- Mana cost: 110 / 135 / 160
- Description: Benedetta clenches Alecto, dashes forward (untargetable during dash), and after a short delay performs a slash along the path, detonating Sword Intent. For 2.5s the Sword Intent deals periodic damage along the path and slows enemies by 50%.
- Additional:
  - Spellvamp ratio: 25%
  - Range ≈ 7.5 units. Benedetta is untargetable for ~0.33s. The skill deals damage at ~0.2s intervals (up to 13 instances).
  - Sword Intent is fully charged after successful cast. Ability does not go on cooldown if interrupted during cast delay. Equipment and battle spell effects queue and apply after arrival.

Trivia
- Benedetta is the 100th hero in the game by chronological release order on the Original Server. Brody is the 100th hero released on the Advanced Server.
- The name Benedetta originates from Italian and derives from the Latin benedictus (meaning blessed). It is the feminine form of Benedetto, a cognate of Benedict.
- Benedetta’s weapon is called Alecto, one of the Furies (vengeful spirits) from Greek mythology.
- Alecto: Final Blow is one of the longest dashes in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, covering a huge distance while dealing continuous damage.
- Benedetta is the only hero with all blink skills; even her passive is a blink dash. Most heroes stand still while attacking, but Benedetta can move while charging her passive attack, making her combat style very fluid.
- She was named "Bella" during early development.
- Benedetta is one of the few heroes in MLBB with a special attack icon due to her Sword Intent charging mechanic; the symbol resembles a sword’s arc indicating the wide-area slash.
- The default attack icon starts blue (shadow/assassin theme) and turns red as Sword Intent reaches full charge.
