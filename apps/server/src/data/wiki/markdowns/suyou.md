# Bio

Thunderous drums and gongs, blazing lights. Suyou lay leisurely on the ledge of a tall building, with his hands behind his head. His lips curled into a smile as he took in the hustle and bustle of Zhu'an at night. A prosperous city reflected in his eyes. Underneath the eaves, laughter could be heard as children wearing traditional Nuo masks ran by.

> "Immortal Nuo, bless the land, Immortal Nuo, our most auspicious guardian!"

Little did the carefree children know that listening right above them was Suyou, the last successor of the Exorcists of Nuo, with the sacred Nuo mask and blade, looking down with a smile. Suyou hopped to his feet like a spring and enjoyed a long stretch. He just had just woken up from a dream about the past.

More than a decade ago, the sky darkened overhead and the wind howled as wails could be heard from all directions. The lingering obsessions and evil thoughts of the dead had gathered in this place, creating a phenomenon known as echoes of malice, and threatening to consume the entire village. A young Suyou could only cower in fear among the rubble and wait helplessly for death to claim him. Suddenly, a massive figure broke through the clouds with a trident in hand. His fierce stature stood between the village and the echoes of malice like an angry mountain. In the next moment, a nimble figure wielding a sacred blade darted past Suyou, towards the evil scourge. It was an Exorcist of Nuo. With one cut, he split the sky. With another, he swept away the scourge like freshly fallen dust. There was no need for a third. That day, the fearsome power of the Immortal Nuo was etched deep into a boy's heart. Inspired by the miracle he had witnessed, Suyou decided to travel the land, helping people in any way he could, like his savior.

Ten years passed, and Suyou found himself following some leads to a small town near Zhu'an in the Cadia Riverlands. The town was shrouded in echoes so thick that one wondered how anything could even survive in such a place. But the townsfolk lived on in bliss, ignorant of the danger they were in. Strange phenomena would form due to the concentration of echoes, only for the townsfolk to look away without a care in the world. Suyou soon found the reason. An old exorcist was living in the town and had been maintaining some kind of fragile balance. The very one who had saved him all those years ago. Even as the echoes were on the verge of devouring the town, the old exorcist continued to lie and reassure the townsfolk that all was well. The young Suyou could not comprehend how his hero could have fallen into such a state. Suyou firmly believed that deceiving these people could only lead to disaster. Why was the old exorcist gambling with their lives? The slightest misstep would surely doom them all.

Suyou was forced to conclude that the exorcist was a swindler, so he set out to warn the town of the danger they were in. However, this led to outright panic and only hastened the growth of the echoes. Only then did Suyou realize that the world was not filled with valiant heroes, but ordinary people with their countless worries and fears. And the oversaturation of negative emotions is what creates the echoes of malice. After learning of the impending disaster, their fear would only feed the echoes until they devoured everything. For the townsfolk, their faith and hope were the silver linings that kept them safe in this dire situation. The power of Nuo does not come from the heavens, but from the strength of human faith. The old exorcist had always been fighting the fear and uncertainty in people's hearts. But the human heart is easily led astray, and lost without guidance. The ones to guide them were the Exorcists of Nuo, using faith to manifest the Immortal Nuo.

To make up for his mistake, Suyou helped the old exorcist prepare for the Nuo ritual. The exorcist had spent most of his life fighting echoes, and the countless injuries he had suffered in those battles left him unable to perform the ritual any longer. As if by fate, he passed the sacred Nuo mask and blade to the young Suyou to carry on in his place. Suyou put on the mask and held the blade tightly. For the first time, Suyou stood on stage as an Exorcist of Nuo. He performed the ritual dance and sang the ancient chants to invoke the Immortal Nuo.

The sky suddenly darkened, as if Suyou had returned to the past. Unaware of the impending doom, the masses looked upon Suyou with awe and expectation. Only then was Suyou able to understand... What is Nuo? Nuo is faith. People's hearts are like candle flames, swaying in whichever direction the wind blows. And at this moment, as an Exorcist of Nuo, Suyou was that wind. When the people see your resolution, they will believe. When the people see your smile, they will revel. When the people see your fear, they will cower. And when the people see your hesitation, they will doubt. Their faith comes from you, and the faith you evoke from them—that is the power of Nuo!

As darkness fell, the faith of the townsfolk below the stage became like flames and illuminated Suyou's performance.

> "Do not fear evil."

The people chanted in their hearts, "The Immortal Nuo will protect us from evil!" over and over. Thus, the Immortal Nuo gained the power to dispel evil.

> "Do not fear disaster."

The people chanted in their hearts, "The Immortal Nuo will save us from disaster!" over and over. Thus, the Immortal Nuo gained the power to avert disaster. Little by little, something emerged from the people. It gathered around the Nuo mask, the sacred blade, and the statue of the Immortal Nuo. The hopes of the people, the power of the human heart, and the sparks of their faith, came together and manifested into three-headed and six-armed Immortal Nuo. Nuo is faith, and the Immortal Nuo is the miracle born from it. The sparks of their faith, gathered to give form to the body. The thoughts of the people, honed as an edge to cut despair.

Suyou wielded the sacred blade and guided the people. He invoked the Immortal Nuo and cut down the echoes of malice. He split the sky, just as his predecessor did. He saw the faith of the people and heard their prayers. At this very moment, he had grasped the true meaning of being an Exorcist of Nuo. Suyou carried the power of the immortal deity within his body to guide the people toward enlightenment. That was his duty.

After passing on the sacred relics and knowledge of the ritual to Suyou, the old exorcist left without a trace. The world always assumes that those who take on great responsibilities must carry heavy burdens and suffer hardships. But Suyou was unfettered, wandering from place to place, appreciating the sights and sounds. Sometimes he would perform the Nuo ritual to reaffirm people's faith, and other times he would draw his sacred blade to cut down evil manifestations. He has seen what the power of faith and the human heart are capable of, and he returns it in kind. His love for the world and faith in others allow him to protect every ray of hope. Just like his name, Suyou, in the Cadian tongue, Exorcist of evil and a wanderer in this world.

# Story

> "An exorcist from Zhu'an who has inherited the power of the Immortal Nuo."

As the last descendant of the Exorcists of Nuo, Suyou carried the power of the immortal deity within his body, using it to guide the people toward enlightenment. He walked the mortal plane like a wandering swordsman, charming and uninhibited, but never forgetting that the power of Nuo comes from the faith of the people. And so, he went about purging evil from the hearts of men and safeguarding their hopes for the future.

# Abilities

## Transient Immortal

- any skill will put Suyou in his Mortal form, grant him 40 Movement Speed and decreasing the interval between Basic Attacks.
- any skill will put Suyou in his Immortal form, increasing the damage of Basic Attacks to 125% and granting him 20% Physical Damage Reduction.
- In this form, for every 20 extra Suyou has, he gains 1% Physical Damage Reduction (up to 40%).

Notes:
- Suyou's basic attack rate in Mortal Form is approximately 1.25 times his total attack speed.
- Loses Immortal Form upon death.
- The Physical Damage Reduction and attack speed bonus are not displayed.

## Blade Surge

Suyou throws his weapon in the target direction, dealing damage based on skill level and physical attack to enemies in its path. Suyou then blinks to the weapon's location, catches it, and performs a slash in the opposite direction, dealing damage based on skill level and physical attack. Suyou channels the Immortal's power and charges in the target direction, dealing damage based on skill level and physical attack to enemies along the path. When hitting an enemy hero, he stops and stuns enemies in a rectangular area in front for 0.6 seconds. The charge increase distance increases with the hold time. Suyou can be controlled while charging, but the skill cannot be interrupted.

Damage values:
- Damage by Tap: 250 / 270 / 290 / 310 / 330 (physical)
- Slash Damage: 375 / 405 / 435 / 465 / 495 (physical)
- Damage by Hold: 250 / 270 / 290 / 310 / 330 (physical)

Notes:
- Tap-cast.
- The dash has a range of 3.8 unit.
- After dashing, Suyou's facing direction is set to the opposite of his casting's direction.
- The slash has a shorter range of 2 unit.
- Hold-cast: despite the description, the mobility from holding the ability is a dash that can go through obstacles. This dash first slightly knockbacks the first enemies hit, then stuns them.
- When Suyou is rooted while holding, the ability is cancelled.
- Hold Blade Surge for up to approximately 0.6 seconds to tap-cast, then keep holding for approximately 0.6–3 seconds to hold-cast, which has a total holding time of approximately 2.4 seconds.
- Suyou can fully charge his hold-casting skill for up to approximately 0.5 seconds to maximize range and up to approximately 0.8 seconds to maximize the in-game camera zooming out.

## Soul Sever

Suyou performs a sweeping attack with his weapon, dealing damage based on skill level and physical attack to enemies in a fan-shaped area. Suyou channels the Immortal's power to perform 3 cleaves in a fan-shaped area. The first two strikes deal damage based on skill level and physical attack each, while the final strike deals enhanced damage based on skill level and physical attack and recovers health for himself.

Damage and healing values:
- Damage by Tap: 300 / 348 / 396 / 444 / 492 (physical)
- Damage by Hold: 250 / 290 / 330 / 370 / 410 (physical)
- Enhanced Damage (final strike): 400 / 464 / 528 / 592 / 656 (physical)
- HP Regen (heal amount): 150 / 170 / 190 / 210 / 230
- Minion Damage Ratio: 60% / 70% / 80% / 90% / 100%

Notes:
- Tap-cast: hold Soul Sever for up to approximately 0.25 seconds to tap-cast, then keep holding for approximately 0.25–1 second to hold-cast, which has a total holding time of approximately 0.75 seconds.
- Each hold-casting deals an attack for every approximately 0.25 seconds.
- The hold-casting strikes have the same range as the attack range in Immortal Form.

## Evil Queller

Suyou glides backward and swings his weapon in the target direction, dealing damage based on skill level and physical attack to enemies in a rectangular area and on his path backward, and reducing their Movement Speed by 30% for 1.5 seconds. Suyou uses the Immortal's power to shoot a deadly arrow in the target direction, dealing damage based on skill level and physical attack to the target hit. The range increases with the hold time. Suyou can be controlled while charging, but the skill cannot be interrupted.

Damage values:
- Damage by Tap: 420 / 460 / 500 / 540 / 580 (physical)
- Damage by Hold: 580 / 635 / 690 / 745 / 800 (physical)

Notes:
- When being controlled, the ability keeps charging.
- The arrow range begins at 3.8 unit, and up to 8 unit.
- Disabled by rooted. When rooted while holding, the ability is not canceled, but the release action is prohibited.
- This skill is treated as an Ultimate with 5 upgradable levels and can be learned at level 1.
- Hold Evil Queller for up to approximately 0.6 seconds to tap-cast, then keep holding for approximately 0.6–3 seconds to hold-cast, which has a total holding time of approximately 2.4 seconds.
- Suyou can fully charge his hold-casting skill for up to approximately 0.75 seconds to maximize range and up to approximately 0.8 seconds to maximize the in-game camera zooming out.

# Trivia

- Suyou is a Chinese name meaning to have.

His concept nuo is based on Chinese popular exorcism religion. Nuo means oath or expelling the evil spirits.

His game sound effects are based on real life samples.

His 3 weapons halberd, dual blades and war bow are kept hidden as jade pieces on his waist. He wears a different mask for different weapon.

His 3 different masks can be seen in this intro and in game when he uses his different hold skills.

Technically, Suyou has 6 skills.

Suyou is the second hero to have a level 5 Ultimate skill, the first one being Julian.

Suyou is the first hero to have the tap-casting and hold-casting mechanic.

His favorite drink is tea.
