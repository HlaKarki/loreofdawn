# Story

The Celestial Palace - Legend has it that the city floating above the skies of the Land of Dawn was once home to the Gods. It is also rumored that this mysterious place holds the key to ruling the world. Eons ago, the ruler of the Celestial Palace utilized the technological secret of the ancients to create **Uranus**, then commanded it to guard the city and await the emergence of a new ruler.

When the scholars of the Land of Dawn found and opened the gates to the Celestial Palace, the battle between the Dawn Zenith and the Dark Abyss quickly spread throughout the realm, disrupting the solitude of the ancient city. The two forces sought to occupy the Celestial Palace as a symbol of victory, their heroes unleashing the full extent of their power and finally reaching an impasse. The massive influx of energy generated throughout the incessant battles eventually awakened the city's defense system, converting the released energy into pure lightning, fed into **Uranus** and reawakened after dormancy within the depths of the Celestial Palace.

> *"Leave now, intruders!"*

His goal—to purge these savage intruders that had disturbed the tranquility of the Celestial Palace.

# Abilities

## Radiance

- Healing Effect: Uranus absorbs the energy generated by incoming attacks to strengthen himself, regenerating a certain amount of HP every 0.8s. Each stack lasts 10s.
- Stack Mechanics: Each time Uranus is hit he gains a stack. When at max stacks (20), Uranus regenerates 48-224 HP (scales with level).

Description: Uranus regenerates HP over time by absorbing energy from incoming attacks, building stacks that increase his regeneration. At full stacks he regenerates a significant amount of HP based on level.

## Ionic Edge

- Magic Skill, AoE, Slow
- Mana Cost: 60 / 65 / 70 / 75 / 80 / 85
- Base Damage by Level: 155 / 175 / 195 / 215 / 235 / 255

Description: Uranus releases two energy blades that orbit around him, dealing scaling magic damage to enemy targets and slowing them by 30% for 2s. Each time this skill deals damage to a target, they will be marked with a stack for 6s (max of 1 stack per target per cast). Each stack increases the damage of this skill by 40%, up to a maximum increase of 320%. Each energy blade will only deal damage to the same target once.

## Transcendent Ward

- Blink, Shield, Movement Speed
- Mana Cost: 80 / 90 / 100 / 110 / 120 / 130
- Path Damage by Level: 80 / 104 / 128 / 152 / 176 / 200
- Area Damage by Level: 160 / 208 / 256 / 304 / 352 / 400
- Energy Shield by Level: 300 / 390 / 480 / 570 / 660 / 750
- Explosion AOE Damage by Level: 300 / 390 / 480 / 570 / 660 / 750

Description: Uranus charges to the target location, dealing scaling magic damage to enemies along the way, and dealing area scaling magic damage to enemies at the target location while slowing them by 25%. Uranus also generates an energy shield for 4s. The shield explodes at the end of its duration or when destroyed, dealing scaling magic damage to enemies nearby.

## Consecration

- BUFF, Speed Up, Healing Effect, Slow Immunity
- Cooldown: 42.0 / 39.0 / 36.0 seconds
- Mana Cost: 100 / 125 / 150

Description: Uranus unleashes the energy stored within his body, removing slow effects on himself, recovering 200-400 HP, and gaining 60% Movement Speed for 8s (decays over time). Uranus directly gains 5-15 stacks of Radiance, increasing shields received and HP regeneration by 20% for 8s.

# Trivia

- His name, "Uranus," comes from the primordial Greek god of the sky, who was the father of the Titans. Uranus is also the name of the seventh planet in the solar system, which aligns with the hero’s cosmic theme.
- His name was nearly "Ouranos," the original Greek spelling of the god Uranus. The developers decided on "Uranus" to match the planet's name and make it easier to recognize globally.
- Uranus is one of the oldest being in lore of the game.
- He is the guardian of the Celestial Palace which is one of the playable maps in game before it was replaced by Sanctum Island.
- Unlike most tanks that rely on shields or lifesteal, Uranus regenerates HP rapidly over time, making him one of the hardest heroes to take down. His Passive (Radiance) allows him to stack HP regeneration whenever he takes damage, making him excel in prolonged fights.
