## Story

After the death of her father, she was adopted by House Vance and became Harley's sister (Harley). Lesley regarded her younger brother as everything to her, and kept protecting Harley secretly.

---

## Lore

Lesley grew up in the Vance household, where her father presided over the noble family's security. During an unexpected attack by a rival family, Lesley's father was killed. As the assailants approached the Vance household, Lesley took up her father's black long rifle and fulfilled her late father's mission. Tears rolled down Lesley's cheeks as she fired shot after shot, surprisingly, each one taking down an assassin. The final tear came as the final shot took down the last of the rival family's men.

Lesley was adopted by the Vance family, with the patriarch of the noble house even putting their sole heir, Harley, under her tutelage. Harley was a natural troublemaker who used his talents in magic to unintentionally cause trouble for the Vance household. Only Lesley could keep Harley under control. Harley's troublemaking streak was the last part of warmth within Lesley's conscience.

After hearing about how Harley had travelled to the dangerous Land of Dawn after defeating the Boss of Dark Magic, Lesley followed him, along with her trusty heirloom rifle. After she found Harley through a long arduous journey, she discovered that the once immature young boy had changed through the numerous life experiences he had gone through. She decided it would be best to have her adopted little brother train alone, while she would silently protect him from within the shadows. From that day on, all the monsters that were inflicted with Harley's magic would also be struck with a black bullet that would impeccably pierce through their heart. The stories of the magical youngster who was followed by a mysterious black sniper soon spread across the Land of Dawn like wildfire.

## Abilities

### Passive — Lethal Shot
- If Lesley doesn't take damage for 5 seconds, her next Basic Attack gains extra range, +50% Critical Chance, and deals 20% extra damage against minions.
- Each point of fixed Physical Penetration Lesley acquires is converted into 0.5% Critical Damage, but her base Critical Damage is reduced to 140%.
- Lesley's Basic Attack restores 5 energy; her (unspecified) [skill] doubles the amount of energy restored.
- The cooldown of the passive resets each time Lesley casts a skill.
- Notes:
  - Instead, inflicts physical damage on turrets.
  - Instead of reducing Lesley's base critical damage to 140%, Lesley's total critical damage is reduced by 60%.
  - The additional critical chance applies only to the enhanced basic attack.

### Skill 1 — Master of Camouflage
- Skill type/effects: Conceal; BUFF
- Cooldown: 5.0 / 4.4 / 3.8 / 3.2 / 2.6 / 2.0
- Energy cost: 30
- Description: Lesley enters Camouflage, gaining double Energy Regen, +40% Movement Speed, and +85–135 Physical ATK (Attack Bonus). The state lasts 3 seconds or until Lesley takes or deals damage. Enemies can detect Lesley's position through distortions of their surroundings.
- Attack Bonus by level: 85 / 95 / 105 / 115 / 125 / 135
- Notes:
  - (Some effect) starts after Master of Camouflage ends.

### Skill 2 — Tactical Grenade
- Skill type/effects: CC; Blink (Physical Skill + Blink)
- Base damage: 150 / 180 / 210 / 240 / 270 / 300
- Scaling: +50 (and physical damage scaling)
- Cooldown: 10.0 / 9.4 / 8.8 / 8.2 / 7.6 / 7.0
- Energy cost: 40
- Description: Lesley throws a tactical grenade in the target direction while slightly jumping back, dealing damage to enemies in a cone and knocking them back. Lesley can cast this skill during Ultimate Snipe to cancel the state and immediately fire a Fatal Bullet at the target (only if unfired Fatal Bullets remain).

### Ultimate — Ultimate Snipe
- Name: Ultimate Snipe
- Skill type/effects: Burst; Physical Skill
- Cooldown: 40.0
- Energy cost: 0
- Description: Lesley gains vision of nearby enemies and can lock onto a target enemy hero and fires 4 Lethal Bullets in succession, each dealing base damage and restoring 10 energy on hit. Bullets can be blocked by other enemy heroes. If interrupted, each remaining Bullet reduces the skill cooldown by 10%. Lesley's Crit Chance is increased by 5%–15%.
- Crit Chance by level: 5% / 10% / 15%
- Base Damage by level: 250 / 300 / 350
- Notes:
  - Lesley can aim for up to 8 seconds.
  - Firing Lethal Bullets lasts up to 3.6 seconds (or earlier if canceled); roughly one bullet every 0.9 seconds.
  - Cooldown starts upon cast.
  - Lesley will recoil after firing a Lethal Bullet; the recoil is not a blink.
  - Camera height increases during the skill.
  - Lesley stops firing if the locked enemy goes too far.
  - You can hold the skill to choose a target before firing or cancel it (it will still go on cooldown). If canceled without shooting any bullets, it is not revealed to the enemy.
  - Aim range: 11 units; max range: 15 units. Indicator is shown on the minimap when holding the skill; the skill does not activate if no enemy hero is within range. All enemies within range are revealed.

## Trivia

- The name **Lesley** has Scottish origins and means **"garden of holly"** or **"fortress of holly"**. It comes from the Scottish surname *Leslie*. Her name might symbolize her **precision, stealth, and resilience**, much like a fortress that stands strong or a sharpshooter hiding in the shadows.
- Unlike other Marksmen, Lesley doesn’t rely on attack speed; she focuses on high burst damage from critical hits.
- Lesley is one of the few marksmen in the game who use Energy instead of Mana.
- Her passive, **Lethal Shot**, allows her to deal 100% critical damage after gaining extra energy, making her a sniper-type hero rather than a typical ADC (Attack Damage Carry).
- Both Lesley and Vance (the royal house) mean “Marsh, Mud, and Fen” in Old English, a name used as a masculine given name and surname in American usage.
- Lesley's quote:
> "Enemy in range, and you are too."
- Lesley is the first and, so far, the only Marksman/Assassin hero in the game, whereas another hero is an Assassin/Marksman.
- In her early designs, she had shorter hair and a more military-style outfit. Developers later gave her a long coat and a more assassin-like appearance, making her more stylish and deadly.
