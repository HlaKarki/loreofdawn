## Story

> "Demon hunter who detest the darkness"

Alucard's father was a comrade-in-arms of Tigreal who disappeared in a battle with the Demons. Therefore, he hated darkness, joined the Monastery of Light, and swore to destroy all Demons. But later he learned the hidden secrets of this battle, so he left the Monastery of Light in anger and fought the darkness in his own way.

## Lore

The last great war is a glorious memory for the Moniyans. The powerful Light's Order, together with the Imperial Border Guards, wiped out the demonic strongholds at the Moniyan and the Barren Lands pass, driving the fiends back to the hinterlands of the Forsaken Wastes. But for young Alucard, the war was nothing but a terrible memory of pain and misery.

His father's second regiment suffered heavy losses due to a rash lone advance. Alucard's father disappeared in the battle and was later declared dead by the Light's Order.

Because they had disobeyed orders, the second regiment was labeled "disobedient" rather than honored for their sacrifice. This deeply wounded Alucard, who had always regarded his father as a hero. Facing shame and ridicule, the flames of revenge burned within him; he resolved to restore his father's honor and eliminate all demons in the Land of Dawn. He left home and journeyed to the ancient Monastery of Light, where, as the orphaned son of a dead soldier, he was accepted.

Over the years Alucard trained in a range of combat skills and became the most promising student. Tigreal, Commander of the Light's Order and a veteran who had once fought with Alucard's father, frequently visited the monastery, teaching Alucard advanced combat skills and recounting his father's deeds. Alucard respected Tigreal greatly and regarded him like an elder brother, though Tigreal grew silent whenever his father's final battle was mentioned. Alucard doubted that his father had died and believed he might still be alive somewhere.

At eighteen, Alucard completed his studies and, encouraged by Tigreal, became the monastery's Arbiter of Light. Ordered to hunt demons and heretics, he took his father's old sword (given to him by Tigreal) and set off. Fueled by hatred of demons and his father's name, he became a legendary demon hunter.

During a demon-hunting operation on the Barren Lands border, Alucard was wounded by a magic blade and sustained an unhealable wound. Darkness poured into his body through the wound and began transforming his arm. To prevent the mutation from spreading, an old smith (a survivor of that fated battle and an ally rescued by Alucard's father) forged him a pair of gauntlets. The smith revealed that the second regiment had been trapped due to the greed and impulsiveness of high-ranked knights; in desperation, Alucard's father covered the retreat of the main force led by Tigreal and was swarmed by demons and disappeared. The man the smith had rescued was the only survivor saved by Alucard's father.

Alucard returned to the capital and questioned Tigreal about the battle and why the truth had been hidden. Tigreal confessed guilt for abandoning his allies and that he had been forced into silence by his family. He offered to make amends, even with his life, but Alucard found himself conflicted — unable to kill Tigreal yet unable to forgive him.

Seeking to restore his father's honor, Alucard demanded truth from the Empire. The powerful knights who had fought in that battle were now influential figures and refused his request, driving him out of the capital. The Monastery of Light condemned Alucard's unnatural right hand and treated him as a heretic. Disillusioned with both the Empire and the church, Alucard betrayed the monastery and chose to fight on his own terms.

He wandered the Land of Dawn, annihilating demons while searching for clues to his father's whereabouts. His demonic right hand granted him great power but was unstable, hidden beneath heavy armor. After many epic battles, Alucard became a legendary figure in Moniyan: where demons ran rampant, the demon hunter with the dark right hand would descend and leave none alive.

## Trivia
- "Alucard" spells backward as "Dracula."
- The name Alucard is popularized by the Castlevania video game franchise (character: Alucard).
- When Mobile Legends: Bang Bang launched as "Mobile Legends: 5v5 MOBA" in mid-2016, Alucard was originally named Dante, inspired by the Devil May Cry character and visually based on Nero from Devil May Cry.
- His Legend skin "Obsidian Blade" uses skill icons identical to his old pre-revamp icons.
- The "Child of the Fall" skin is based on Sephiroth; the sword for that skin is based on the Buster Sword from Final Fantasy.
- His Lightborn skin uses armor and a greatsword similar to Saber (Artoria Pendragon) from Fate/Stay Night.
- In earlier artwork, Alucard wielded dual swords instead of a single greatsword; this was later changed to make his weapon style more distinct from other sword-wielding heroes.
- His demonic hand is visible in some Media (MLA) and earlier models/portraits.
- In older versions, his lifesteal could amplify beyond 100%, making him extremely hard to kill in fights.
