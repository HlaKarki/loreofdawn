## Story

> "Mysterious astrologer"

Born into a family of Agelta astrologers, she is the greatest astrologer in the Land of Dawn. Through the treasure Starmoon Casket, she learned that the tyrant Khufra was about to wake up. The re-sealing of Khufra became her new mission as an astrologer.

## Lore

The astrologers, who have constantly been observing astrospace, possess occult abilities to explain and predict everything in the world, including both their processes and results. To become an astrologer, candidates must pass rigorous assessments that test both willpower and astrological abilities. Going down the generations, the elders finally select Esmeralda, a young girl living in the Western Desert, to be a new successor in the Land of Dawn.

After becoming the successor, Esmeralda guards the Starmoon Casket that has been passed down for generations. Hoping to open it and gain the mysterious power of ancient times, she sets off for the Minos Labyrinth to uncover its secrets. On her way, the ground suddenly shakes, the desert breaks apart, and a black sandstorm appears. The Starmoon Casket opens and a stream of mysterious power fills Esmeralda. Protected by this power, she is unharmed and experiences flashes of old memories.

She senses the Casket calling to her, warning that Khufra, the ruthless tyrant of the Western Desert long ago, has returned. Guided by the Starmoon Casket, Esmeralda turns toward the deep desert, where an ancient city stands. After the Casket opens, two streams of power emanate and center on Esmeralda — Frostmoon (magic) and Stardust (great power). Impressed by the Casket's might, Esmeralda recalls memories from the first generation of astrologers. Approaching the ancient city, she vaguely senses an unknown love story between Khufra and some astrologers; seeing the end of that love, she feels sad and sorry. But her mission remains: to seal Khufra and restore peace to the Western Desert.

## Trivia

- The name Esmeralda is of Spanish and Portuguese origin, meaning **"emerald"** (the precious green gemstone). It comes from the Spanish word *esmeralda*, derived from the Greek *smaragdos* (σμάραγδος), meaning *green gem*.
- Her name complements her Starmoon Casket passive, representing the combination of the stars (Stardust) and the moon (Frostmoon).
- Esmeralda's name and character design are somewhat inspired by the character with the same name in the 1996 Disney film The Hunchback of Notre Dame.
- Esmeralda is one of the heroes whose active skills can be cast while moving using the joystick.
- Esmeralda is the only hero who can grant a shield to enemies.
- She wears golden jewelry (bangles, earrings, a headpiece) resembling traditional Middle Eastern fashion, particularly Egyptian styles.
- Her skin name "Cleopatra" is based on Queen Cleopatra of Egypt.
- Her "Poison Vine" skin is based on Poison Ivy from the DC Universe.
- Alongside Khufra, she has voice lines that can only be activated by interacting with that specific opposing hero (Khufra).
- Currently, Esmeralda and another unspecified hero are the only heroes classified as both Mage and Tank.
