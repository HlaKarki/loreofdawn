# Bio

Legends from the Land of Dawn say that the Western Desert's Minoan Kingdom possesses a mysterious labyrinth. In the maze is a statue of giant minotaur, which the people consider to be the mecca of the Minoan Kingdom. Some say that there is a group of mercenaries who guard the entire maze. The Kingdom produced a lot of precious jade, which brought the Minoan people great wealth. Very quickly, the entire kingdom was lost in the intoxicating allure of gems. Even many mercenaries, tasked with guarding the sacred maze become corrupted, abandoning their duty. Only one stayed―Aldous, who had never forgotten his duty and guard the Minoan Labyrinth.

Until one day, a god came from the heavens to punish these lands. The powers of light and dark decimated the entire Minoan Kingdom, which even Aldous could not escape. The two streams dark and light energy surged into his body. Aldous thought he was about to die. However, the two streams of energy collided, causing great changes to his body. His skin grew long-black scales that could resist most attacks and monstrously large arms that could easily strike fear into people's hearts.

When Aldous awoke from his daze, he found his people's kingdom all gone and the mysterious maze was now sealed by some unknown force. The remaining minotaurs decided that Aldous would stay to defend the maze, while the others travel across the Land of Dawn to find answers. News of a mysterious force sealing the Minoan Maze spread quickly to the Abyssal Kingdom. To gather this strange energy, Selena decided to bring her Abyssal Demons with her to the entrance of the Minoan Maze―A huge battle was about to begin.

# Abilities

## Contract: Transform

After every 2 enhanced basic attacks, Aldous gains a shield for 3 seconds on his next basic attack. Shield value: 500 plus 3 times the current stack count. This effect can only be triggered once every 5 seconds. Shield values example progression: 503 / 506 / 509 / … / 2000.

Terms: Physical Basic Attack, Shield.

## Contract: Soul Steal

Aldous unleashes his inner energy to enhance his next Basic Attack to kill enemies and absorb their soul. The enhanced Basic Attack deals base damage and extra physical damage based on the number of Soul Steal stacks. Extra physical damage formula: 5 times the number of stacks.

Base Damage by skill level: 200 / 250 / 300 / 350 / 400 / 450. Total physical attack scaling: 100%.

If this attack kills large neutral monsters (for example Lord or Turtle), an enemy hero, an enemy turret, a Siege Minion, or other non-hero unit, Aldous will gain stacks of Soul Steal: 12 stacks for large neutral monsters and heroes in early levels as listed, up to a maximum of 650 stacks. The enhanced Basic Attack deals 300% extra Base Damage to minions and creeps. Aldous gains additional stacks when nearby enemy Siege Minions or other non-hero units die.

Notes:
- The enhanced basic attack lasts for 5 seconds or until it is consumed.
- Cooldown starts upon casting the skill, but the skill cannot be used again until the enhanced basic attack is consumed or expires.
- Aldous charges towards a target when attacking using his enhanced basic attack.
- The enhanced basic attack's base damage and extra damage do not critically strike.
- Gain stacks when dying enemies have available.

## Contract: Explosion

Aldous assumes a defensive stance, gaining 30% Damage Reduction and 20% extra Movement Speed for up to 2 seconds or until manual cancellation. He then deals damage to nearby enemies and stuns them for 0.5 to 1 second(s), where the stun duration scales with the defensive stance duration.

Base damage calculation examples:
- Base damage = [80 + (20 × skill level)] + [(40 + 10 × skill level) × skill duration].
- Physical bonus = 20% + (10% × skill duration).
- Stun duration = 0.5 s + (0.25 s × skill duration), where skill duration ranges from 0 s to 2 s.

Damage ranges by level: Minimum Damage 100 / 120 / 140 / 160 / 180 / 200. Maximum Damage 200 / 240 / 280 / 320 / 360 / 400. Total physical attack scaling values as listed: 20% for one example and 40% for another scale entry.

Terms: Dodge, Damage Reduction, Stun.

Cooldowns by level example: 12.0 / 11.5 / 11.0 / 10.5 / 10.0 / 9.5. Mana costs by level example: 80 / 90 / 100 / 110 / 120 / 130.

## Contract: Chase Fate

Aldous gains vision of all enemy heroes for 5 seconds. During this time, if Aldous uses this skill again, he can dash toward a chosen enemy hero. When Aldous hits that enemy hero, he will deal damage and knock back the target for 1 second. Aldous can interrupt certain enemy mobility or channeling effects on the target during the dash. Using the skill a second time cancels the dash and causes Aldous to blink to the designated direction instead.

Base Damage on hit: 250 / 325 / 400 (by Ultimate level). The ultimate deals physical damage based in part on Aldous' maximum HP in certain calculations as noted in scaling.

Notes:
- With perfect timing, it is possible to interrupt Aldous from dashing using some enemies' skills or spells.
- The dash can only be interrupted by suppression effects.
- Aldous can freely use items and battle spells while channeling or dashing.
- Inside certain zones that restrict target selection, he cannot lock on enemies outside the zone and ongoing travel is cancelled. Likewise, he cannot select targets inside such a domain, and does not pursue further when arriving at the target's last known location.

# Trivia

- The name Aldous is a boy's name of English origin meaning "old." Aldous has long been associated with British writer Huxley, author of Brave New World.
- His voice line "Abandon hope, all ye who enter here." is a direct reference to Dante's quote in Inferno.
- While still in development Aldous' first given name and hero title was Armstrong, Gravekeeper. It was later changed to Aulrad, Contractor. However, in Patch 1.2.78, his name was changed again to Aldous.
- Aldous "Red Mantle" refers to his former appearance without his damaged body.
- Aldous is the sole survivor of the divine punishment that devastated the Agelta Drylands. He was blessed by the Twilight Orb, which not only saved his life but also granted him immense power, granting him strength nearly equal to that of Gods or Supreme beings.
- Aldous is one of only two heroes capable of harnessing the power of the Twilight Orb; the identity of the other hero is not specified here.
