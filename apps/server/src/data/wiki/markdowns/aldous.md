# Story

> "Guardian of the Minoan Maze"

A guard of the Minos Labyrinth, who kept his pledge even after the kingdoms fall. He has blocked off intruders who covered the kingdoms heritage for a century with an unusually huge arm.

# Bio

Legends from the Land of Dawn say that the Western Desert's Minoan Kingdom possesses a mysterious labyrinth. In the maze is a statue of giant minotaur, which the people consider to be the mecca of the Minoan Kingdom. Some say that there is a group of mercenaries who guard the entire maze.

The Kingdom produced a lot of precious jade, which brought the Minoan people great wealth. Very quickly, the entire kingdom was lost in the intoxicating allure of gems. Even many mercenaries, tasked with guarding the sacred maze become corrupted, abandoning their duty. Only one stayed―Aldous, who had never forgotten his duty and guard the Minoan Labyrinth.

Until one day, a god came from the heavens to punish these lands. The powers of light and dark decimated the entire Minoan Kingdom, which even Aldous could not escape. The two streams dark and light energy surged into his body. Aldous thought he was about to die. However, the two streams of energy collided, causing great changes to his body. His skin grew long-black scales that could resist most attacks and monstrously large arms that could easily strike fear into people's hearts.

When Aldous awoke from his daze, he found his people's kingdom all gone and the mysterious maze was now sealed by some unknown force. The remaining minotaurs decided that Aldous would stay to defend the maze, while the others travel across the Land of Dawn to find answers. News of a mysterious force sealing the Minoan Maze spread quickly to the Abyssal Kingdom. To gather this strange energy, Selena decided to bring her Abyssal Demons with her to the entrance of the Minoan Maze―A huge battle was about to begin.

# Abilities

## Contract: Transform

After every 2 stacks, Aldous gains a 500 (+3 × stack count) shield for 3 seconds on his next Basic Attack. This effect can only be triggered once every 5 seconds.

- Shield values scale from 503 / 506 / 509 / … / up to 2000 (per original progression).
- Triggers on Physical Basic Attack.
- Grants a temporary Shield that absorbs incoming damage for the duration.

## Contract: Soul Steal

Aldous unleashes his inner energy to enhance his next Basic Attack. The enhanced Basic Attack deals base physical damage plus extra physical damage equal to 5 × number of Soul Steal stacks.

- Base Damage: 200 / 250 / 300 / 350 / 400 / 450 (scales with skill level).
- Extra Damage from stacks: 5 × number of Soul Steal stacks (scales up; shown as 5 / 10 / 15 / … / up to 2500 in original progression).
- Mana Cost: 60 / 65 / 70 / 75 / 80 / 85 (varies by level).
- Effect: If this attack kills a Lord/Turtle/enemy hero/enemy turret/Siege Minion/other non-hero unit, Aldous gains stacks of Soul Steal (up to 650 stacks). The enhanced Basic Attack deals 300% extra Base Damage to Minions and Creeps. Aldous gains extra stacks when nearby enemy Siege Minions or other non-hero units die.

Notes:
- The enhanced basic attack lasts for 5 seconds or until it is consumed.
- Cooldown starts upon casting the skill, but the skill cannot be used again until the enhanced basic attack is consumed or expired.
- Aldous charges towards a target when attacking using his enhanced basic attack.
- The enhanced basic attack's base damage and extra damage do not critically strike.

## Contract: Explosion

Aldous assumes a defensive stance, gaining 30% Damage Reduction and 20% extra Movement Speed for up to 2 seconds or until manual cancellation. He then deals area physical damage to nearby enemies and stuns them for 0.5–1.0 second(s) (stun duration scales with defensive stance duration).

Scaling and formulas (as provided):

- Base damage formula: base damage = [80 + (20 × skill level)] + [(40 + 10 × skill level) × skill duration].
- Physical bonus: 20% + (10% × skill duration).
- Stun duration: 0.5 s + (0.25 s × skill duration).
- Skill duration bounds: 0 s ≤ skill duration ≤ 2 s.

Damage ranges shown in original data:
- Minimum Damage: 100 / 120 / 140 / 160 / 180 / 200.
- Maximum Damage: 200 / 240 / 280 / 320 / 360 / 400.
- Scaling: includes additional physical attack ratios (examples in original: total-pa 20, pd 40 depending on component).

- Cooldown: 12.0 / 11.5 / 11.0 / 10.5 / 10.0 / 9.5 (varies by level).
- Mana Cost: 80 / 90 / 100 / 110 / 120 / 130 (varies by level).

Effects:
- Grants Damage Reduction while in stance.
- Deals area damage and stuns enemies when the stance ends or the skill is activated.

## Contract: Chase Fate

Aldous gains vision of all enemy heroes for 5 seconds. During this time, if Aldous uses this skill again, he dashes toward a chosen enemy hero. When Aldous hits that enemy hero, he deals physical damage and knocks back the target for 1 second. Aldous can interrupt certain abilities during the dash. Using the skill a second time cancels the dash and blinks Aldous toward the designated direction instead.

- Base Damage (on hit): 250 / 325 / 400 (per original progression).
- Cooldown: 60.0 / 55.0 / 50.0 (varies by level).
- Mana Cost: 150 / 200 / 250 (varies by level).

Notes (from original):
- With perfect timing, it is possible to interrupt Aldous from dashing using some abilities.
- The dash can only be interrupted by being suppressed.
- He can freely use equipment and battle spells during the dash.
- Inside certain domains, he cannot lock on enemies outside and ongoing travel is cancelled; likewise, he cannot select targets inside some domains, and does not pursue further when arriving in the target's last location.

# Trivia

- The name Aldous is a boy's name of English origin meaning "old." Aldous has long been associated with British writer Huxley, author of Brave New World.
- His voice line "Abandon hope, all ye who enter here." is a direct reference to Dante's quote in Inferno.
- While still in development Aldous' first given name and hero title was Armstrong, Gravekeeper. It was later changed to Aulrad, Contractor. However, in Patch 1.2.78, his name was changed again to Aldous.
- Aldous "Red Mantle" refers to his former appearance without his damaged body.
- Aldous is the sole survivor of the divine punishment that devastated the Agelta Drylands. He was blessed by the Twilight Orb, which not only saved his life but also granted him immense power, granting him strength nearly equal to that of Gods or Supreme beings.
- Aldous is one of only two heroes capable of harnessing the power of the Twilight Orb, the other being is.
