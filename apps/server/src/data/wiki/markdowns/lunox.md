# Bio

As the final battle started, the Twillight Orb devoured the energies within the Mage and opened, from which stepped out a girl, who was surrounded by the power of Chaos and Order. Her eyes are deep as the ancient lake.

The mysterious atmosphere she created then put all of the heroes into dreamland. The power of Chaos and Order have been struggling against each other since the ancient time, and the struggle had driven the world into desperation. Lunox, the mysterious maiden, is the one who survived the desperation. With her unique talent, she can project her dream into reality and change it.

One day, a sound whispered to her and told her to balance the two powers with her dreamland ability. But the cost would be greater than ever. For the world she loves, she accepted this fate, channeled the two powers into dreamland and became one of the Twilight Orb, at the cost of falling into an endless sleep. But the sound also told Lunox that one day, she will wake up to proceed with her fate: to find other Twilight Orbs that are forgotten by history.

# Story

> "Miraculous creatures born in Twilight Orb"

Lunox was born with magical powers, and her dreamworld reflects and changes the real world. With this power, she uses her dreamworld to temporarily maintain the balance of energy between order and chaos. But as the power of order and the power of chaos rushed into her dreams, Lunox fell into a long sleep and turned into one of the "Twilight Orbs."

# Abilities

## Dreamland Twist

Lunox is twisted by the powers of Chaos and Order.

- Description: When Lunox uses Power of Order, she gains 0.5% Spell Vamp for every 1% Magic Penetration. When Lunox uses Power of Chaos, she gains 0.5% Magic Penetration for every 1% of Spell Vamp.
- Notes:
  - Despite the description, percentage magic penetration with 1 Order stack is indeed subtracted and converted into spell vamp and vice versa with 1 Chaos stack.
  - Only percentage Magic Penetration is converted. Net value Magic Penetration from items or other flat sources is not converted.
  - Upon death, Lunox loses the current stack of Order and Chaos.

## Starlight Pulse

Lunox summons a rain of starlight upon nearby enemies, dealing scaling magic damage based on level and stats. The starlight then returns to Lunox, each returning to restore HP and recovering based on level and spell vamp. The HP restored is doubled when hitting enemy heroes. Grants one stack of Order after use.

- Level Scaling:
  - Base Damage: 200 / 230 / 260 / 290 / 320 / 350
  - HP Restoration (per return): 75 / 80 / 85 / 90 / 95 / 100
- Spellvamp Ratio: 50%
- Additional Details:
  - Both skills 1 and 2 share the same cooldown and skill level. Levelling up one ability levels another.
  - Has the range of 4.5 unit.
  - If a target dies before a starlight reaches them, the starlight will not return, resulting in no heal.
  - Lunox fully benefits from returning starlights even when untargetable.
  - Fully bounces to unsighted, concealed or invisible enemies, but not to untargetable enemies.

## Chaos Assault

Lunox unleashes Chaos Energy at an enemy, dealing magic damage based on base value plus a percentage of the target's Max HP (scaling with level). Grants one stack of Chaos after use.

- Level Scaling:
  - Base Damage: 250 / 270 / 290 / 310 / 330 / 350
  - Extra Percentage Damage (to heroes): 2.5% / 3% / 3.5% / 4% / 4.5% / 5% of target's Max HP
  - Damage to Creeps: 1.5% / 1.8% / 2.1% / 2.4% / 2.7% / 3% of target's Max HP
- Additional Details:
  - Both skills 1 and 2 share the same cooldown and skill level.
  - Has the range of 5 unit, similar to her attack range. The animation appears slightly further than the effective range.
  - Fully affects unsighted enemies, such as in a bush, invisible, or concealed.
  - When affected by certain states, this ability may be unaffected by cooldown reduction, cooldown speed, nor cost mana (per specific in-game interactions).

## Cosmic Fission

When Lunox uses the power of Order and Chaos, she deals scaling magic damage to targets along the way and slows them by 40% for 2 seconds.

- Level Scaling:
  - Base Damage: 300 / 340 / 380 / 420 / 460 / 500
- Spellvamp Ratio: 50%
- Additional Details:
  - Has the range of 5 unit, beginning slightly behind Lunox.
  - The ability first displays the animation and sound, then the cooldown, damage and effect occur gradually in a rectangular area. In some cases when being controlled during the cast delay, only the animation is displayed.

## Order & Chaos (Power of Order: Brilliance / Power of Chaos: Darkening)

Lunox is twisted by the powers of Chaos and Order. She can choose between the Power of Order (Brilliance) or the Power of Chaos (Darkening). Each variant has its own effect and separate cooldown.

- General Ultimate:
  - Cooldown (base): 30.0 / 26.0 / 22.0 seconds
  - Mana Cost (base): 120 / 160 / 200

- Power of Order: Brilliance
  - Description: Lunox extends the Light Barrier, becomes invincible for 2.5 seconds, and gains 30% extra Movement Speed while dealing magic damage every 0.5 seconds to nearby enemies.
  - Base Damage per pulse: 100 / 120 / 140
  - Radius: 3.2 unit
  - Notes:
    - Brilliance and Darkening have separate cooldown timers.
    - Items and abilities like certain actives can be used during the process.
    - Does not remove ongoing control effects or impending buffs, and does not pass through obstacles.
    - Disappears from the minimap for the duration.
    - Is still attackable by creeps, minions, Lord and Turtle.
    - The remaining invincible time is visible to both allies and enemies.
    - The first pulse takes effect right upon cast, resulting in a total of 6 damage pulses.

- Power of Chaos: Darkening
  - Description: Lunox blinks in the target direction, dealing scaling magic damage to nearby enemies and slowing them by 30% for 4 seconds. For the next 4 seconds, Lunox greatly reduces the cooldown of her Skill 3 (this cooldown reduction is not affected by cooldown reduction stats).
  - Base Damage: 250 / 350 / 450
  - Dash Distance: 3.5 unit; damage and slow effect radius: 3.2 unit
  - Notes:
    - Brilliance and Darkening have separate cooldown timers.
    - The cast range becomes visible for the duration.
    - The cooldown of Skill 3 is set to 0.3 seconds for the duration and is not affected by cooldown reduction and cooldown speed.
    - Skill 3 does not cost mana for the duration.
    - The duration begins as soon as the ability is cast; if the landing is interrupted, the damage and slow effect do not take effect.
    - The remaining duration is visible to both allies and enemies.
    - If the ultimate is learned while Lunox has neither Order nor Chaos stack, a default variant is selected by the game.

# Trivia

- Lunox' name is a portmanteau of two Latin words lumos and nox, respectively "light" and "dark" in English.
- Her old data title is named "the Enlightened One", in reference to the name of Buddha.
- She is the main protagonist of the Determination event along with Belerick.
- Lunox's Light and Darkness concept was reused for Luo Yi.
- Her complementary "Bloody Mary" skin is a reference to Mary I of England; the name also refers to a popular drink.
- Lunox's light powers come from her mother, and her dark powers come from her father.
- Lunox is Unique God's gift to the world (Land of Dawn).
