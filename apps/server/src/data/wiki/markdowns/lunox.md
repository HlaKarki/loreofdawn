# Story

> "Miraculous creatures born in Twilight Orb"

Lunox was born with magical powers, and her dreamworld reflects and changes the real world. With this power, she uses her dreamworld to temporarily maintain the balance of energy between order and chaos. But as the power of order and the power of chaos rushed into her dreams, Lunox fell into a long sleep and turned into one of the "Twilight Orbs."

# Bio

As the final battle started, the Twillight Orb devoured the energies within the Mage and opened, from which stepped out a girl, who was surrounded by the power of Chaos and Order. Her eyes are deep as the ancient lake.

The mysterious atmosphere she created then put all of the heroes into dreamland. The power of Chaos and Order have been struggling against each other since the ancient time, and the struggle had driven the world into desperation. Lunox, the mysterious maiden, is the one who survived the desperation. With her unique talent, she can project her dream into reality and change it.

One day, a sound whispered to her and told her to balance the two powers with her dreamland ability. But the cost would be hunger than ever. For the world she loves, she accepted this fate, channeled the two powers into dreamland and became one of the Twilight Orb, at the cost of falling into an endless sleep. But the sound also told Lunox that one day, she will wake up to proceed with her fate: to find other Twilight Orbs that are forgotten by history.

# Abilities

## Dreamland Twist

- **Description:** Lunox is twisted by the powers of Chaos and Order. When Lunox uses Power of Chaos, she gains 0.5% Spell Vamp for every 1% Magic Penetration. When Lunox uses Power of Order, she gains 0.5% Magic Penetration for every 1% of Spell Vamp.

- **Mechanics / Notes:**
  - Only percentage Magic Penetration is converted. Net value Magic Penetration (such as from items that grant flat penetration) is not converted.
  - Upon death, Lunox loses the current stack of Order and Chaos.
  - Despite the description, percentage magic penetration with 1 Order stack is subtracted and converted into spell vamp and vice versa with 1 Chaos stack.

## Starlight Pulse

- **Description:** Lunox summons a rain of starlight upon nearby enemies, dealing scaling magic damage. The starlight then returns to Lunox, each restoring HP (amount scales with level). The HP restored is doubled when hitting enemy heroes. Grants one stack of Power of Order after use.

- **Mana cost:** 75 / 80 / 85 / 90 / 95 / 100 (per level)
- **Base damage:** 200 / 230 / 260 / 290 / 320 / 350
- **HP restored:** 75 / 80 / 85 / 90 / 95 / 100
- **Spell vamp ratio:** 50%
- **Range:** 4.5 unit

- **Notes:**
  - Both skills 1 and 2 share the same cooldown and skill level. Leveling up one ability levels the other.
  - If a target dies before a starlight returns, that starlight will not return and no heal occurs.
  - Lunox fully benefits from returning starlights even when untargetable.
  - Fully bounces to unsighted, concealed, or invisible enemies, but not to untargetable enemies.

## Chaos Assault

- **Description:** Lunox unleashes Chaos Energy at an enemy, dealing Magic Damage (base damage scales with level and magic power). Against creeps it deals damage based on a percentage of their Max HP. Grants one stack of Power of Chaos after use.

- **Mana cost:** 30 / 35 / 40 / 45 / 50 / 55 (per level)
- **Base damage:** 250 / 270 / 290 / 310 / 330 / 350
- **Extra percentage damage to heroes (Max HP):** 2.5% / 3% / 3.5% / 4% / 4.5% / 5%
- **Damage to creeps (Max HP):** 1.5% / 1.8% / 2.1% / 2.4% / 2.7% / 3%
- **Range:** 5 unit (similar to her attack range)

- **Notes:**
  - Both skills 1 and 2 share the same cooldown and skill level.
  - The animation appears slightly further than the effective range.
  - Fully affects unsighted enemies (such as in a bush), invisible, or concealed enemies.
  - When affected by certain effects (unspecified in source), this ability may not be affected by cooldown reduction, cooldown speed, nor cost any mana.

## Cosmic Fission

- **Description:** When Lunox uses the power of Order and Chaos, she deals scaling magic damage to targets along the way and slows them by 40% for 2 seconds.

- **Base damage:** 300 / 340 / 380 / 420 / 460 / 500
- **Spell vamp ratio:** 50%
- **Cooldown:** 10.0 / 9.5 / 9.0 / 8.5 / 8.0 / 7.5 (per level)
- **Mana cost:** 70 / 80 / 90 / 100 / 110 / 120 (per level)
- **Range:** 5 unit (begins slightly behind Lunox)

- **Notes:**
  - The ability first displays the animation and sound, then the cooldown, damage and effect gradually in a rectangular area. If Lunox is controlled during the cast delay, sometimes only the animation is displayed.
  - Has a slow effect of 40% for 2 seconds on hit.

## Order & Chaos

- **Description (general):** Lunox is twisted by the powers of Chaos and Order. She can access two alternate ultimate modes: Power of Order (Brilliance) and Power of Chaos (Darkening). Each mode has separate cooldown timers.

- **Cooldown:** 30.0 / 26.0 / 22.0 (per level)
- **Mana cost:** 120 / 160 / 200 (per level)

- **Power of Order — Brilliance:**
  - **Effect:** Lunox extends the Light Barrier, becomes invincible for 2.5 seconds, gains 30% extra Movement Speed, and deals damage every 0.5 seconds to nearby enemies.
  - **Base damage per pulse:** 100 / 120 / 140
  - **Radius:** 3.2 unit
  - **Spell vamp ratio:** 50%
  - **Notes:**
    - Brilliance and Darkening have separate cooldown timers.
    - Can use certain items during the process.
    - Does not remove ongoing control effects or impending buffs; does not pass through obstacles.
    - Disappears from the minimap for the duration but is still attackable by creeps, Lord and Turtle.
    - The remaining invincible time is visible to both allies and enemies.
    - The first pulse takes effect right upon cast, resulting in a total of 6 damage pulses.
    - Known bug: certain battlefield effects may not display correctly when graphic settings are low.

- **Power of Chaos — Darkening:**
  - **Effect:** Lunox blinks in the target direction, dealing damage to nearby enemies and slowing them by 30% for 4 seconds. For the next 4 seconds, Lunox greatly reduces the cooldown of Skill 3 (this reduced cooldown period is unaffected by cooldown reduction and cooldown speed).
  - **Base damage:** 250 / 350 / 450
  - **Dash distance:** 3.5 unit; slow and damage effect radius: 3.2 unit
  - **Notes:**
    - The cast range becomes visible for the duration.
    - During the 4s enhancement, Skill 3's cooldown is set to 0.3s and is not affected by cooldown reduction or cooldown speed, and Skill 3 does not cost mana for the duration.
    - The duration begins as soon as the ability is cast; if the landing is interrupted, the damage and slow effect do not take effect.
    - The remaining duration is visible to both allies and enemies.
    - If Lunox has neither Order nor Chaos stack when learning the ultimate, the ultimate is selected by default.

# Trivia

- - Lunox' name is a portmanteau of two Latin words *lumos* and *nox*, respectively "light" and "dark" in English.
- Her old hero title is named "the Enlightened One", in reference to the name of Buddha.
- She is the main protagonist of the **Determination** event along with Belerick.
- Lunox's Light and Darkness concept was reused for Luo Yi.
- Her complementary "Bloody Mary" skin is a reference to Mary I of England; it is also the name of a popular drink.
- Lunox's light powers come from her mother, and her dark powers come from her father.
- Lunox is Unique God's gift to the world (Land of Dawn).
