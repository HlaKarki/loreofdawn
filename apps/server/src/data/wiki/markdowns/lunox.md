# Bio

As the final battle started, the Twillight Orb devoured the energies within the Mage and opened, from which stepped out a girl, who was surrounded by the power of Chaos and Order. Her eyes are deep as the ancient lake.

The mysterious atmosphere she created then put all of the heroes into dreamland. The power of Chaos and Order have been struggling against each other since the ancient time, and the struggle had driven the world into desperation. Lunox, the mysterious maiden, is the one who survived the desperation. With her unique talent, she can project her dream into reality and change it.

One day, a sound whispered to her and told her to balance the two powers with her dreamland ability. But the cost would be hunger than ever. For the world she loves, she accepted this fate, channeled the two powers into dreamland and became one of the Twilight Orb, at the cost of falling into an endless sleep. But the sound also told Lunox that one day, she will wake up to proceed with her fate: to find other Twilight Orbs that are forgotten by history.

# Story

> "Miraculous creatures born in Twilight Orb"

Lunox was born with magical powers, and her dreamworld reflects and changes the real world. With this power, she uses her dreamworld to temporarily maintain the balance of energy between order and chaos. But as the power of order and the power of chaos rushed into her dreams, Lunox fell into a long sleep and turned into one of the "Twilight Orbs."

# Abilities

## Dreamland Twist

Lunox is twisted by the powers of Chaos and Order.

- When Lunox uses Power of Chaos, she gains 0.5% Spell Vamp for every 1% Magic Penetration.
- When Lunox uses Power of Order, she gains 0.5% Magic Penetration for every 1% of Spell Vamp.

Terms:

- Spell Vamp
- Magic Penetration

Notes:

- Despite the description, percentage magic penetration with 1 Order stack is indeed subtracted and converted into spell vamp and vice versa with 1 Chaos stack.
- Only percentage Magic Penetration is converted. Net value Magic Penetration, such as from items or buffs, is not converted.
- Upon death, Lunox loses the current stack of Order and Chaos.

## Starlight Pulse

Lunox summons a rain of starlight upon nearby enemies, dealing magic damage based on level and stats. The starlight then returns to Lunox, each restoring health and recovering based on level and stats. The HP restored is doubled when hitting enemy heroes. Grants one stack of Power of Order after use.

Level scaling:

- Base Damage: 200 / 230 / 260 / 290 / 320 / 350
- HP Regen: 75 / 80 / 85 / 90 / 95 / 100

Other:

- Mana cost: 75 / 80 / 85 / 90 / 95 / 100
- Spellvamp ratio: 50%
- Skill type: Magic Damage Skill
- Effect: Healing Effect

Notes:

- Both skills 1 and 2 share the same cooldown and skill level. Levelling up one ability levels another.
- Has the range of 4.5 unit.
- If a target dies before a starlight reaches, the starlight will not return, resulting in no heal.
- Lunox fully benefits from returning starlights even when untargetable.
- Fully bounces to unsighted, concealed or invisible enemies, but not to untargetable enemies.

## Chaos Assault

Lunox unleashes Chaos Energy at an enemy, dealing magic damage based on base value and a percentage of the target's Max HP. Against creeps, the percentage is lower as specified below. Grants one stack of Power of Chaos after use.

Level scaling:

- Base Damage: 250 / 270 / 290 / 310 / 330 / 350
- Extra Percentage Damage: 2.5% / 3% / 3.5% / 4% / 4.5% / 5% (of Max HP)
- Damage to Creeps: 1.5% / 1.8% / 2.1% / 2.4% / 2.7% / 3% (of Max HP)

Other:

- Mana cost: 30 / 35 / 40 / 45 / 50 / 55
- Skill type: Magic Damage Skill
- Term: Max HP

Notes:

- Both skills 1 and 2 share the same cooldown and skill level.
- Has the range of 5 unit, similar to her attack range. The animation is slightly further than the effective range.
- Fully affects unsighted enemies, such as in a bush, invisible or concealed.
- When affected by Darkening, this ability is neither affected by cooldown reduction, cooldown speed nor costs any mana.

## Cosmic Fission

When Lunox uses the power of Order and Chaos, she will deal magic damage to the targets along the way and slow them by 40% for 2 seconds.

Level scaling:

- Base Damage: 300 / 340 / 380 / 420 / 460 / 500

Other:

- Mana cost: 70 / 80 / 90 / 100 / 110 / 120
- Cooldown by level: 10.0 / 9.5 / 9.0 / 8.5 / 8.0 / 7.5
- Skill type: Magic Damage Skill
- Effect: Movement Speed (Slow)
- Spellvamp ratio: 50%

Notes:

- Has the range of 5 unit, beginning slightly behind the caster.
- The ability first displays the animation and sound, then the cooldown, damage and effect gradually in a rectangular area, which in some cases when being controlled during the cast delay, only the animation is displayed.

## Order & Chaos

Lunox is twisted by the powers of Chaos and Order. When Lunox uses Order, she gains Brilliance and becomes invincible while dealing continuous damage to nearby enemies. When Lunox uses Chaos, she gains Darkening and can blink in a target direction and greatly reduces the cooldown of Cosmic Fission for a brief period.

General:

- Cooldown by level: 30.0 / 26.0 / 22.0
- Mana cost by level: 120 / 160 / 200
- Base Damage: 100 / 120 / 140
- Terms: Invincible, Blink

Power of Order: Brilliance

- Lunox extends the Light Barrier, becomes invincible for 2.5 seconds, and gains 30% extra Movement Speed while dealing magic damage every 0.5 seconds to nearby enemies.
- Base Damage per pulse: 100 / 120 / 140
- Spellvamp ratio: 50%
- Has the radius of 3.2 unit.

Notes for Brilliance:

- Brilliance and Darkening have separate cooldown timers.
- Certain items and abilities can be used during the process.
- Does not remove any ongoing control effects or impending buff, such as Cyclone Eye, and does not pass through obstacles.
- Disappears on the minimap for the duration.
- Is still attackable by creeps, minions, Lord and Turtle.
- The remaining invincible time is visible to both allies and enemies.
- The first pulse takes effect right upon cast, resulting in a total of 6 damage pulses.
- Bug note: If the graphic setting is not suitable, certain battlefield effects may disappear or not display when invincible.

Power of Chaos: Darkening

- Lunox blinks in the target direction, dealing magic damage to nearby enemies, slowing them by 30% for 4 seconds. For the next 4 seconds, Lunox enhances her Cosmic Fission to greatly reduce the cooldown of Cosmic Fission (unaffected by CD Reduction).
- Base Damage: 250 / 350 / 450
- Blink distance: 3.5 unit; slow and damage radius: 3.2 unit.

Notes for Darkening:

- Brilliance and Darkening have separate cooldown timers.
- The cast range of Darkening becomes visible for the duration.
- The cooldown of Cosmic Fission is set to 0.3 seconds and is not affected by cooldown reduction and cooldown speed for the duration.
- Cosmic Fission does not cost mana for the duration.
- The duration begins as soon as the ability is cast, which means if the landing is interrupted, the damage and slow effect of this skill do not take effect.
- The remaining duration is visible to both allies and enemies.
- If Lunox has neither Order nor Chaos stack upon learning ultimate, this ultimate is selected by default.

# Trivia

- Lunox' name is a portmanteau of two Latin words *lumos* and *nox*, respectively "light" and "dark" in English.
- Her old hero title is named "the Enlightened One", in reference to the name of Buddha.
- She is the main protagonist of the Determination event along with Belerick.
- Lunox's Light and Darkness concept was reused for Luo Yi.
- Her complementary "Bloody Mary" skin is a reference to Mary I of England, so called due to her execution of numerous Protestants during her 6-year reign. It is also the name of a popular drink.
- Lunox's light powers come from her mother, and her dark powers come from her father.
- Lunox is Unique God's gift to the world (Land of Dawn).
