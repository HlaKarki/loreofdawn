# Story

The Shura clan have thrived in isolation for countless generations, in the Arcapada universe. Their men, renowned for being the world's bravest fighters, and their women famed for being the world's greatest beauties. At the heart of the Shura clan's beliefs lies the legend of the Three Thousand Worlds. The legends speaks of three thousand secret worlds, which upon entering, one must face various levels of psychic and physical trials and advance to world after world upon victory. The people of the Shura clan had discovered a way to enter these worlds, and only those who have conquered over three hundred worlds could be granted the title of Asura, raising them to the level of a demigod in the eyes of their people. However, in the innumerable eons of Shura history, none managed to conquer the fabled three thousand world, until Martis appeared.

With ambition and determination capable of shaking the very heavens, Martis trained every day of his life to become stronger, tirelessly advancing through the secret worlds, each personal breakthrough against the legions of demons granting him a sense of unique personal satisfaction. No one knew how many years passed before Martis reached the final and most hard core secret world. However, his desire for glory drove him onward. After numerous defeats, he fell into despair, but even then, he refused to give up. He transformed his sheer thirst for victory into his twin blades of righteous fury, now known as the Asura's Teeth, and for countless days and nights, battled against the demons of the final world with strength and determination that had never been seen before on the mortal plane. He finally became the first Asura to defeat the three thousand secret worlds and has ever since been regarded as the 'Asura King', ruling, more than tens of milions of aspiring Asura.

# Abilities

## Asura's Wrath

Each time Martis uses a skill, his Attack Speed will be increased by 30%, up to a maximum of 120%. Lasts 4 seconds. At full stacks, he gains 6*Hero Level extra Attack.

## Asura Aura

- Cooldown by level: 7.0 / 6.6 / 6.2 / 5.8 / 5.4 / 5.0 seconds.
- Base Damage: 350 / 410 / 470 / 530 / 590 / 650 (physical damage).
- Extra Physical Attack scaling: 130 physical attack.

Description:

Martis moves forward a short distance and deals damage to enemies hit, slowing them by 40% for 2 seconds. This skill affects enemies in a fan-shaped area in front of him.

Notes:

- This skill makes Martis move forward a little, thus it counts as a blink-type movement despite not having a blink tag.
- This skill is disabled while inside certain enemy ultimates that suppress or disable skills.
- This skill can enable or interact with other heroes' ultimates in specific cases.

## Mortal Coil

- Cooldown: 8.0 seconds.
- First-phase Base Damage: 225 / 240 / 255 / 270 / 285 / 300 (physical damage).
- Extra Damage (secondary hits) scaling: 300 / 320 / 340 / 360 / 380 / 400 (physical damage).

Description:

Martis dashes in the target direction up to three times, each strike dealing damage based on level and stats to enemies hit. Enemies struck by the first and third strikes are knocked back. The attack direction can be changed once using the joystick. Use Again: Martis dashes again in the target direction, dealing additional damage based on level and stats to enemies in his path. Martis gains Control Immunity and 60% Damage Reduction while casting this skill.

Notes:

- This skill is considered a blink-type movement and can be used to traverse terrain gaps in some cases.
- The skill's damage and effects scale with level and physical attack.
- The skill can be interrupted by strong suppression effects that fully disable skills. It can also be disabled by rooted effects that prevent movement-dependent actions.

## Decimation

- Cooldown by level: 36.0 / 32.0 / 28.0 seconds.
- Base Damage: 800 / 900 / 1000 (physical damage).
- Extra Physical Attack scaling: 100 physical attack.

Description:

Martis lunges at a designated enemy hero, dealing damage (deals True Damage if the enemy's HP is below 50%). If the enemy is killed by this skill, Martis can cast the skill again within 10 seconds and gains 100% extra Movement Speed that gradually decays over 5 seconds. Each successive cast also increases the skill's damage by 15% (up to 30%).

Notes:

- When an enemy hero's HP drops below certain thresholds, a visual mark appears beneath them indicating vulnerability to this skill.
- There are two animations for Decimation: in the first attempt he stabs the enemy with his blade after dashing in; in subsequent slays he jumps and stabs the enemy from above with his pair of blades. Both animations do not differ in effect in-game.
- The re-cast and bonus effects are only granted upon eliminating or attempting to eliminate an enemy hero with this skill.

# Trivia

- The name Martis is derived from the Roman god of war, Mars. In Roman mythology, Mars was the god of war, battle, and violence, often associated with aggression and conflict. The name Martis retains this connection to warrior-like strength, power, and fighting prowess.
- Martis' Quote "3000 worlds and not a single worthy foe" was a bluff, he was actually defeated several times and was even put in despair
- The term "3000 Worlds" is a hyperbolic reference to the 3000 Trials, where individuals must face and clear each trial (World/Level). Those who successfully complete 300 of these levels are considered to be at the level of a Demi-God in the eyes of the Asura Clan.
- After completing the 3000 Trials, Martis encountered the Demi-God Gatotkaca, and a fierce battle ensued. Both were equally matched in strength, and their fight alone created an entrance to the Timerift. After the 3000 Trials, Martis met a Demi-God Gatotkaca and fought, both were equal in strength and their fight alone opened an entrance to the timerift.
- Martis is canonically one of the most powerful hero in MLBB. Gatotkaca being his only equal and to stop the Ashura wrath.
