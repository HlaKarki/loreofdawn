# Story

The Shura clan have thrived in isolation for countless generations, in the Arcapada universe. Their men, renowned for being the world's bravest fighters, and their women famed for being the world's greatest beauties. At the heart of the Shura clan's beliefs lies the legend of the Three Thousand Worlds. The legend speaks of three thousand secret worlds, which upon entering, one must face various levels of psychic and physical trials and advance to world after world upon victory. The people of the Shura clan had discovered a way to enter these worlds, and only those who have conquered over three hundred worlds could be granted the title of Asura, raising them to the level of a demigod in the eyes of their people. However, in the innumerable eons of Shura history, none managed to conquer the fabled three thousand world, until Martis appeared.

With ambition and determination capable of shaking the very heavens, Martis trained every day of his life to become stronger, tirelessly advancing through the secret worlds, each personal breakthrough against the legions of demons granting him a sense of unique personal satisfaction. No one knew how many years passed before Martis reached the final and most hard core secret world. However, his desire for glory drove him onward. After numerous defeats, he fell into despair, but even then, he refused to give up. He transformed his sheer thirst for victory into his twin blades of righteous fury, now known as the Asura's Teeth, and for countless days and nights, battled against the demons of the final world with strength and determination that had never been seen before on the mortal plane. He finally became the first Asura to defeat the three thousand secret worlds and has ever since been regarded as the 'Asura King', ruling more than tens of millions of aspiring Asura.

# Abilities

## Passive — Asura's Wrath
- Effect: Each time Martis uses a skill, his Attack Speed is increased by 30%, up to 120% maximum. Lasts 4 seconds.
- Additional: At full stacks, he gains (6 * Hero Level) extra Attack.
- Type: Buff

## Skill 1 — Asura Aura
- Core effects:
  - Draws enemies into a fan-shaped area in front of him.
  - Crowd control (CC) and AoE.
  - Slows enemies (movement speed reduced by 40%) for 2 seconds.
- Damage / scaling:
  - Base Physical Damage: 350 / 410 / 470 / 530 / 590 / 650
  - Extra Physical Attack scaling: 130
- Cooldown: 7.0 / 6.6 / 6.2 / 5.8 / 5.4 / 5.0
- Notes:
  - This skill causes Martis to move forward slightly (functions similarly to a blink).
  - Disabled while inside Minsitthar's ultimate.
  - Enables Phoveus' ultimate.

## Skill 2 — Mortal Coil
- Core effects:
  - Martis strikes in the target direction three times. Each hit deals physical damage to enemies hit.
  - Enemies hit by the first and third strike are knocked back (airborne / CC).
  - Can be used again ("Use Again"): Martis dashes in the target direction, dealing additional damage to enemies in his path.
  - Martis gains Control Immunity and damage reduction while casting the skill.
- Damage / scaling:
  - First/primary base damage: 225 / 240 / 255 / 270 / 285 / 300
  - Extra damage (secondary): 300 / 320 / 340 / 360 / 380 / 400
  - Extra Physical Attack scaling: 120 (first), 150 (extra)
- Cooldown: 8.0
- Properties / tags: Blink, AoE, Control Immunity, Damage Reduction
- Notes / interactions:
  - The attack direction can be changed once using the joystick during the cast.
  - The skill provides short control immunity and 60% damage reduction when casting.
  - The skill can be interrupted by certain suppression/rooting effects and can be affected by knockback or other CC that interrupts actions.
  - Certain items (e.g., Brute Force Breastplate) or passive interactions may modify effects or interactions with other heroes' skills.

## Ultimate — Decimation
- Name: Decimation
- Core effects:
  - Martis lunges at a designated enemy hero, dealing heavy damage (deals True Damage if the enemy's HP is below 50%).
  - If the enemy is killed by this skill, Martis can cast the skill again within 10 seconds.
  - Upon kill, he gains 100% extra Movement Speed that gradually decays over 5 seconds.
  - Each successive cast within the window increases the skill's damage by 15% (up to 30%).
- Damage / scaling:
  - Base Damage: 800 / 900 / 1000
  - Extra Physical Attack scaling: 100
- Cooldown: 36.0 / 32.0 / 28.0
- Properties / tags: Burst, Buff, Current HP check, Movement Speed bonus; interacts with resurrection/immortality effects
- Notes:
  - When an enemy hero's HP drops below a threshold, an indicator appears beneath them.
  - There are two animations for the skill (first dash stab; subsequent jump-and-stab), but the gameplay effect is equivalent.
  - The skill can be interrupted by crowd control and is disabled by rooting in some cases.
  - Killing certain resurrected heroes or triggering immortality effects may still allow recast behavior depending on interactions.

# Trivia
- The name Martis is derived from the Roman god of war, Mars. In Roman mythology, Mars was the god of war, battle, and violence, often associated with aggression and conflict. The name Martis retains this connection to warrior-like strength, power, and fighting prowess.
- Martis' quote:
> "3000 worlds and not a single worthy foe"
  - This was a bluff — he was actually defeated several times and experienced despair during his trials.
- The term "3000 Worlds" refers to the 3000 Trials: individuals must face and clear each trial (world/level). Those who complete 300 of these levels are considered at the level of a demi-god in the eyes of the Asura Clan.
- After completing the 3000 Trials, Martis encountered the demi-god Gatotkaca; their fierce battle was equal in strength and opened an entrance to the timerift.
- Martis is canonically one of the most powerful heroes in MLBB. Gatotkaca is presented as his only equal, able to stand against the Asura's wrath.
