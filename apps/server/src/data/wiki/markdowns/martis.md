# Story

The Shura clan have thrived in isolation for countless generations, in the Arcapada universe. Their men, renowned for being the world's bravest fighters, and their women famed for being the world's greatest beauties. At the heart of the Shura clan's beliefs lies the legend of the Three Thousand Worlds. The legends speaks of three thousand secret worlds, which upon entering, one must face various levels of psychic and physical trials and advance to world after world upon victory. The people of the Shura clan had discovered a way to enter these worlds, and only those who have conquered over three hundred worlds could be granted the title of Asura, raising them to the level of a demigod in the eyes of their people. However, in the innumerable eons of Shura history, none managed to conquer the fabled three thousand world, until Martis appeared.

With ambition and determination capable of shaking the very heavens, Martis trained every day of his life to become stronger, tirelessly advancing through the secret worlds, each personal breakthrough against the legions of demons granting him a sense of unique personal satisfaction. No one knew how many years passed before Martis reached the final and most hard core secret world. However, his desire for glory drove him onward. After numerous defeats, he fell into despair, but even then, he refused to give up. He transformed his sheer thirst for victory into his twin blades of righteous fury, now known as the Asura's Teeth, and for countless days and nights, battled against the demons of the final world with strength and determination that had never been seen before on the mortal plane. He finally became the first Asura to defeat the three thousand secret worlds and has ever since been regarded as the 'Asura King', ruling, more than tens of milions of aspiring Asura.

# Abilities

## Asura's Wrath

Each time Martis uses a skill, his Attack Speed will be increased by 30%, 120% maximum. Lasts 4 seconds. He gains (6*Hero Level) extra Attack at full stacks.

## Asura Aura

- Cooldown: 7.0 / 6.6 / 6.2 / 5.8 / 5.4 / 5.0 seconds
- Base Damage: 350 / 410 / 470 / 530 / 590 / 650 (physical damage based on skill level)
- Extra Physical Attack: 130

Description:
- Draws enemies to a fan-shaped area in front of him and deals scaling physical damage to enemies hit.
- Applies a Movement Speed slow of 40% for 2 seconds.

Notes:
- This skill makes Martis move forward a little, thus it counts as a blink-type movement despite not having a blink tag.
- This skill is disabled while inside Minsitthar's ultimate.
- This skill enables Phoveus' ultimate.

## Mortal Coil

- Cooldown: 8.0 seconds
- Base Damage (first set): 225 / 240 / 255 / 270 / 285 / 300 (physical damage based on skill level)
- Extra Damage (second set): 300 / 320 / 340 / 360 / 380 / 400 (physical damage based on skill level)

Description:
- Martis strikes in the target direction three times, each hit dealing scaling physical damage to enemies struck. Enemies hit by the first and third strike are also knocked back. The attack direction can be changed once using the Joystick.
- Use Again: Martis dashes in the target direction, dealing scaling physical damage to enemies in his path. Martis gains Control Immunity and 60% Damage Reduction while casting this skill.

Notes:
- The skill consists of multiple slashes with short delays between them: a quick area slash near Martis, followed by another forward slash, and finally a finishing slash.
- The second phase of the skill knocks nearby opponents away from Martis upon contact.
- This skill provides mobility (blink/dash) and has strong CC interactions (knockback / airborne) and can be interrupted by suppression effects.

## Decimation

- Cooldown: 36.0 / 32.0 / 28.0 seconds
- Base Damage: 800 / 900 / 1000 (physical damage based on skill level)
- Extra Scaling: 100 extra physical attack scaling

Description:
- Martis lunges at a designated enemy hero, dealing scaling physical damage (deals True Damage if the enemy's HP is below 50%). If the enemy is killed by this skill, Martis can cast the skill again within 10 seconds and gains 100% extra Movement Speed that gradually decays over 5 seconds. Each successive cast also increases the skill's damage by 15% (up to 30%).

Notes:
- When an enemy Hero's HP drops below 40%, a marker will appear beneath them.
- There are two animations for this skill: a dash-stab animation on the first cast and a jump-stab animation on subsequent casts; both deal the same in-game effects.
- If Martis eliminates a hero who is resurrected by certain resurrection effects, the skill's recast and bonuses can still trigger under some conditions.
- This skill can be interrupted by crowd control effects during the dash and can be affected by Rooted-type disabling effects.

# Trivia

- The name Martis is derived from the Roman god of war, Mars. In Roman mythology, Mars was the god of war, battle, and violence, often associated with aggression and conflict. The name Martis retains this connection to warrior-like strength, power, and fighting prowess.
- Martis' Quote "3000 worlds and not a single worthy foe" was a bluff, he was actually defeated several times and was even put in despair
- The term "3000 Worlds" is a hyperbolic reference to the 3000 Trials, where individuals must face and clear each trial (World/Level). Those who successfully complete 300 of these levels are considered to be at the level of a Demi-God in the eyes of the Asura Clan.
- After completing the 3000 Trials, Martis encountered the Demi-God Gatotkaca, and a fierce battle ensued.
