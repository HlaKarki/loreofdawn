## Lore

A year and a half ago, the Scholars' Union of Eruditio welcomed a new student into their ranks. Unlike the other newcomers, this young girl named Beatrix seemed to possess profound knowledge of and astonishing talent for science. Before long she stood head and shoulders above her peers, becoming one of the most promising scholars of the entire Scholars' Union. Even Dr. Rooney, who was watching her quietly from afar, was impressed by her progress. He believed that, if she were to apply herself to her studies, Beatrix could one day become the greatest scientist Eruditio had ever seen.

Yet becoming the best scientist was not something Beatrix placed any great importance in. She was born in Castle Gorge in the Moniyan Empire, to a family of merchants. For generations, her family had been well established as a wealthy merchant family in the empire. To maintain their trade network, Beatrix's parents often traveled around the Land of Dawn for business, leaving the young Beatrix in the care of their loyal butler, Morgan.

With the means and liberty granted by her family, Beatrix spent a free-spirited childhood under Morgan's care. She gained a penchant for mischief and became stubborn and capricious. One day her parents returned from the far west with advanced gadgets from Eruditio. Tired of mundane toys, Beatrix obsessed over these novelties; they sparked a deep interest in technology. Morgan regularly shuttled between Eruditio and Castle Gorge to bring her the latest inventions, an expensive endeavor. She studied them tirelessly and developed a remarkable natural talent for science, eventually building her own laboratory in the castle grounds and researching various technologies and weaponry.

After her 16th birthday, Beatrix left for Eruditio to continue her studies, seeking to learn about Leviathan technologies. She absorbed knowledge at incredible speed, but her obsession and unconventional approaches upset conservative Union members. Locked out of the Leviathan Workshop, she provoked controversy by proposing a radical idea: connecting the human body to communication terminals to establish direct neurological links. The idea resembled concepts promoted by the rogue Laboratory 1718, and she was interrupted and dismissed from the debate. Feeling unfairly treated, she left Eruditio without farewell and returned to Castle Gorge to realize her inventions with Morgan's help.

About half a year later, a competition called "Survival: Nexus" swept Eruditio. Convenient terminals allowed participants to control avatars with their brainwaves and battle in a virtual realm. Beatrix was the creator of this technological system; she enticed participants with large prize money and watched her project gain popularity among scholars and citizens.

However, Laboratory 1718 maliciously hacked the network. Participants connected to the system were taken over; Laboratory 1718 stole the "Iron Guardians" — a robot army sealed beneath the Eruditio Congress Building. Those trapped in the terminals, still immersed in virtual battles, unwittingly controlled the robots and caused massive destruction across Eruditio. The city plunged into crisis.

The remaining scholars joined the Eruditio City Guard under Dr. Rooney and retreated to the Leviathan Workshop to defend the city's technological achievements. At a critical moment, Beatrix returned in her airship, raining down firepower to prevent her invention from being used to destroy the city. With her aid, the City Guard beat back the robot forces and seized control of the robots and the virtual network from Octavius, the insane biochemist. Peace was restored.

After averting the crisis, Beatrix earned Dr. Rooney's attention and tutelage. She came to understand the essence of technology, gifted her "Operation Dawnbreak" terminal system to the Scholars' Union, and decided to remain in Eruditio to continue her studies. The doors to the Leviathan Workshop were opened to her at last.

## Lethal Nexus

> "Beatrix's theoretical proposition was not accepted. She felt unfairly treated. Without bidding farewell, Beatrix departed Eruditio and returned to her laboratory back in Castle Gorge. With Morgan's help, she utilized her family's wealth and resources and began bringing her invention concepts to reality. About half a year or so had passed, the "Survival: Nexus" competition began to take Eruditio by storm. Convenient terminals allowed participants to virtually control avatars solely with their brainwaves, immersing themselves in a virtual realm. The creator behind this technological marvel was none other than the student who had stormed out of Eruditio just half a year earlier: Beatrix. Thanks to the enormous prize money she'd offered to the competition victor, Eruditio scholars and citizens alike were queuing up to partake in "Survival: Nexus". Beatrix watched on with glee as her prized project proves the conservative thinkers of the Scholars' Union. But then, disaster struck."

## Trivia (selected)
- The name Beatrix comes from Viatrix (Late Latin Viator), meaning "Voyager" or "Traveler".
- In the New Arrival Shop, Beatrix's hero description: "A gifted crack shot with a variety of strong and lively weapons".
- Carrying different weapons puts Beatrix in different moods, explaining different voice tones when picking up weapons.
- According to Lethal Nexus character info, Beatrix is 17 years old and makes weird inventions as a hobby.
- In an April 2021 question (Trial of Knowledge), Beatrix dislikes the number four.
  - Note: The number 4 is considered unlucky in some East Asian cultures because of homophony with the word for "death".
- Beatrix is the first hero with up to four different dying animations.
- Beatrix's skills do not benefit from some unspecified stat(s) but do benefit from others (source contains truncated specifics).
- Beatrix is among heroes whose passive converts one stat to another (stat-conversion mechanic).
- Beatrix can attack turrets from outside turret range using certain weapons; she is the fourth hero with that capability.
- Including Survival: Nexus and Ravage mode, Beatrix has a total of 10 weapons.
- Known weapons listed: pair of [unnamed], a heavy machine gun, a boomerang, laser gun, energy gun, flamethrower, sword; several weapons currently have no known names.
- Beatrix was originally called Boudicca.
- In Advanced Server testing, one of her abilities was previously a circular AoE before being revamped.
- Her Ultimate's original line was "Bullets around!" before being changed.
- She originally saluted with the right hand in her hero animation; now she salutes with her left while holding a weapon in her right.
- Born in Castle Gorge (Moniyan Empire), currently resides in Eruditio.
- "X Factor" refers to a noteworthy talent/quality and is also a variable with significant impact.
