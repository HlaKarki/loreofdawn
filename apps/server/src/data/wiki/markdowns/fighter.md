Fighters are close-range combatants that possess a mixture of offensive and defensive capabilities.

**Fighter's** damage will add up over time to make it a major threat.

Each **fighter** has a unique blend of mobility, damage, disruption and durability.

The role of a **Fighter** is an intermediate melee hero, between durability or damage, hence makes fighters serve as multi-purpose combatant, such as the Semi-Tank, Crowd Controller, or Burst Damage Dealer.
