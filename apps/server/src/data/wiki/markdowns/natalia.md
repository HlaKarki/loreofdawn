# Story

> "Assassin exiled by the Moniyan Empire."

The chief assassin of the Church of Light. She once made a mistake during an assassination mission, making companion encounter unprecedented disaster. Although she was able to escape with the assistance of Tigreal, she was later exiled to the border to the Empire to perform more dangerous tasks.

# Bio

Natalia was orphaned from the war and she was raised as a little girl by the Monastery of Light. Natalia was trained into becoming one of the Light assassins. This young phenom had risen to become the top assassin for the Monastery, via her outstanding talents.

However, on one assassination mission, she failed in her execution, causing her companions to meet with unprecedented disaster and death. Although she managed to escape danger, with Tigreal's help, this was undoubtedly a severe blow to Natalia's pride. To prove herself again, she exiled herself to the outskirts of the land, far from the Moniyan Empire and the Monastery of Light, to carry out more dangerous missions. It is never known who her target is or where she might strike next. All that people know is that Natalia intends to win back all that she once possessed.

# Abilities

## Assassin Instinct

- If Natalia stays in a bush without taking damage for 1 second, she will enter Camouflage state and gain 15% Movement Speed.
- In this state, Natalia's next Basic Attack will teleport her behind her target and deal scaling damage (damage based on level and stats) while silencing the target for 0.25 seconds. Dealing damage, taking damage or using skills will immediately end the Camouflage state, but her next Basic Attack will remain enhanced for another 1 second.
- Natalia's base Crit Damage is reduced to 150%, but her damage to Creeps is increased to 150%.
- Her basic attack range is also increased to 3.8 units during Camouflage state.

## Claw Dash

- Cooldown: 10.0 / 9.6 / 9.2 / 8.8 / 8.4 / 8.0 seconds
- Mana Cost: 50 / 55 / 60 / 65 / 70 / 75
- Description: Natalia dashes in the target direction, dealing damage to enemies in her path (damage based on skill level and Natalia's physical attack). After hitting an enemy, she can cast this skill again within 5 seconds.
- Base Damage: 250 / 270 / 290 / 310 / 330 / 350 (physical)

## Smoke Bomb

- Cooldown: 12.0 seconds
- Mana Cost: 80 / 85 / 90 / 95 / 100 / 105
- Quote: "No one beats me in my own game."
- Description: Natalia releases a Smoke Bomb at her location, slowing enemies within by 25%–75% while Natalia becomes immune to enemy Basic Attacks for 4 seconds.
- Slow Effect: 25% / 35% / 45% / 55% / 65% / 75%

## The Hunt

- Cooldown (per charge): 3.0 seconds
- Mana Cost: 90 / 110 / 130
- Recharge time per charge: 30.0 / 27.0 / 24.0 seconds
- Description: Passive: Natalia gains extra Crit Chance. Active: Natalia immediately enters Assassin Instinct state. Natalia can store up to 2 charges of her Ultimate and each charge has a recharge time as listed. Creep kills will reduce the current remaining recharge time by 50% of the full recharge time.
- Crit Chance: 15% / 30% / 45%
- Notes: The cooldown of this skill is not affected by Cooldown Reduction.
