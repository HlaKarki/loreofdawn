## Story

> "Assassin exiled by the Moniyan Empire."

- The chief assassin of the Church of Light (Church of Light).  
- She once made a mistake during an assassination mission, causing her companions to encounter unprecedented disaster.  
- Although she was able to escape with the assistance of Tigreal (Tigreal), she was later exiled to the border to the Empire (Moniyan Empire) to perform more dangerous tasks.

---

## Lore

- Natalia was orphaned from the war and she was raised as a little girl by the Monastery of Light (Monastery of Light).  
- Natalia was trained into becoming one of the Light assassins.  
- This young phenom had risen to become the top assassin for the Monastery, via her outstanding talents.  
- However, on one assassination mission, she failed in her execution, causing her companions to meet with unprecedented disaster and death.  
- Although she managed to escape danger, with Tigreal's help (Tigreal), this was undoubtedly a severe blow to Natalia's pride.  
- To prove herself again, she exiled herself to the outskirts of the land, far from the Moniyan Empire and the Monastery of Light (Moniyan Empire), to carry out more dangerous missions.  
- It is never known who her target is or where she might strike next.  
- All that people know is that Natalia intends to win back all that she once possessed.

## Abilities

### Passive — Assassin Instinct
- Scaling: base 200, total physical attack scaling 110%, armor-piercing? (pd)
- Effect:
  - If Natalia stays in a bush without taking damage for 1 second, she will enter Camouflage state and gain 15% Movement Speed.
  - In this state, Natalia's next Basic Attack will teleport her behind her target and deal damage while silencing the target for 0.25 seconds.
  - Dealing damage, taking damage or using skills will immediately end the Camouflage state, but her next Basic Attack will remain enhanced for another 1 second.
  - Natalia's base Crit Damage is reduced to 150%, but her damage to Creeps is increased to 150%.
- Notes:
  - Her basic attack range is also increased to 3.8 units during Camouflage state.

### Skill 1 — Claw Dash
- Scaling: base 250–350, total physical attack scaling 50% (pd)
- Effects: Blink; AoE; Physical Damage Skill
- Cooldown: 10.0 / 9.6 / 9.2 / 8.8 / 8.4 / 8.0
- Mana cost: 50 / 55 / 60 / 65 / 70 / 75
- Description: Natalia dashes in the target direction, dealing damage to enemies in her path. After hitting an enemy, she can cast this skill again within 5 seconds.
- Base Damage by level: 250 / 270 / 290 / 310 / 330 / 350

### Skill 2 — Smoke Bomb
> "No one beats me in my own game."

- Effects: Buff; Slow
- Cooldown: 12.0
- Mana cost: 80 / 85 / 90 / 95 / 100 / 105
- Description: Natalia releases a Smoke Bomb at her location, slowing enemies within by 25%–75% while Natalia becomes immune to enemy Basic Attacks for 4 seconds.
- Slow effect by level: 25% / 35% / 45% / 55% / 65% / 75%

### Ultimate — The Hunt
- Charges/Mechanic: Natalia can store up to 2 charges of her Ultimate; each charge has a recharge time of 30 seconds. Creep kills will reduce the current remaining recharge time by 50% of the full recharge time.
- Cooldown (per charge): 3.0 (note: the skill cooldown is not affected by Cooldown Reduction)
- Mana cost: 90 / 110 / 130
- Recharging time: 30.0 / 27.0 / 24.0
- Effects: Conceal; Buff; Blink; increases Crit Rate; affects Physical Basic Attack
- Description:
  - Passive: Natalia gains extra Crit Chance.
  - Active: Natalia immediately enters Assassin Instinct state.
- Crit Chance by level: 15% / 30% / 45%
- Notes: The cooldown of this skill is not affected by Cooldown Reduction.

## Trivia
- Natalia's name has Latin origins and means "Christmas Day;" the name was usually given to girls born in the Christmas period. Coincidentally, she was released in December 2016.
- Natalia is likely inspired by the Assassins of the Assassin's Creed video game series, as evident in her design and quotes (Assassin's Creed).
- Natalia is one of the heroes who has an ability that interacts with the environment (the bush).
- Her ultimate quote, "Nothing is true!", is a phrase attributed to Hassan-i Sabbah, the founder of the Order of the Hashshashin from which the term "assassin" originates.
> "Nothing is true!"
- Cyber Spectre is the skin that won Best Design in MLBB's 1st Skin Design Competition by MGL.
- Natalia's Collector Skin "Warrioress Paragon" was given away for a limited time, making it the first free collector skin ever.
- Natalia's free collector skin and Miya's collector skin are related. Miya's arrows can open portals to the future; Natalia came through this portal.
