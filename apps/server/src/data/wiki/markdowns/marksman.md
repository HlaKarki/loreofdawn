# Marksmen

**Marksmen** are ranged heroes whose power is almost entirely based on their basic attacks: by using their reach to land massive continuous damage from a distance, marksmen are capable of taking down even the most difficult opponents when positioned behind the safety of their team, and perform better at securing objectives such as Lord and turrets.

Marksmen are classified into three types: pure, primary, and secondary.
