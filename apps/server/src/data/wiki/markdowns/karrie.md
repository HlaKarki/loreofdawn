# Karrie

# Profile

- Alias: Lost Star
- Origin: Alaghat
- Species: Yasson
- Gender: Female
- Weapons: Speedy Lightwheel
- English VA: Feena Glynn
- Japanese VA: Daimon Kami (大門 香実)

# Story

> "A Yasson marksman who came from another dimension. Summoned by the prophet, she is determined to break the prophecy."

Karrie was trained by the Empire to become a war machine from when she was a child because of her incredible talent. After one battle, the seriously wounded Karrie fused with the consciousness of an alien prophet, so she knew the fate of the demise of her hometown, and to find a way to break the prophecy, she left Alaghat.

# Bio

Alaghat is a mysterious technique empire. There lives a strange humanoid race - Yasson. They are in human form while having a pair of deformed feet. They are agile and born to fight. They are also good at techniques that allow them to create a significant civilization and to invade others.

Since Karrie was a child, she was very talented and trained into an excellent and arrogant warrior leader. But at the same time, she lost her feelings and became a machine. During one battle, she was overconfident and got seriously injured. But a prophet from another race saved her and injected his consciousness into her brain. Since then there has been a voice in her head; the prophet told her that the ruin of her empire is about to come and there will be no Yasson anymore. After her escape from death, Karrie didn't believe the voice in her head. But she started to suspect it was right after returning to her empire. In order to save her empire, and for her own redemption, Karrie chose to believe and came to the Land of Dawn.

What kind of destiny is waiting for her?

# Abilities

## Lightwheel Mark

Karrie's Basic Attacks and skills apply a stack of Lightwheel Mark on hit. Once a target has 5 stacks of Lightwheel Marks, the marks will turn into a lightwheel, dealing True Damage equal to 6%-8% of the target's Max HP to them (only up to 300 damage against Creeps).

- Effect: Applies Lightwheel Mark on hit; at 5 stacks convert to a lightwheel that deals True Damage equal to 6%–8% of target's Max HP.
- Creep cap: Damage against Creeps is capped at 300.
- Damage type: True Damage.

## Spinning Lightwheel

Karrie releases a sphere of surging energy in the target direction, dealing damage to enemies in its path. The sphere will stop upon hitting an enemy hero or reaching its max travel distance, continuously dealing sustained damage to nearby enemies and slowing them by 80% for 1s.

- Mana cost (per level): 40 / 45 / 50 / 55 / 60 / 65.
- Base Damage (per level): 250 / 275 / 300 / 325 / 350 / 375.
- Sustained Damage (per level while sphere persists): 150 / 160 / 170 / 180 / 190 / 200.
- Slow: 80% movement speed slow for 1 second when affected.
- Damage type: Physical Skill.

## Phantom Step

Karrie dashes in the target direction while throwing a lightwheel at the nearest enemy, dealing damage and applying a stack of Lightwheel Mark on them. When in Dual Wield state, Karrie throws two lightwheels instead.

- Mana cost (per level): 35 / 40 / 45 / 50 / 55 / 60.
- Base Damage (per level, thrown lightwheel): 150 / 175 / 200 / 225 / 250 / 275.
- Skill effects: Blink (dash) + damage + applies Lightwheel Mark on hit.
- Damage type: Physical Skill.

## Speedy Lightwheel

Karrie enters Dual Wield state for a duration, during which she gains Movement Speed and throws two lightwheels with each Basic Attack, each dealing a portion of Basic Attack damage and inheriting a portion of Attack Effects.

- Mana cost (per level): 70 / 80 / 90.
- Duration (per level): 6 / 8 / 10 seconds.
- Attack Damage (per level): 50% / 60% / 70% of Basic Attack damage for each lightwheel.
- Attack Effect inheritance (per level): 50% / 60% / 70% of Attack Effects are inherited by each lightwheel.
- Skill effects: BUFF (Dual Wield), Burst; grants Movement Speed (20% at base) and makes Basic Attacks throw two lightwheels.

# Trivia

- The name Karrie is a feminine given name, primarily an alternate form of Carrie, and a diminutive of Caroline or Carol, meaning "beloved" or "friend".
- Karrie is one of the heroes who can deal more than one type of damage with her skills (Physical and True).
- Her second skill "Phantom Step" is the reuse skill of the first generation.
- Her Wheel of Justice skin is based on Bastet.
- Karrie was officially released in Mobile Legends in 2017. Her arrival marked a shift in the marksman role, as she was designed to be a tank-buster hero capable of melting through the durability of tanky heroes, something that few other marksmen could do at the time.
