# Karrie

- Alias: Lost Star  
- Origin: Alaghat  
- Species: Yasson  
- Gender: Female  
- Weapons: Speedy Lightwheel  
- English VA: Feena Glynn (imdb:nm8441206)  
- Japanese VA: Daimon Kami (大門 香実)

## Story

> "A Yasson marksman who came from another dimension. Summoned by the prophet, she is determined to break the prophecy."

Karrie was trained by the Empire to become a war machine from when she was a child because of her incredible talent. After one battle, the seriously wounded Karrie fused with the consciousness of an alien prophet, so she knew the fate of the demise of her hometown, and to find a way to break the prophecy, she left Alaghat.

## Lore

- Alaghat is a mysterious technique empire.
- There lives a strange humanoid race — Yasson.
- They are in human form while having a pair of deformed feet.
- They are agile and born to fight.
- They are also good at techniques that allow them to create a significant civilization and to invade others.
- Since Karrie was a child, she was very talented and trained into an excellent and arrogant warrior leader.
- At the same time, she lost her feelings and became a machine.
- During one battle, she was overconfident and got seriously injured.
- A prophet from another race saved her and injected his consciousness into her brain.
- Since then there has been a voice in her head; the prophet told her that the ruin of her empire is about to come and there will be no Yasson anymore.
- After escaping death, Karrie didn't initially believe the voice in her head, but she began to suspect it was right after returning to her empire.
- To save her empire and for her own redemption, Karrie chose to believe and came to the Land of Dawn.
- What kind of destiny is waiting for her?

## Abilities

### Passive — Lightwheel Mark (Kishin Densetsu)
- Effect: BUFF  
- Description: Karrie's Basic Attacks and skills apply a stack of Lightwheel Mark on hit. Once a target has 5 stacks of Lightwheel Marks, the marks turn into a lightwheel, dealing True Damage equal to 6%–8% of the target's Max HP (capped at 300 vs creeps).  
- Terms: True Damage, Max HP

### Skill 1 — Spinning Lightwheel (Kishin Densetsu)
- Type: Physical Skill  
- Cooldown: 8.0 s  
- Mana Cost: 40 / 45 / 50 / 55 / 60 / 65  
- Description: Karrie releases a sphere of surging energy in the target direction, dealing damage to enemies in its path. The sphere stops upon hitting an enemy hero or reaching max distance, then continuously deals sustained damage to nearby enemies and slows them by 80% for 1 s.  
- Base Damage (per level): 250 / 275 / 300 / 325 / 350 / 375  
- Sustained Damage (per level): 150 / 160 / 170 / 180 / 190 / 200

### Skill 2 — Phantom Step (Kishin Densetsu)
- Effects: Blink, Damage  
- Cooldown: 4.5 / 4.1 / 3.7 / 3.3 / 2.9 / 2.5 s  
- Mana Cost: 35 / 40 / 45 / 50 / 55 / 60  
- Description: Karrie dashes in the target direction while throwing a lightwheel at the nearest enemy, dealing damage and applying a stack of Lightwheel Mark. In Dual Wield state, she throws two lightwheels.  
- Base Damage (per level): 150 / 175 / 200 / 225 / 250 / 275

### Ultimate — Speedy Lightwheel (Kishin Densetsu)
- Effects: BUFF, Burst  
- Cooldown: 35.0 s  
- Mana Cost: 70 / 80 / 90  
- Description: Karrie enters Dual Wield state for a duration, during which she gains Movement Speed and throws two lightwheels with each Basic Attack. Each lightwheel deals a percentage of Basic Attack damage and inherits a portion of Attack Effects.  
- Duration: 6 / 8 / 10 s  
- Attack Damage (per level): 50% / 60% / 70% (each lightwheel)  
- Attack Effect inheritance (per level): 50% / 60% / 70%  
- Terms: Physical Basic Attack, Attack Effect

## Trivia

- The name Karrie is a feminine given name, primarily an alternate form of Carrie, and a diminutive of Caroline or Carol, meaning "beloved" or "friend."
- Karrie is one of the heroes who can deal more than one type of damage with her skills (physical and true).
- Her second skill "Phantom Step" is the reuse skill of the first generation.
- Her Wheel of Justice skin is based on Bastet.
- Karrie was officially released in Mobile Legends in 2017. Her arrival marked a shift in the marksman role, as she was designed to be a tank-buster hero capable of melting through the durability of tanky heroes at the time.
