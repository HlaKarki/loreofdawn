# Bio

At the beginning of the Era of Strife, human beings were just a new and weak race on the Land of Dawn. They worked in all corners of the world and created their own history.

They are both good and evil; they can sacrifice their lives for justice, but also betray their compatriots for their own selfish desires; they themselves are a combination of light and darkness. The huge potential contained in human beings was discovered by the light and dark factions. In order to compete for humans' faith, the Endless War broke out for the second time.

After more than a century of development, while Brun was serving as the patriarch of the Antalus family, they became one of the most powerful and prosperous families in all mankind. Alice is Brun's youngest daughter, but compared with her two distinguished elder brothers, little Alice appears insignificant. Her identity as an illegitimate daughter blindfolded her and she was never valued by the family.

Alice gradually grew up in this malicious environment. She hated everyone around her, hated this unfair destiny, hated the "hypocritical gods" the family believed in, and the darkness continued to spread in her heart.

One day, soldiers returning from the battlefield vied for spoils and divided them as usual. Alice stood in a corner not far away, staring at them coldly.

> "Why do you bring back junk like this? It really gets in the way."

Someone said as they threw a bead to the ground. Alice looked toward the bead, but unexpectedly, this bead, which seemed to be dim and dull to the others, burst into a breathtaking rose-colored light in Alice's eyes. It rolled slowly to Alice's feet, and the moment she touched it with her hand, a demon's voice flooded her mind.

> "Offer your soul, my dear Alice. Accept our power and you will get everything you want."

> "If I get what I want, what do you want from me?"

> "It's very simple. All I need is the faith of your family. Abandon the King of Light and plunge into the embrace of the Abyss."

Faith was not a big issue for Alice, but she remembered the vow that she would utter every time she offered a sacrifice: the never-extinguishing holy light will punish all betrayers. No one knew what the punishment was. Just as Alice was hesitating, Alice's brother Simon found her in the corner. He came to Alice and pushed her down fiercely, saying:

> "It’s you, you superfluous person… What are you doing here?"

Seeing that Alice was not going to answer, he snatched the bead she was holding tightly in her hand. Alice tried to snatch it back, but he kicked her into the air, "A bastard is just a bastard, but a broken bead can be regarded as a treasure." He glanced back at Alice, who had fallen to the ground and was not getting up, he snorted coldly, and continued, "You're just like this bead – superfluous junk."

He flung the bead forcefully at Alice's lower abdomen. The pain caused Alice to let out a blood-curdling scream. The disturbance from the corner attracted the attention of others around. Laughter filled the room for a while, and Alice felt out of breath.

When the crowd dispersed, all the built up humiliation and frustration broke out at this moment. Alice clutched her stomach and stood up with difficulty. She said to the demon:

> "If I succeed, will you give me enough power to take all lives?"

> "Of course, my dear Alice."

> "Okay, then I promise you."

> "That's more like it, hahahaha..."

A scorching energy burned a black mark on the back of Alice's hand, and then disappeared. She put away the bead and left quietly.

Brun was old, but the heir of the Antalus family was not yet elected. Of his two sons, the younger Simon was the one he favored, much to the chagrin of his eldest son Colin. Alice knew that this was her best way in.

Alice secretly provoked animosity between the two brothers, and used this to divide all the tribesmen into two parties who kept fighting. During this time, Alice always stayed by Brun's side, playing the role of a good daughter while at the same time hinting to Brun in a roundabout way that his two sons wanted to kill him and take his place as tribal patriarch.

Without too much effort from Alice, the ridiculous family bond between a father and his two sons was torn to pieces in a power struggle. As the gap between them deepened, a tragedy finally befell them: Colin hatched a plot that killed Simon, and Brun executed Colin under the bewitching influence of Alice.

After Colin's death, Brun fell ill, and Alice assumed the responsibility of the patriarch. At this time, the family was ruined, and all the allies who had been following them betrayed them one by one. On the one hand, Alice took care of Brun, and on the other hand, she used a strong hand to cheer up the family and gained the support and trust of the tribe. Seeing the time was right, she quietly killed Brun by poisoning him.

At the funeral, those of the Antalus family realized that the family had fought for the light for generations, but the light had never blessed them. Nowadays, allies left one after the other, not caring one bit for the friendships of the past. They felt angry but helpless about this. Incited by Alice, the tribesmen came to the altar, threw the holy objects to the ground in a rage, and gave feedback to the King of Light using their own actions.

At the moment when the sacred object was destroyed, a dazzling light fell from the sky above the altar, piercing into the bodies of all the tribespeople including Alice. As they wailed, pitch black wings mixed with blood broke out of their bodies, and sharp fangs grew out of their mouths. This was the punishment mentioned in the oath, the curse that will never be eliminated. From then on, they became monsters who would never see the sun again. They could only survive relying on the blood of other races.

After the light faded, countless blood-stained demonic hands stretched out from the ground. By touching them, the Antalus family members were given the reward called "Eternal Life" by the Abyss. Alice also received the demon’s reward at this time – gaining enough power to take away all lives.

When everything faded, they realized they had fallen into the gap between light and darkness and would no longer be accepted by the world. Under the guidance of the demon, Alice led her tribe to profess their faith in the Lord of the Abyss. After that, the Antalus family disappeared from the world forever. In the faction of the demons, there was an additional race called the Blood Demons. Under the leadership of Queen Alice, they wandered in the darkness and became the most loyal servants of the Abyss.

# Story

> "The Queen of the Abyss who ruined lives of others."

Alice was born in the era of strife long ago. As an illegitimate daughter, she suffered humiliation since she was a child, bewitched by the demons. Alice provoked disputes within the family, eventually taking control of the entire family and leading all of its members to betray the Lord of Light. But their betrayal triggered a divine punishment and turned him into blood demons. As a result, Alice led them to join the Abyss and they had fought for the darkness ever since.

# Abilities

## Crimson Blood Banquet

When Alice's skills hit, she gains 1 stack of Crimson Blood Banquet, lasting 4 seconds. Once Crimson Blood Banquet reaches 2 stacks, it activates, dealing magic damage plus an additional amount equal to 0.5% to 2.5% (scales with level) of the target's Max HP to nearby enemies. The effect refreshes its duration when hitting heroes and recovers HP for Alice every 0.5 seconds. HP recovery from minions is reduced. Can deal up to 45 (+12 per hero level) damage to creeps.

Notes:
- Healing and damage scale with skill level and Alice's stats.
- Spellvamp interactions apply where applicable.

## Crimson Gleam

Alice fires blood energy forward, dealing magic damage based on level (Base Damage: 100 / 120 / 140 / 160 / 180 / 200). Before the blood energy dissipates, this skill can be cast again to activate a blink, allowing Alice to teleport to the blood energy's location.

Cooldown:
- 18.0 / 16.0 / 14.0 / 12.0 / 10.0 / 8.0 seconds (cooldown starts after the stream disappears or when cast again)

Additional:
- Spellvamp ratio: 50%
- Player can hold the skill button to locate the blood sphere.

## Doom Waltz

Alice performs a deadly dance, dealing magic damage to nearby enemies based on level (Base Damage: 350 / 390 / 430 / 470 / 510 / 550) and slowing them by 70% for 1 second. If Crimson Blood Banquet (her passive) is active at this time, it also scorches enemies, dealing 300% of the passive's damage and healing Alice for the same amount.

Cooldown: 4.0 seconds

Additional:
- Spellvamp ratio: 50%.

## Throne of Ruin

Alice surrounds herself with blood, gaining Control Immunity and 50% Damage Reduction while slowing herself by 50%. After 1.5 seconds of charging, she descends with force, dealing area magic damage to all enemies in range (Base Damage: 500 / 600 / 700) and immobilizing them for 1 second. While charging her Ultimate, Alice cannot cast other skills.

Cooldown: 60.0 / 55.0 / 50.0 seconds

Additional:
- Spellvamp ratio: 50%.
- Damage and effects scale with level and Alice's stats.

# Trivia

- The name Alice comes from the Old French name "Aalis", which itself is derived from the Germanic name "Adalheidis" (Adelaide). It means "noble" or "of noble kind."
- She is mostly based by Lilith the Vampiress from Magic Rush; and they both are Vampire Queens with huge regen abilities.
- Alice has the most base mana from levels 4 (alongside Zhuxin) to 15.
- Her full name is Alice Antalus.
- Alice is often regarded as the main antagonist in the Land of Dawn and Mobile Legends: Adventure, appearing in various hero lores as the antagonist.
- Alice’s design shares some similarities with Maleficent, the iconic Disney villain, particularly in their dark, elegant, and sinister aesthetic.
- While not explicitly called a vampire, Alice’s blood-draining abilities and immortality make her MLBB’s vampire equivalent. Her lifesteal mechanic allows her to sustain herself in battle, much like classic vampire lore.
- Currently, Alice and some other heroes are classified as both Mage and Tank in the game.
