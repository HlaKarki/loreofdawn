# Story

> The nightmare of the Abyss who's Willing to betray anyone in order to survive.

Helcurt was born into the Styx Clan of the Abyss and bore the stigma of his clan's betrayal against the Lord of the Abyss. However, in a twist of fate, he was put on the Tribunal tasked with executing traitors for the Lord. To survive, Helcurt was willing to do anything, including hunting his own kin. Yet, during the great uprising against the Lord of the Abyss, Helcurt was the one who slew him. He was born a traitor and never knew the honor of loyalty, but even as a traitor, he had the right to live.

## Born in the Darkness

> Born in the darkness, Helcurt was bestowed with the genius of stalking and ambushing. He dwelt in the shadows, seeking only his prey. Enslaved by the Abyss, he waited for that split second his prey would falter. Now, Helcurt steps forth from the gloom, his blade at the ready. The time of lurking has ended, and the hunt shall begin. Official MLBB socials

They were traitors the moment they were born.

In the darkest depths of the Abyss, the Styx Clan once bared their fangs against the Lord of the Abyss.

The Styx Clan were the deadliest hunters to roam the shadows, born to spread the darkness. They could stalk their enemies for days in complete silence before finally slitting their throats and vanishing into the night. Their talents made them the most feared assassins of the Abyss. But even more terrifying was their beast-like nature, because when survival is at stake, there is no predicting what a beast would do.

> Survive...

Confronted with the Styx Clan's rebellion, even the mighty Lord of the Abyss felt fear. But he could not allow such transgressions against the name of the darkness.

Against the sheer power of the Lord of the Abyss, the Styx Clan was defeated. They were branded as traitors and suffered a most cruel punishment: they were "gifted" the Lord of the Abyss's blood, to be forever shackled to his will. This not only physically prevented them from raising their claws against the Lord of the Abyss, but also served as the greatest form of humiliation. The beasts of the Abyss were thus tamed like dogs.

Helcurt was the offspring of this clan of traitors. He was born in the midst of the great war and witnessed his kin being used as cannon fodder. Their broken bodies filled the battlefields, and eventually, it would be his turn.

> Survive...

The only thought going through Helcurt's mind was to survive. He ran and fought with all his might across the battlefield, and emerged victorious from the merciless meat grinder. After the battle, he was summoned by the Lord of the Abyss. Seeing Helcurt's short, feral stature, the Lord mockingly declared that Helcurt was the "most loyal dog" of his clan, and placed him in the Tribunal to judge and execute traitors.

> You wretched thing. do as I will and you'll get your share of scraps.

Helcurt obeyed. The first traitor judged was one of his clansmen. A veil of darkness shrouded his path that night, and amidst the fearful expressions from his kin, Helcurt slit the traitor's throat with a flash of the blade, fulfilling his mission. The blood flowed silently as he dropped the lifeless body. He carried out the execution without hesitation, but in that instant, he experienced an eternity of anguish.

> Survive...

He was born with the stigma of a traitor, and now he must live up to that name and hunt his own. He became the Blade of the Tribunal, slaughtering his brethren who had betrayed the Lord of the Abyss, even if they were only trying to survive. No one knew what Helcurt was thinking on these hunts. All he knew was that he had to live on. Though he was born in the darkness, walked in the darkness, and shall forever remain in the darkness, he was still alive.

In the murky swamps where he could alone, Helcurt marked each passing year by tying a knot on a rope. This was his proof that he was alive. Living was his most humble and proud desire, even if he did so like a dog in the shadows......

Helcurt had survived to see the end of the war, but with the absence of the gods, madness spread through the Abyss. The source of it all was the Lord of the Abyss, who had lost his way without his dark god. For thousands of years he controlled the Abyss using his corrupted blood, and now his madness infected them in the same manner. This was the 310CX-i Curse. Left with no choice, the demons of the Abyss, led by Thamuz, turned against the Lord of the Abyss. They surged toward the Lord of the Abyss's palace, and Helcurt, leading the Tribunal, stood as the last line of defense like an imposing wall.

Amidst the chaos, Helcurt gripped his rope tightly.

> A thousand knots, a thousand years...

The coarse feel of the rope reminded him that he was still alive.

Though born in the darkness, born a traitor, he had the right to live.

> Survive...

Helcurt turned around and came face to face with the towering Lord of the Abyss before emitting a beastly roar. With all his might, he purged the cursed blood and caused it to burst out of his body. The sheer force caused his bones to jet out from his skin like blades. In an instant, he was on top of the Lord of the Abyss, slashing away with his blades, freeing himself from the shackles of the Blood Curse in a most fitting fashion.

> When you decided my fate, you should have remembered that I was born a traitor...

He had bared his fangs against the Lord of the Abyss. Just like his ancestors a thousand years ago.

He was born a traitor.

He had always been a traitor.

# Abilities

## Passive — Shadow of Styx (Prowler / Dark fog)
- Type: Buff  
- Quote: "He who hides in the shadow."  
- Description:
  - Helcurt remains in the concealment state until spotted by the enemy team, gaining 15% extra Movement Speed and continuously regenerating HP if he has not dealt any damage.
  - When spotted, he gains 50% extra Attack Speed and 50% extra Movement Speed (decreasing over time) for 4 seconds, and leaves the concealment state 0.75 seconds later.
  - Any hero killed after being hit by Helcurt leaves a dark fog that explodes and spreads (10s cooldown). Helcurt conceals himself while within the fog.
- Notes:
  - "Conceal" grants Helcurt Invisibility.
  - The sound effect when losing the concealment is audible to nearby units.

## Skill 1 — Hidden Terror (Passive / Active)
- Type: Mobility / CC  
- Cooldown: 15.0 / 14.0 / 13.0 / 12.0 / 11.0 / 10.0 (reduced faster when in concealment)  
- Level scaling (Base Damage): 150 / 180 / 210 / 240 / 270 / 300  
- Description:
  - Passive: When Helcurt is in concealment state, the skill terrifies enemy heroes hit and its cooldown decreases 3× faster.
  - Active: Helcurt blinks to the target location and deals physical damage to enemies within the area, reducing their Movement Speed by 60% for 0.75s.
- Terms: Blink, CC

## Skill 2 — Deadly Stinger (Passive / Active)
- Type: Burst  
- Cooldown: 4.0 / 3.7 / 3.4 / 3.1 / 2.8 / 2.5  
- Base Damage (level): 180 / 200 / 220 / 240 / 260 / 280  
- Extra physical attack scaling: +42  
- Description:
  - Passive: Helcurt gains one stinger for every 1.5s spent in concealment or on each Basic Attack. Up to 5 stingers can be stored.
  - Active: Helcurt fires all stingers forward, each dealing physical damage to enemies (minions and creeps take 70% damage). Deadly Stinger counts as a Basic Attack but cannot Crit.
- Attack effects: 100%  
- Notes:
  - This skill cannot trigger certain attack-effect stacking mechanics (example: Endless Strike).

## Ultimate — Dark Night Falls
- Type: Buff / Debuff  
- Cooldown: 60.0 / 55.0 / 50.0  
- Duration: 3 seconds  
- Quote: "He who resurrects in the night."  
- Description:
  - Helcurt summons a dark night, reducing all enemies' sight range and causing them to temporarily lose sight of their allies for 3s. During this time, Helcurt gains Movement Speed (40% / 45% / 50% by level).
  - The dark night makes it easier for Helcurt to leave enemies' sight range.
- Notes:
  - Does not affect enemy turrets.
  - Does not affect units inside certain abilities' areas.
  - Enemies' attacks and abilities are not restricted by vision.
  - Kills during the duration without Helcurt dealing damage are not considered assists.

# Trivia

- Helcurt is one of the heroes who doesn't walk, but rather crawls.  
- If you look closely at Helcurt's portrait, you can notice his white, cat-like pupils.  
- He is heavily inspired by Alma from Magic Rush (Alma — external source).  
- He was banned for 41 days and 21 hours from 16 May 2023 01:00:00 to 26 June 2023 22:00:00, the longest official ban in MLBB history.  
- Helcurt's sound that indicated his concealment could previously be heard by enemies; this has since been removed.  
- He was among the last heroes to receive a "Zodiac" type skin (Scorpio).  
- Various notes about past patches and skill interactions exist (see patch notes for details).
