## Star Trail

- Description: summoned by Novaria will continuously slow nearby enemies by 20% and grant vision of them for 2 seconds.
- Effect: summoned Astral Spheres apply a continuous slow and grant vision of affected enemies.
- Base Damage (scaling by level): 260 / 285 / 310 / 335 / 360 / 385
- Extra Percentage Damage: 4% / 4.4% / 4.8% / 5.2% / 5.6% / 6%

Note: This passive's slow and vision mechanics are persistent for the stated duration when enemies remain within the sphere's area.

## Astral Meteor

- Description: Novaria summons an Astral Sphere at the target location, continuously dealing damage to nearby enemies six times. After 2 seconds, the sphere will explode, dealing final damage to nearby enemies and increasing Astral Sphere's slow effect to 2.5 times the original slow effect (50% slow).
- Cooldown (per level): 8.0 / 7.6 / 7.2 / 6.8 / 6.4 / 6.0 seconds (base shown above).
- Mana Cost (per level): 60 / 70 / 80 / 90 / 100 / 110 (base shown above).
- Damage Details:

- Notes:
  - The damage, excluding the final one, reveals enemies until they get out of the area of effect. The final damage reveals heroes for 2 seconds.

Note: Damage values scale by level as listed above.

  - Novaria cannot go beyond the battlefield for an unspecified amount of distance within the skill duration.

# Trivia

- The name Novaria is of Latin origin and means fresh and innovative. The name "Novaria" is reminiscent of "nova," a term used in astronomy to describe a star that suddenly increases in brightness. This aligns with her celestial theme and her role as a luminous force on the battlefield.
- Her attire features shimmering, glowing patterns resembling stars and constellations, highlighting her celestial nature.
- Novaria’s entire design is inspired by the cosmos, stars, and celestial elements. She is a manifestation of the **Astral Artificer** from the **Valley of Stars**, and her design reflects her deep connection to astral energy and cosmic forces. The dominant colors in her design are **blue**, **purple**, and **white**, all of which are traditionally associated with the night sky and space.
- Unlike most mages, she can pass through walls, helping her reposition for better attacks or escape dangerous situations.
- Unlike most mages who deal damage within a fixed range, Novaria’s long-range skill rewards players for positioning and perfecting their aim, making her one of the few mages who functions like a ranged marksman in a sense.
