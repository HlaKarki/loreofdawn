'''Heroes''' are the playable characters in ''[[Mobile Legends: Bang Bang]]''. Each has a unique ability known as a [[skill]]. Because the outcome of the match is dependent on their intervention. During a match, two opposing teams choose five heroes out of 128 to gain experience and gold and level up their abilities in order to destroy the opponent's base. Most heroes have a distinct [[role]] that defines how they affect the battlefield, though many heroes can play multiple roles.

==List of heroes==
{{Main|List of heroes}}
There are over 100 unique heroes, each with their own set of skills. {{hi|Zetian}} is currently the latest among them all, with {{Hi|Obsidia}} being an upcoming hero.

==Hero roles==
{{Main|Hero roles}}
Heroes are divided into six categories based on their standard roles in the game and other relevant characteristics. The following roles are: [[Tank]], [[Fighter]], [[Assassin]], [[Mage]], [[Marksman]], and [[Support]].

==Hero updates==
{{Main|Hero updates}}
Heroes can rise and fall, and eventually be forgotten, so they have the potential to be upgraded to new and improved gameplay and visual standards known as a revamp.

==Hero skills==
{{Main|Hero skill}}
A skill is a one-of-a-kind action performed by and available in-game only to a specific hero. Each of these special moves contributes to a hero's skill set. Almost all heroes will have an innate ability, two normal abilities, and one ultimate ability. In some rare cases, a hero may have three normal abilities and one ultimate ability.

==Obtaining a hero==
To use a hero in a match, you must obtain them by purchasing directly by using {{BP|Battle Points}}, {{DM|Diamond}}, or {{TIX|Tickets}}. A hero can also be obtained in certain events.

Every week, a random selection of five heroes is made available for use in all matches with the exception of the [[Ranked]] game mode, but this does not mean you obtained them.

[[Trial card]]s can also be used to try a hero for free for a limited time. Once activated, the hero can now be used to participate in matches (except Ranked) until the free trial period ends.
