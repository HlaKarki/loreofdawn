[[File:Tank_Icon.png|thumb|left|65px]]'''Tanks''' heroes are the most durable among all in Mobile Legends. These front-line giants help in attacking enemies. They are usually found leading the charge, choosing the right times and situations to initiate aggression. Many '''tanks''' can also protect their more fragile teammates by stunning or pushing around dangerous foes and limiting their damage potential. In Mobile Legends, most classes of heroes are really versatile in terms of abilities and with the item crafting variations. A "'''tank'''" can be played as a DPS if you choose the right equipment. Usually, '''tanks''' choose to lane on the bottom tower. '''Tanks''' have high defense and hitpoints, but low damage. When used properly, a '''tank''' could be the strongest hero in the game.

==Trivia==
===General===
*{{hi|Grock}} and {{hi|Hylos}} are the only tanks who are unplayable in Mayhem Mode.
*All of {{hi|Tigreal}}, {{hi|Franco}}, {{hi|Gatotkaca}}, and {{Hi|name=Hylos}} skills have a {{AVC1|CC}} effect.
*{{Hi|Franco}} and {{Hi|Atlas}} have moves that can hook the enemy and have a [[Crowd control|CC]] effect.
*{{hi|Hylos}} and {{hi|Minotaur}} are the only heroes based on Ancient Greek mythological figures.
*{{hi|Franco}} used to be a {{Fighter|Fighter}} with different animation skills, but his role was purely changed into a tank in Season 3.
*{{hi|Gatotkaca}} was a pure {{Tank|Tank}} before.
*{{hi|Alice}} was a pure {{Mage|Mage}} before.
*{{hi|Baxia}} is a {{Tank|Tank}} that his passive can inflict {{AVC1|Life Drain}} to enemy heroes.
*{{Hi|Lolita}} is a {{Support|Support}}/{{Tank|Tank}} who can reflect most ranged attacks using her second skill. She could absorb the damage and reflect it back to the enemy before she got revamped.
*{{hi|Esmeralda}} can have half of her {{AVC1|Max HP}} as a {{AVC1|shield}} and also has an unique ability to ignore her enemies' {{AVC1|shield}}s; to reduce her shield, [[Esmeralda/Abilities#Counters|see here]].
*{{Hi|Chip}} has a move which summons a portal that both allies and enemies can go into, teleporting them to the other end of the portal.
*{{hi|Lolita}}, {{hi|Hilda}}, {{hi|Esmeralda}}, {{hi|Alice}} , {{Hi|Carmilla}} and {{hi|Edith}} are the only tanks are female.
*{{hi|Masha}}'s {{Tank|Secondary Tank}} role was removed. She is now a {{Fighter|Pure Fighter}} since [[Patch Notes 1.4.94|Patch 1.4.94]], later her role was restore changed back in [[Patch Notes 1.6.66|Patch 1.6.66]].
*{{hi|name=Balmond}}'s {{Tank|Secondary Tank}} role. He is now a {{Fighter|Pure Fighter}} since [[Patch Notes 1.5.46|Patch 1.5.46]].
*{{hi|Kaja}} was formerly a {{Tank|Tank}}/{{Support|Support}} hero. On his latest revamp, he was changed to a {{{Support|Support}}/{{Fighter|Fighter}} hero. 
*{{hi|Alice}}'s original role was {{Mage|Mage}}/{{Tank|Tank}} prior to her revamp, but was then later swapped to {{Tank|Tank}}/{{Mage|Mage}}.
