A magician (abbreviated as mage) is a hero with a longer range, ability-based area of effect damage, and [[crowd control]] that they may utilize to strike from a distance. Mages primarily focus on magic skills that deals [[magic damage]], usually burst damage, and as a consequence, they spend a lot of gold on equipment that allows them to do more damage and have shorter skill cooldowns.

Though mages typically focus on killing prime targets in combat, they can also use their innate crowd control and utility to manipulate opponents, protecting their team from them or setting them up for a takedown, and in the right circumstances, can damage and control multiple enemies at the same time. Regardless of their might, mages are fundamentally frail and succumb fast to direct attacks.

==Trivia==
===General===
*{{hi|Selena}} is the only mage that can shift and change her attack style early on in the game.
*The only mage heroes having more than three active abilities in a single form are {{hi|Zhask}}, {{hi|Lunox}}, and {{hi|Pharsa}}.
**Prior to her makeover, {{hi|Pharsa}} only had three active abilities. Since then, the {{ai|Curse of Crow}} has been included as the first ability, and the {{ai|Wings by Wings}} has been replaced as a special skill from the first ability.
**During the creation of {{hi|Luo Yi}}'s abilities, she had four abilities; however, she was modified into a three-skill hero in a later patch.
*{{hi|Pharsa}} was temporarily banned from [[Brawl]] games due to her {{ai|Feathered Air Strike}}, which provided an unfair advantage to her team (ultimate ability). For that specific game style alone, her ultimate ability's CD has been increased from 8 to 30 seconds.
*The only heroes that employ the concept of [[wikipedia:Yin and yang|Yin-yang]] are {{hi|Kagura}} and {{hi|Luo Yi}}.
*The only heroes who employ the concept of light and dark are {{hi|Lunox}} and {{hi|Luo Yi}}.
*{{hi|Nana}}'s original role was {{Support|Support}}/{{Mage|Mage}} later swapped to {{Mage|Mage}}/{{Support|Support}} by [[Patch 1.6.94]] or [[Patch 1.7.08]] she was reduced to {{Mage|Pure Mage}} due to a lack of a support like ability and to increase her damage to make her viable as a [[Mid Laner]]
**{{hi|Faramis}} is currently the only hero who bares the {{Support|Support}}/{{Mage|Mage}} role.
**The closest heroes who's skills are similar to {{Mage|Mage}}/{{Support|Support}} hybrid roles are {{hi|Luo Yi}}, {{hi|Xavier}} and {{hi|Zetian}}. {{hi|Luo Yi}}'s {{ai|Diversion}} helps her allies to a certain location while {{hi|Xavier}}'s {{ai|Mystic Field}} and {{hi|Zetian}}'s {{ai|Fury of the Phoenix}} gave allies speed boost however they are still labeled as {{Mage|Pure Mage}} many of the players are still requesting them to be added to the {{Support|Support}} role.
*Because of the introduction of [[Magic Damage (Specialty)|Magic Damage]] specialty in [[Patch Notes 1.4.94|Patch 1.4.94]], {{hi|Gusion}}, {{hi|Karina}}, {{hi|Guinevere}}, and {{hi|Silvanna}} become abstracted from the mage role.
*{{hi|Bane}} is currently the only hero who bares the {{Fighter|Fighter}}/{{Mage|Mage}} role as of [[Patch 1.9.64]] {{hi|Julian}}'s role was originally {{Fighter|Fighter}}/{{Mage|Mage}} but was swapped to {{Assassin|Assassin}}/{{Fighter|Fighter}} due to how his gamestyle is similar to that of an {{Assassin|Assassin}} and although it is not mentioned it is prompted to be to be more lore accurate said to be an "assassin" to murder heretics by the orders of the Church of Light (see [[Julian#Story|Julian Lore]]).
*{{hi|Vexana}} is the only mage capable summoning a puppet in the form of the targeted enemy.
*(After Vexana's Revamp back in S25, her Ultimate has now been adjusted to Eternal Guard. Refer to [[Patch Notes 1.6.78 (Advanced Server)]] for more information.)
*{{hi|Alice}}'s original role was {{Mage|Mage}}/{{Tank|Tank}} prior to her revamp, but was then later swapped to {{Tank|Tank}}/{{Mage|Mage}}.
