==Rotation Guide==
*As you are most squishy and lacks damage output, the gold laner should stay in lane and tries to hoard up gold as much as possible:
**Secure last hit of minions by abilities
**Kill gold crab if possible
**Peel turret's golden shield if possible.
**Slay nearby small jungle creep if no minions are nearby.
*Avoid dying at all costs. If there is no enemy sight on map, try to hide in the bush between the first and second turret, as it will make most ganking attempts fail.
*Consider {{ii|Magic Blade}} and {{ii|Rose Gold Meteor}} for better survival.
*If the enemy team ganks you with 3-4 members the high possibility is that they will dive turret, give up the minion.
*It is not recommended to purchase a decent boot, but a basic boot only. Some marksmen work fine with full attack items.
===Before 2 minutes===
*Your wave clear speed is slow, so focus on minions first.
*Keep in mind in 1:15-1:30, the enemy jungler finishes jungling, avoid disclosing your location.
===2-5 minutes===
*Clearing minions in gold lane is still priority, you can rotate to mid lane to clear minions when both team contest the turtle, then quickly return to gold lane.
*You can help your team if there is a fight near the Turtle pit, then quickly returns to lane, choose a way that goes through the least bushes.
===5 minutes onward===
Rotate to all other lanes to find minion waves to farm, or clear small jungle creep camps.
