'''Marksmen''' are ranged heroes whose power is almost entirely based on their basic attacks: by using their reach to land massive continuous damage from a distance, marksmen's are capable of taking down even the most difficult opponents when positioned behind the safety of their team, and perform better at securing objectives such as the [[Lord]] and [[turret]]s.

Marksmen's are classified into three types: pure, primary, and secondary.

== Trivia ==
=== General ===
*{{Hero Icon|name = Irithel}} is the first Marksman hero who can move and use the basic attack at the same time.
**{{Hero Icon|name = Kimmy}} is the only other Marksman who can move and attack at the same time.
*{{hi|name=Kimmy}} and {{hi|name=Beatrix}} (using {{ai|name=Mechanical Genius (Renner)|Renner}} or {{ai|name=Mechanical Genius (Bennett)|Bennett}} or {{Ai|name=Wesker}}) are the only heroes whose {{Ability Icon|name = Basic Attack}} necessitate manual aiming.
*{{Hero Icon|name = Yi Sun-Shin}} is the only [[hero]] that can change the base's model.
*{{Hero Icon|name = Miya}} is the only Marksman who can go {{AVC1|Invisible|color=Blue}}, by using her {{Ability Icon|name = Hidden Moonlight}}.
**{{Hero Icon|name = Lesley}} can also go to half-invisible via {{Ability Icon|name = Master of Camouflage}}, but she can be detected by distortions in the field such as {{AVC1|AoE skills|color=yellow}}, {{AVC1|revaling skills|reveal}}, etc. 
*{{Hero Icon|name = Moskov}} and {{Hero Icon|name = Clint}} are the only Marksmen whose passive lets their {{Ability Icon|name = Basic Attack}} penetrate through targets in a straight line.
*{{Hero Icon|name = Layla}} has the lowest durability of any hero but has the longest basic attack range in the game, enough to destroy a defensive tower outside its range.
**{{Hi|name=Beatrix}} can also attack defensive turrets from outside their range with {{Ai|Renner|name=Mechanical Genius (Renner)}}.
*{{Hero Icon|name = Roger}} and {{Hero Icon|name = Kimmy}} are the only Marksmen who are unplayable in Mayhem Mode.
*{{Hero Icon|name = Bruno}} is the only hero who never uses hands in any attack.
** He is also the only hero with the sport-related mode of attacking.
** He is the only mechanical marksman in the game, having mechanical legs, although his other skins are human.
* Marksman is the only category of heroes that exclusively features Human or Humanoid heroes (such as Elves), with the only possible exceptions being {{Hero Icon|name = Karrie}}, having non-human legs for being a Yasson, and {{Hero Icon|name = Roger}} in his wolf form.
*{{Hero Icon|name = Wanwan}} is considered to be the most difficult marksman because of her {{Ability Icon|name = Tiger's Pace}} affecting her {{Ability Icon|name = Crossbow of Tang}} can't be activated unless she hits all the weakness points on the target in 3 directions which will benefit her with her passive.
*{{Hero Icon|name = Popol and Kupa}} is the only hero who has 5 abilities. He is the only hero which always accompanied by his pet resulting in a 2v1 situation.
*{{hi|name=Brody}} is the only marksman hero in the game who can charge his {{Ability Icon|name=Basic Attack|basic attack}} and he is also the slowest marksman.
*{{Hero Icon|name = Beatrix}} is the only marksman hero who carries '''4 weapons''' at once, including {{ai|name=Mechanical Genius (Renner)|Renner}}, {{ai|name=Mechanical Genius (Bennett)|Bennett}}, {{ai|name=Mechanical Genius (Wesker)|Wesker}} and {{ai|name=Mechanical Genius (Nibiru)|Nibiru}}.
*{{hi|name=Kimmy}} and {{hi|name=Natan}} are the only marksmens that have {{AVC1|Magical Damage|Magic Damage}} specialty.
