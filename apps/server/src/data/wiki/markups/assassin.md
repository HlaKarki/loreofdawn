An '''Assassin''' is an agile hero that specializes in killing or disabling roaming targets picking them off as they try to replenish HP. Focused on infiltration, deception, and mobility, '''Assassins''' are opportunistic hunters who find favorable moments within a fight before jumping into the fray. Regardless of the size of the enemy team, '''Assassins''' specialize in positioning and artful killing. They strike when the time is right – no sooner, no later. '''Assassins''' are more suitable to be played by experienced players as they have low HP and high burst damage. They are ideal for taking out opponents easily, but are easier to be killed due to bad positioning and wrong timing.

==Assassin Types==
Assassins are classified into three types: pure, primary, and secondary.

==Trivia==
*Two of the assassins have Season Exclusive skins: {{hi|Alucard}} and {{hi|Fanny}}.
*{{hi|Lesley}}, {{hi|Aamon}}, and {{Hero icon|name=Natalia}} have a special stealth called '''Camouflage''' making them half-invisible or semi-invisible to enemies.
*{{hi|Natalia}} and {{hi|Lancelot}} are the only assassins whose first skill can be cast again if it hits an enemy.
*{{hi|Harley}} and {{hi|Kadita}} changed their roles into Mage/Assassin Heroes without changing their skills.
*{{hi|Gusion}} and {{hi|Karina}} had their roles changed into {{Assassin|Pure Assassin}} due to the addition of {{AVC1|Magic Damage}} Specialty in [[Patch Notes 1.4.94|Patch 1.4.94]] to making {{hi|Selena}} the only hero with a {{Assassin|Assassin}}/{{Mage|Mage}} hybrid role.
*{{hi|Fanny}} is the hardest hero to handle because of the complexity of her skills, with a 100% difficulty rating, but is also considered one of the strongest heroes. She is also the most agile hero in the game.
*{{hi|Natalia}} is the only assassin who can {{AVC1|Silence|CC}} enemies

*{{hi|Ling}} is the only hero who can walk a wall to wall with a special camouflage.

*{{hi|Karina}}, {{hi|Fanny}}, {{hi|Natalia}}, {{hi|Selena}}, {{hi|Lesley}}, {{hi|Kadita}}, {{hi|Benedetta}}, {{hi|Mathilda}}, and {{Hero icon|name=Joy}} are the only Assassins that are female.
