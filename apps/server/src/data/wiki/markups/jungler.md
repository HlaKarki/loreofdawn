== Meta Junglers ==
It is recommended that you practice each Jungle hero at least once, so that you know enemy skill types, cooldowns, and learn matchups and recommended skill builds for different heroes. A Jungler will determine the objectives that are taken like the turtle and the lord that can change the tides in a match.

These heroes are currently meta and will dominate (most of the time as shown in tournaments) if you mastered them (In no particular order): {{Hero-box-xs|name=Baxia}}{{Hero-box-xs|name=Yi Sun-shin}}{{Hero-box-xs|name=Joy}}{{Hero-box-xs|name=Fredrinn}}{{Hero-box-xs|name=Suyou}}{{Hero-box-xs|name=Hayabusa}}{{Hero-box-xs|name=Fanny}}

==Notes for Junglers==
It is recommended that you play every Jungler at least once to learn their skills, and matchups/recommended equipment builds for different heroes.

Be aware of invading Roamers/Junglers. Be alert when these heroes are on the enemy team:
{{Hero-box-xs|name=Hilda}}{{Hero-box-xs|name=Hylos}}{{Hero-box-xs|name=Franco}}{{Hero-box-xs|name=Natalia}}{{Hero-box-xs|name=Harley}}{{Hero-box-xs|name=Grock}}{{Hero-box-xs|name=Khufra}}

These junglers are weak early game/when at level 1-3. If you use these heroes, be aware of invades from the enemy team:
{{Hero-box-xs|name=Fanny}}{{Hero-box-xs|name=Ling}}{{Hero-box-xs|name=Granger}}{{Hero-box-xs|name=Gusion}}

(Fanny and Ling are weak until they get Blue Buff.)

During the first 5 minutes of the match, gold from Minions is reduced by 70%, and you '''will not share''' minion rewards with other teammates.

{{Battle spell|Retribution}} is recommended for Junglers, as it does extra damage to creeps/minions.

For more tips, refer to [[Jungle]] guide.
