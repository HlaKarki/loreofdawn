'''Fighters''' are close-range combatants that possess a mixture of offensive and defensive capabilities. A '''fighter's''' damage will add up over time to make it a major threat. Each '''fighter''' has a unique blend of mobility, damage, disruption and durability. The role of a '''Fighter''' is an intermediate melee hero, between durability or damage, hence makes fighters serve as multi-purpose combatant, such as the Semi-Tank, Crowd Controller, or Burst Damage Dealer.

Fighters are classified into three types: pure, primary, and secondary.

==Trivia==
===General===
*{{hi|Alucard}} and {{hi|Ruby}} are the only fighters with large lifestealing abilities (not given by equipment or emblems).
**Additionally, they are some of the few heroes that can heal themselves with their skills without specific equipment, along with {{hi|Alpha}} with {{ai|Force Swing}}, and {{hi|Dyrroth}} with {{ai|Wrath of the Abyss|Wrath of the Abyss (through 3rd {{ai|Basic Attack}})}}.
*{{hi|Roger}} is the only one who can transform in the early game and change his mode of attack.
*{{hi|Jawhead}} is only the hero can throw any hero (allied and enemy alike), monster or minion (excluding Turtle and Lord).
**His allies cannot longer be thrown when his allies have {{ii|Throw Forbidden}} until purchase back to {{ii|Allow Throw}}.
*{{hi|Sun}} is the only hero who can summon clones of himself (only for a limited time like Guinevere's second skill).
**{{hi|Guinevere}} can also summon herself but it has limited-time to vanish via {{ai|Spatial Migration}} without affecting her hitpoints.
*{{hi|Bane}} is the only fighter whose {{ai|Deadly Catch}} can damage a defensive turret.
*{{hi|Freya}} and {{hi|Lapu-Lapu}} are the only heroes that switch to a different weapon when using their ultimate skills ({{ai|Valkyrie Descent}} and {{ai|Chieftain's Rage}} respectively).
**However, {{hi|Lapu-Lapu}}'s ultimate {{ai|Chieftain's Rage}} was replaced to {{ai|Bravest Fighter}} in revamped [[Patch Notes 1.5.24|Patch 1.5.24]].
**This should not be confused with {{Hi|Roger}} switches form entirely.
*{{hi|Aulus}}, {{hi|Roger}}, {{hi|Martis}}, {{hi|Minsitthar}}, and {{hi|Badang}} are the the only fighters who are unplayable in Mayhem Mode.
*{{hi|Leomord}} is the only hero who can change his mode of moving by executing his first {{ai|Phantom Steed}}.
**He is also the only hero to have different abilities for him and his steed.
*{{hi|Kaja}} was formerly a {{Tank|[[Tank]]}}/{{Support|[[Support (Role)|Support]]}} hero. Since his latest revamp, he had no longer had a {{Tank|tank}} role, and is now a {{Fighter|Fighter}}/{{Support|[[Support (Role)|Support]]}} hero.

*{{hi|Masha}} is the only hero that has three health bars.
*{{hi|Masha}}, {{hi|Yu Zhong}}, {{hi|Sun}}, and Fredrinn are the only fighter heroes with more than three active abilities in a singular form.
